Snowball was a seasonal Item in Deadlock.
Snowball was unlocked for all players during the 2025 Winter holiday event. It was a free colorless item that did not take an item slot and used a "Cosmetic" slot added during the event, appearing to the right of your active items.
Players could throw a Snowball that dealt Spirit Damage. It leveled up and increased in damage, velocity, radius, charges, projectile count and reduced cooldown as the player unlocked Christmas tree ornaments by winning matches with different heroes. Throwing a Snowball at an ally would reset its cooldown.
The Snowball level could be tested in the Hideout and Sandbox with the console command citadel_snowball_level_override + the level number.

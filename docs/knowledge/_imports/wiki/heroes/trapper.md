Trapper is an unreleased Hero in Deadlock, previously available in the Hero Labs gamemode. Trapper catches vermin on his Silktrap and exterminates them with Bottled Phantasmicide. He can protect allies from harm with Pest Barrier, as his enemies get swarmed by his Crawling Plague of spectral spiders.
A hard-working civil servant of the New York Municipal Coven, Trapper is ready to clock in during Maelstrom day to earn some basketball tickets.
Trapper was added to Hero Labs on November 7, 2024, and removed from the game when the mode was discontinued on August 18, 2025.

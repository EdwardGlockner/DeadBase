Mina is a Hero in Deadlock; a glass cannon that delivers quick bursts of Spirit damage at range with her passive, Love Bites. She can transform into bats and escape when things get a bit too messy. Each time she triggers Love Bites, her vampiric powers grow stronger. This builds toward her ultimate, Nox Nostra, which unleashes a massive swarm of bats that hunt down and silence nearby enemies.
Mina Ha was an ambitious and rich girl betrothed to American royalty, the eldest heir to the Fairfax Industries fortune. However, her hopes were dashed when her fiancé was assassinated prior to the wedding. Her parents, seeking only the best for their daughter, contracted a vampire to turn Mina into a vampire and grant her eternal life. Now learning her new powers and navigating vampire high society, Mina's ambitious eye is set on becoming the next Viscount of New York. 
Mina was released on August 18, 2025 as the first hero in the Six New Heroes update.

Wrecker is an unreleased Hero in Deadlock, previously available in the Hero Labs gamemode. Stunning enemies with his Wrecking Ball, Wrecker will Consume them to satisfy his hunger, then spit out their remains in a Bio Blast. His foes will suffer devastating damage once he charges his Overload.
Employed by Eldritch Tactical Solutions, Wrecker is a monstrous creature who made a reputation as "The Butcher of Ixia" for his dirty work in extraplanar wars.
Wrecker was added to Hero Labs on October 24, 2024, and removed from the game when the mode was discontinued on August 18, 2025.

The Bear Pit is an underground arena of blood sport in New York where spectators can bet on prize fights.

## Summary

Known fighters include Lash and Bebop, who are rivals. Wraith is a prominent gambler in matches.

Lash once lost a fight to Bebop.1]

Bebop wants to retire from the Bear Pit once he completes the Ritual and gets his wish.2]

## Trivia

- In Neon Prime, Kali was a former champion of the pit who came out of retirement, only to lose to Bebop. Kali sustained injuries severe enough to send him into an almost 2-decade hiatus afterwards.3]

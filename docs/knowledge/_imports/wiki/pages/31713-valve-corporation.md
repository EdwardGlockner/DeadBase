Valve Corporation, also known as Valve Software, is an American video game developer, publisher, hardware, and digital distribution company known for their digital distribution platform Steam, hardware like Steam Deck and Steam Machine, as well as critically acclaimed video game franchises such as Half-Life, Team Fortress, Portal, Left 4 Dead and Dota. They are currently developing Deadlock.

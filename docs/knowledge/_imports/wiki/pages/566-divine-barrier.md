Divine Barrier is a Tier 4 Vitality Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400. Its component, Guardian Ward, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

## Affected Abilities

Following abilities' debuffs can be dispelled from a target using Divine Barrier's active ability. Note that some of these abilities prevent target from acting and require a teammate to use the cleansing ability.

- Abrams: Shoulder Charge, Seismic Impact

- Apollo: Disengaging Sigil, Riposte, Itani Lo Sahn

- Bebop: Exploding Uppercut, Sticky Bomb (the bomb is dropped on the ground), Grapple Arm (cast microslow + T1 debuff), Hyper Beam (effect is negligible)

- Billy: Bashdown, Blasted

- Celeste: Light Eater, Dazzling Trick, Shining Wonder

- Drifter: Rend (T3 debuff), Stalker's Mark

- Dynamo: Kinetic Pulse

- Graves: Jar of Dead (T2 slow), Grasping Hands, Essence Theft, Borrowed Decree

- Grey Talon: Rain of Arrows (T1 slow), Spirit Snare, Guided Owl

- Haze: Sleep Dagger, Fixation

- Holliday: Powder Keg, Crackshot, Spirit Lasso

- Infernus: Napalm, Flame Dash, Afterburn, Concussive Combustion

- Ivy: Entangling Thorns, Stone Form, Air Drop (T2 slow, T3 silence)

- Kelvin: Frost Grenade, Arctic Beam

- Lady Geist: Life Drain, Malice, Soul Exchange

- Lash: Ground Strike (T2 displacement + slow), Flog (T1 slow), Death Slam

- McGinnis: Spectral Wall, Heavy Barrage (T1 slow)

- Mina: Love Bites, Nox Nostra

- Mirage: Fire Scarabs, Dust Devil, Djinn's Mark

- Mo & Krill: Scorn (T3 debuff), Burrow, Sand Blast, Combo

- Paige: Captivating Read, Rallying Charge

- Paradox: Pulse Grenade, Time Wall, Kinetic Carbine

- Pocket: Barrage, Enchanter's Satchel (T3 slow), Affliction

- Rem: Pillow Toss, Naptime

- Seven: Lightning Ball (T2 slow; effect is negligible), Static Charge, Power Surge (T3 debuff)

- Shiv: Serrated Knives, Slice and Dice

- Silver (Transformed): Mauling Leap, Tail Whack

- Sinclair: Vexing Bolt (T1 debuff), Rabbit Hex

- The Doorman: Call Bell, Luggage Cart, Hotel Guest

- Victor: Pain Battery (T1 slow), Aura of Suffering (T1 slow, T3 debuff; effect is negligible), Shocking Reanimation

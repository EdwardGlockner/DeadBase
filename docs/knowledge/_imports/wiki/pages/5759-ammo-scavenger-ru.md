Ammo Scavenger was a Tier 1 Spirit Item that provides more Ammo. Securing or denying souls gives you ammo back and you earn one stack of Spirit Power.

It could be purchased from the Shop for 500.

Ammo Scavenger's effect of earning back ammo when claiming orbs became a global mechanic when the item was removed, but players earn back 1 ammo instead of 2.

## Notes

- When a Soul is secured or denied, all stacks of this item's Spirit Power are refreshed for the duration (30s at base).

- Also applies when killing Denizens.

- You do not receive stacks when securing spawn room souls.

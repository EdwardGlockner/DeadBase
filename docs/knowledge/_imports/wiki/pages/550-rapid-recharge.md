Rapid Recharge is a Tier 3 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200. Its component, Extra Charge, makes up 800 of the cost.

## Notable interactions

- If Sinclair copies Vindicta's Assassinate with Rapid Recharge, they will start with 3 charges. After using Assassinate once, they can keep the copy indefinitely as long as they have one or more charges left.

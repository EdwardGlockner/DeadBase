A victim of John Hathorne during the Salem Witch Trials, Vindicta got a second chance of life when her spirit was brought back to the mortal plane during the first Maelstrom (the event that introduced the supernatural to the public at large). Waging a one ghost war against "The Friends of Humanity", a neo inquisition formed by Hathorne's descendants; Vindicta looks to keep the supernatural safe from Hathorne's ilk, and she isn't afraid to stack as many bodies as needed to do it.

Vindicta has been dead for roughly 200 years9] before returning to life during the first Maelstrom, 50 years before the ritual.10] She calls herself a "ghost", though she believes that's a simplification of her condition.11] After the initial shock of finding herself in early 20th century New York, she was lost for a while before finding a new purpose in hunting down the Friends of Humanity.12]13]

Vindicta's main target is the current leader of the Friends of Humanity, also named John Hathorne, who is a descendant of the original Hathorne who killed her.14]15] Vindicta has killed John Hathorne's brother while he was on a speedboat,16] and assassinated someone else during the Boston Marathon.17]

At some point in recent years, Vindicta started working with Grey Talon, as he was also hunting the Friends of Humanity to avenge his murdered family.18]19]

Vindicta's exploits have become known in Ixia. According to Apollo, Vindicta is called "Hasha Vogali", which is Ixian for "Death's Whisper".20]

### Personality

Vindicta is jaded and pragmatic with a single-minded drive for revenge.21] She doesn't believe in the law or the government,22]23] or that there will be any justice for the crimes of the Friends of Humanity unless people like her and Grey Talon take action against them.24] Even if Vindicta kills Hathorne, she intends to keep killing until the Friends of Humanity are exterminated as she has "all the time in the world".25]4] Vindicta also thinks there are more people worth killing besides the Friends of Humanity,26] although she hasn't considered becoming a professional assassin.27]

After returning from the dead, Vindicta says she was "lost" for a while before finding her purpose of revenge.13] She ultimately sees her actions as her "saving life".28]29] She thinks naiveté is dangerous because it can get people hurt.30]

Some heroes find Vindicta's methods too extremist, even if they sympathize with her cause.31]32]33]

### Appearance

Vindicta appears as a spectral light blue-skinned young woman with glowing blue eyes, long braided blueish-white hair with a red ribbon, dark purple lips, and black mascara running down her face. She wears a red jacket with a black necktie over a dark blue skirt with ragged edges. She carries stakes, bullets and a bell on a large belt. She's barefoot, which other characters comment on.34]35]36] Her weapon is a sniper rifle.

### Information

Vindicta will occasionally hum the song Simple Gifts after using a shot of her ultimate.37] This is a song that originated from the Shaker religion in the early 1800s.

## Character Relationships

### Grey Talon

Vindicta and Grey Talon have hunted the Friends of Humanity together and regard each other as friends.38]19]39]

Vindicta calls Grey Talon by his first name and believes that, if her father was like Grey Talon, she would never have died during the Salem Witch Trials.18]

Vindicta doesn't know what Grey Talon saw in Shiv.40]

When killing Drifter, Vindicta dedicates it to Grey Talon.41]42]

## Character Interactions

- Vindicta is referred to as Goody Proctor by Mo & Krill.43]

- Vindicta hates Mina,44] believing her to be "outrageously stupid".45]

- Vindicta believes her life was harder than Pocket's,46]47] while Pocket believes Vindicta's rage is misguided and she just wants to watch the world burn.32]48]

- Vindicta believes Ivy is naive and warns her that, if she doesn't smarten up soon, someone she cares about will be hurt.30]

- Haze warns Vindicta to "quit while she's ahead" because murdering John Hathorne will "galvanize" the Friends of Humanity.25] She calls Vindicta a "domestic terrorist".31]

- Apollo commends Vindicta for her war against the Friends of Humanity, although he dislikes her dishonorable tactics.49]50]

- Graves wishes she could help Vindicta with her problems.51]52]

- Venator believes that Vindicta lost her way and says that her rage has become indiscriminate. 33]53]

- Abrams,54] Kelvin,55] Paige56] and Warden57] are sympathetic to Vindicta's cause.

- Rem likes that Vindicta also doesn't wear shoes.61]

- Drifter thinks Vindicta is fun,62] although her lack of preference for murder does prevent him from liking her.63]

- Wraith shows interest in getting Vindicta to assassinate people for her.27]

## Removed Lore

### Neon Prime - Hornet

Vindicta was named Hornet in Neon Prime.

Hornet was the leader of Strike Team Endeavor, a once decorated black ops group trained by The Alliance Special Forces turned enemy of the state.64]65]

Hornet was voiced by Jeannie Tirado, who currently voices Ivy.

All heroes have abilities that are unique to them. Abilities are unlocked by spending ability points, with costs varying between 1, 3, or 5 points depending on the level of the ability acquired. An abilities use is limited by cooldowns and charges. Most abilities are disabled when a hero is silenced or stunned.

Spirit Strike is a Tier 1 Спиритизм Item.

It has a passive which causes the hero's melee attacks to deal additional spirit damage and reduce the target's spirit resist. This effect has a cooldown of 8 seconds.

Spirit Strike is a Component of Похищение спиритизма.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

Superior Duration is a Tier 3 Спиритизм Item that provides Bullet Resist and extra Duration for your Items and Abilities.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200 (its Component, Увеличитель длительности, makes up 1,600 of the cost).

## Notes

- Ability duration affects your ability, item, buff, and debuff durations.

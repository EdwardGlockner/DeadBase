Mina is a Hero in Deadlock; a glass cannon that delivers quick bursts of Spirit damage at range with her passive, Love Bites. She can transform into bats and escape when things get a bit too messy. Each time she triggers Love Bites, her vampiric powers grow stronger. This builds toward her ultimate, Nox Nostra, which unleashes a massive swarm of bats that hunt down and silence nearby enemies.

Mina Ha was an ambitious and rich girl betrothed to American royalty, the eldest heir to the Fairfax Industries fortune. However, her hopes were dashed when her fiancé was assassinated prior to the wedding. Her parents, seeking only the best for their daughter, contracted a vampire to turn Mina into a vampire and grant her eternal life. Now learning her new powers and navigating vampire high society, Mina's ambitious eye is set on becoming the next Viscount of New York.

Mina was released on August 18, 2025 as the first hero in the Six New Heroes update.

## Trivia

- Mina is named after the literary character Mina Harker, from Bram Stoker's Dracula. Ironically, in the novel, Mina was stalked by Dracula and never becomes a vampire herself.

- Mina's ultimate Nox Nostra means "Our Night" in Latin.

- Mina is the second hero to use "vampire" as a codename (VampireBat), as there is another hero referenced in the localization files as "hero_vampire", but it has been removed from the game.

- Mina's family is implied to be East Asian, due to the "Ha" last name. Her name is also written in Asian localizations as a traditional Asian name, unlike other heroes.

- Mina was one of the heroes in a private build who was accidently leaked on stream in May 2025.3] Mina's model was leaked by an anonymous source along with a WIP version of the shop UI, ahead of the Shop Rework update.

- Mina's pistol New Money has the words "Nuit Rouge 7" ("Red Night 7" in French) inscribed on the side, similar to perfume labels. This does not appear in the weapon art.

- "Grabbing a bite in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Mina in the Hideout.

- Mina holds her pistol in her left hand, like Lady Geist. While this is done for gameplay purposes so her umbrella doesn't cover her model, it could also reflect her admiration for Geist.

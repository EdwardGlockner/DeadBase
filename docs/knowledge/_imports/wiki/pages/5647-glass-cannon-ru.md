Glass Cannon is a Tier 4 Оружие Item that grants increased Weapon Damage while reducing Max Health. Its passive effect causes hero kills to grant permanent Fire Rate (up to 8 stacks), with death removing one stack.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

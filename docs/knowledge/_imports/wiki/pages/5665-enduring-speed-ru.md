Enduring Speed is a Tier 2 Живучесть Item that provides Move Speed and Bonus Health to the purchasing hero.

Its passive effect grants the user Movement Slow Resist.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600. Its component, Беговые ботинки, makes up 800 of the cost.

Enduring Speed is a Component of Громила.

## Hidden stats

Enduring speed also provides a 30% Dash Distance Reduction Resist.

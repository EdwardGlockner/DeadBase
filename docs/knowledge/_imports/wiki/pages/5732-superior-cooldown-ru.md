Superior Cooldown is a Tier 3 Spirit Item that provides bonus Health Regeneration and reduces the cooldown of all abilities of the purchasing hero.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200 (its Component, Сжатая перезарядка умений, makes up 1,600 of the cost).

## Trivia

- Cooldown Reduction works on both abilities and items.

- When bought Cooldown Reduction is applied retroactively to abilities already on cooldown.

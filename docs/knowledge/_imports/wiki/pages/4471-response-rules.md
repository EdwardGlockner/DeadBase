This page lists triggers for various voice lines, also known as responses. These triggers are controlled by the game files called response_rules and generated_response_rules.

An enemy's location can be pinged by aiming at the enemy and clicking Mouse 3, or clicking at the enemy's icon in the Minimap. This will also highlight the enemy in red for the player and allies.

### Missing Enemy

A missing enemy can be pinged by holding Tab and clicking Mouse 3 at the enemy's portrait.

### Warn Ally

An ally can be warned of danger by aiming at them and clicking Mouse 3, or holding Tab and clicking at their portrait or Minimap icon with Mouse 3.

## Patrons

### Match Start

Patrons' Match Start lines are played in the zipline at the beginning of the match. They are hero-specific.

### Big Healing

Big Healing lines are played when a Hero has healed its allies for a total of 5000 HP.

## Unused Lines

### Win and Lose

Win and Lose are lines in which the Heroes react to either winning or losing the match. There are specific lines for winning or losing short games (less than 30 minutes) and long games (more than 50 minutes).

### Pass on zipline

"Pass on zipline" lines are intended to be played when two ally Heroes cross paths on a zipline. Based on the context, the Hero who says it is going back to the base.

### Killed By

"Killed By" lines were responses to being killed by specific enemy Heroes. These lines were only present in Neon Prime.

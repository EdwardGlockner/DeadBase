### Map Spawns

- Golden Statues have a chance to drop a permanent Cooldown Reduction buff.

- Powerup runes can provide a temporary Casting buff that provides +12% to +25% Cooldown Reduction for 160 seconds (scaling with game time from 5 to 40 minutes).

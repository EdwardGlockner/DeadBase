Teleporters can be used to quickly traverse the map horizontally. There are currently 2 pairs of teleporters on the Map, next to the underground Secret Shops, which are available to use from the beginning of the match. Teleporters take four seconds to channel after entering the booth, and a Hero will gain a small speed boost after teleporting.

Teleporter locations appear on the Minimap as an icon of a person stepping through a portal, facing in the direction of the corresponding exit.

## Notes

- Soul Urn couriers cannot use teleporters or Ziplines when delivering the urn.

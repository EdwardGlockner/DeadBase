Mid-Boss, also known as the Mid Boss, The Boss or Temple Guardian, is a powerful neutral jungle boss with strong attacks and a regenerating shield to defend itself. Teams of Heroes may attempt to kill Mid-Boss which immediately grants permanent stats for the team that defeats it, as well as an opportunity to obtain Rejuvenator Credits, allowing heroes to revive upon death.

### Rejuvenator Credit

The Rejuvenator credit grants all team members a buff that allows them to revive with 100% health in the place of their death after 3s. When a player dies, they respawn in place, lose the buff, and their team’s Rejuvenator credits are reduced by 1 (each player can consume only one credit).

The Rejuvenator credit lasts 3 minutes. The top UI bar displays the remaining Rejuvenator credits. Once all credits are used, the buff is removed from all team members.

## Respawn

The Mid-Boss respawns 7 minutes after its first death. Then it respawns 6 minutes after its second death, and 5 minutes after its third and subsequent deaths.

A timer until the Mid-Boss respawn can be seen as its icon fills up in the minimap. Pressing Tab shows players the exact time in minutes/seconds.

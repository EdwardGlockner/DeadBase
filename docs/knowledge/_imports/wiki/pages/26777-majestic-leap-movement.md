## Majestic Leap Cancel

During the descent from the air, you are able to conserve some momentum and go further by air jumping. By doing this, you cancel Majestic Leap. This is called a Majestic Leap Cancel.

This does not get rid of leap state.

Majestic Leap Cancelling allows you to travel further than a regular Majestic Leap. This can allow you to escape further or chase down an enemy player.

To execute, activate Majestic Leap, then descent. While descending, jump to cancel Majestic Leap and maintain the momentum.

Powerups, also known as Bridge Buffs or Runes, are temporary buffs that spawn as a glowing stone on the bridges connecting two buildings near the Urn spawn and dropoff points, starting at 5 minutes and then every 5 minutes thereafter. They require a Heavy Melee Attack to claim.

Powerups are displayed on the Minimap with unique icons indicating their type.

Powerups start weaker and grow stronger (from 5 minutes to 40 minutes). Exact values are the following:

- Starts at +15 Spirit Power and +12% Cooldown Reduction for 160s

- Ends at +65 Spirit Power and +20% Cooldown Reduction for 160s

- Starts at +12% Fire Rate and +35% Ammo for 160s

- Ends at +35% Fire Rate and +70% Ammo for 160s

- Starts at +2 Stamina, +1.5m/s Sprint Speed, +20% Stamina Regeneration Rate, and +40% Zipline Speed for 160s

- Ends at +4 Stamina, +4m/s Sprint Speed, +50% Stamina Regeneration Rate and +80% Zipline Speed for 160s

- Starts at +200 Bonus Health and +4 (fixed) Health Regen for 160s

- Ends at +750 Bonus Health and +40 (fixed) Health Regen for 160s

These temporary stat increases do not appear in the in-game stats, but they are listed as active effects on the left side of the player's HUD. The duration of powerup effects appears in the lower left of the HUD, above the soul count. When another player has the powerup, the icon is shown below their healthbar.

Bridge buffs will be randomized at the 5 minute spawn, but at 10 minutes are guaranteed to spawn the other 2 buffs. (There might be an order to this, ie a bridge that spawns the gun buff at 5 minutes will always be a spirit buff at 10 minutes, unless the spirit buff was paired with the gun buff at 5 minutes.)

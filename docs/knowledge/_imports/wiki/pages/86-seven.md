Seven is a Hero in Deadlock. A megalomaniac scientist who substitutes his absence of ethics with a wide array of electric attacks, Seven excels in rapidly taking control of the battlefield by denying areas with Lightning Balls and Surging his gun to punish his foes for staying close together. After putting his enemies in their place with Static Charge, Seven is free to discharge himself into a Storm Cloud and fry them alive.

It is unknown what actions led to Seven being imprisoned within Lost Whisper. However, Seven managed to turn what was meant to be his execution into the bloody murder of his captors. Finally free and waiting within the underbelly of the Cursed Apple, he wishes to reach "apotheosis" by completing the ritual.

## Trivia

- Seven was named Gigawatt in Neon Prime.

- Seven's chestplate has numbers that form a Gorowase, a form of Japanese wordplay with numbers. 4279 can be read as Shi (4) Ni (2) Na (7) Ku (9) = Shininaku (死になく), or "Become unkillable" (rough translation).

- Seven's name may be a reference to Numerology, in which the number 7 is associated with spirituality and the search for knowledge.2]

- Seven was the only hero with a limping animation which played when he was low on health. This has since been removed.

- "Plotting in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Seven in the Hideout.

- Seven's character select line "With these hands I will destroy, and with these hands I will rebuild" is a quote from the song The Good Doctor by The Protomen.

Grey Talon is a Hero in Deadlock. He is a clever, long-range combatant that uses his powerful bow in combination with traps and mystical flight to pin down and destroy his foes. With the ability to charge his arrow shot for powerful effect, Grey Talon can harry his foes at a distance before launching a guided Spirit Owl to finish them off.

A founder of the monster hunting organisation The Baxter Society, Grey Talon happily retired after 40 years of service to support his extended family. He was living well until he was alerted that that his son and daughter-in-law had died in a mysterious house fire. Now, he hunts the perpetrators of the arson, bow in hand.

## Background

### Lore

Grey Talon's motivation in the ritual is to bring retribution to the Friends of Humanity, an extremist neopolitical group who he believes are behind the murder of his family.1]2]3]

Grey Talon is known to be dangerous, having the most confirmed kills out of everyone in the Baxter Society.4] Grey Talon's talents and commitment to the society earned him a reputation as a "legend".5] Even though Grey Talon is retired, the Baxter Society still heavily respects him.6]

Grey Talon's spirit owl is named Rohan.7] His son was named Nathan.8] It's implied that Grey Talon's wife is still alive and wouldn't approve of his quest for revenge.9]

Grey Talon has visited South Ixia and said it was "beautiful".10]

Grey Talon was handsome in his younger years.11]

## Character interactions

- Most characters respect Grey Talon and dislike fighting him.12]

- Grey Talon and Vindicta have hunted the Friends of Humanity together and regard each other as friends.13]14]15] Vindicta calls Grey Talon by his first name and believes that, if her father was like Grey Talon, she would never have died during the Salem Witch Trials.16]

- Silver can't believe Grey Talon stood up for Shiv.22] Venator warns Grey Talon that, if Shiv slips up, he won't be able to protect Shiv from the Swords of Saint Benedict.23]

- Grey Talon and Drifter have a long history with each other. Drifter refers to Grey Talon by his first name.24] While Grey Talon despises Drifter,25]26] Drifter holds Talon in high regards.27]28] Grey Talon suspects Drifter could have killed his family, although Drifter affirms that if he did murder them, he would have made Grey Talon watch.29] There was a particularly brutal interaction between the two in Oklahoma.30]

- Grey Talon respects Dynamo's courage for enduring his body being torn apart and wishes he had the same optimism Dynamo has.31]

- Abrams and Paige remind Grey Talon of his daughter-in-law.32]33]

- Haze and Grey Talon know each other. She refers to him by his first name and sent a card when his son Nathan died. They haven't seen each other for "a while".8]34]

- Calico used to work with Grey Talon in the Baxter Society. While Grey Talon is disappointed in Calico for becoming an assassin,35] he doesn't resent her and was content to leave her alone.36]37]38] Calico views Grey Talon as a "relic of a bygone era",39] but also says she will mourn his death if she kills him in-game.40]

- Warden once looked up to Grey Talon and heavily respects him.41]42] When on opposing teams, Warden is shocked that Talon would willingly work for the Patrons.43] While Grey Talon appreciates Warden's conviction,44] he believes that both he and Warden live "hollow lives."45]

- Kelvin is sorry for Grey Talon, but insists to him that his rage won't bring his family back.46]47]

- Mo & Krill like Grey Talon for his war against the Friends of Humanity.2]

- Holliday likes Grey Talon due to their shared goal of revenge.48] Calico compares Holliday's introverted personality to Grey Talon's.49]

- Yamato respects Grey Talon for his age and skill.50]51]

- Viscous shows concern for Grey Talon's sadness and offers The Cube to him to try and cheer him up.52]

- Rem refers to Grey Talon as "grandpa" and wants to play with him.53]54]

- While trapped in the Baroness, The Doorman taunts Grey Talon by saying that before his retirement, he prioritized his work in the Baxter Society over his family and "robbed them of a father".55]

- Billy,56] Calico57], Lady Geist58], Mina59], McGinnis,60] Silver,61] Viscous62] and Vyper63] comment on Grey Talon's health, due to his age.

## Trivia

- Grey Talon's current model is inherited from his design in Neon Prime, in which he was called Orion. He had an animal labeled "archer_pet", which was reused as the creature in Trapper's cage.

- Grey Talon's bow was originally called Koschei.

- Grey Talon is currently the only hero whose Bullet Damage is directly affected by Spirit Power.

- "Mourning in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Grey Talon in the Hideout.

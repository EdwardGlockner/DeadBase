Superior Duration is a Tier 3 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200. Its component, Duration Extender, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

## Notes

- Healing items such as Healing Rite, Healing Nova and Rescue Beam cannot be increased, as they heal for a fixed value instead of per second.

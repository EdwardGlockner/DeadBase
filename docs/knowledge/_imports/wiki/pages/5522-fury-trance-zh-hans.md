Fury Trance is a Tier 3 活力 Item that provides Bullet Lifesteal and Bonus Health. Its active gives the user increased Fire Rate and Spirit Resist, at the cost of being Silenced and not able to use or recover Stamina for its duration.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200, with its Component, 子弹回复, making up 1,600 of the cost.

Mystic Reverb is a Tier 4 Спиритизм Item that provides Spirit Lifesteal.

It has a passive that allows an imbued ability to Slow the target and nearby enemies on hit, then deals extra damage to all affected targets after a delay.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

## Notes

- The debuff effect is applied to the first enemy hit. If the imbued ability is an area-of-effect and it hits multiple heroes, only one enemy will receive the debuff.

## Known Bugs

- The initial reverb damage can kill the primary target, but is non-lethal to secondary targets, leaving them with at least 1 hp remaining.

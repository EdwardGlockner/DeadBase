Cadence is an unreleased Hero in Deadlock. She is an Ixian woman and the lead singer of the jazz band Cadence and the Requiem Boys.

## Background

### Lore

Cadence is the lead singer of the jazz band Cadence and the Requiem Boys. She sings the hit song "Paying the Price".1] She is a side character in Ivy's visual novel.

Cadence is Ixian.2]

## Character interactions

- Плющик works for Cadence in one of her visual novel paths.

- Абрамс accidentally calls Cadence "Candice".3]

- Инфернус, Мо и Мелкий, Заточка and Вязкус apologize when killing Cadence.4]5]6]7] Тень says she's her "favorite jazz singer".8]

- Макгиннис is "not a fan" of Cadence.9] Холлидей tells her to "get a real job".10]

- Инфернус wants to watch Cadence's set after the ritual.11]

## Trivia

- Cadence's current character model is adapted from Neon Prime's Sumo. She is a large woman wearing a viking helmet, based on the "fat lady" opera singer stereotype. She currently doesn't have animations.

- Two of Cadence's abilities use music from Cara Mia Addio, the "turret opera" song from the end credits of Portal 2.

- Cadence's placeholder minimap icon uses an image of the Neon Prime version of Haze.

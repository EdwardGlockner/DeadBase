List of voice lines for Wrecker, as well as lines from other characters referencing Wrecker.

Some of Wrecker's lines are spoken in a fictional language, believed to be an updated version of Ozkavosh from Dota 2.

The style of voice acting and filter is very similar to Dehaka from Starcraft II/Heroes of the Storm.

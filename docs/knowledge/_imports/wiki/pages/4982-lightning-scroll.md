Lightning Scroll is a Tier 4 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400. Its component, Mystic Slow, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

Notable interactions

- Lightning Scroll does trigger for ultimates that deal Pure Damage otherwise, such as Shiv's Killing Blow execution, if the player survives with Cheat Death.

- Silver triggers Lightning Scroll whenever any ability or attack hits an enemy while she is transformed.

- Vindicta's Assassinate triggers Lightning Scroll independently for every shot, including shots on the same target.

Radiant Regeneration is a Tier 3 Спиритизм Item. It has a passive effect that grants the player bonus Health Regeneration when they deal Spirit Damage to enemy heroes. Its second passive provides Move Speed and a small Heal when you cast an ability.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200, with its Component, Мистическое восстановление, making up 800 of the cost.

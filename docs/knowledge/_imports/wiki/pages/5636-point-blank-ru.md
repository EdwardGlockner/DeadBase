Point Blank is a Tier 3 Оружие Item that grants increased Bonus Health and Melee Resist. Its passive increases the user's Weapon Damage while close to their target, and causes their bullets to apply Movement Slow.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200 (its Component, Ближняя дистанция, makes up 800 of the cost).

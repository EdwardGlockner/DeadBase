Fortitude is a Tier 3 Vitality Item that grants extra bonus health. After a duration of time with no incoming damage, you gain a percentage of max health regen. When above a percentage of health, weapon damage and movement speed will increase.

When the Fortitude buff is active, it appears as a glowing orange Ouroboros symbol in the player's back.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200 (its Component, Добавочное здоровье, makes up 800 of the cost).

Trophy Collector is a Tier 3 Vitality Item that provides Sprint Speed and Out of Combat Regen. Its passive grants a Stack whenever the user gets a kill or assist. Each stack gives the user extra Health, Sprint Speed, Ability Range, and Souls per Minute.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600, with its Component, 疾跑靴, making up 800 of the cost.

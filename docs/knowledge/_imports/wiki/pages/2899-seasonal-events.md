## Winter

### 2025

The 2025 Winter event ran from December 15 to January 20.

During the event, all players received a unique Snowball item that could be leveled up by winning matches with different Heroes, which also unlocked ornaments for the Christmas tree in the Hideout. Players who participated in the Winter challenge also unlocked a souvenir above their fireplace in the Hideout.

The Shopkeeper also received new holiday-themed lines for all heroes, and Drifter has holiday-themed songs when using Eternal Night.

### 2024

The 2024 Winter event ran from December 17 to January 17.

It added a new main menu animation, remixed music for the menu and shops, and Winter-themed decorations in the streets, buildings, and Curiosity Shop. The Shopkeeper featured a Santa mask and unique lines. In addition, the Denizens were modelled after snowmen. Dynamo and the Newscaster had unique holiday voice lines.

All the mainline Heroes received unique Winter skins, which could be unlocked by completing their respective challenge. A list of which skins were unlocked during the event can be seen in the "Showcase" tab in the player's profile, next to the "Match History" button. Players who participated in the Winter challenge also unlocked a snow globe above their fireplace in the Hideout.

#### Skin challenges

As the winter skin challenges weren't listed in-game or in patch notes, the challenges were discovered and shared by the player community over the duration of the event.

These challenges could be achieved only in a normal match or a labs match, not in a custom game or against bots. The conditions for each challenge must be completed in one match.

Winter skins were tracked in-game by a Santa hat icon in the main menu that showed how many skins had already been unlocked. Heroes with Winter skins had the Santa hat icon and a red background in the Heroes page.

The skins can be tested in sandbox mode or custom games (with cheats enabled) with the console commands ent_fire player setbodygroup hat,1 and ent_fire player setbodygroup xmas,1.

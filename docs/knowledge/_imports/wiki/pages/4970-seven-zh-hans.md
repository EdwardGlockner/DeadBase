## 背景故事

### Lore

Seven claims he was imprisoned in Lost Whisper because he "offended small-minded simpletons with fragile egos". He says he "doesn't like" murder, but rather is "indifferent" to it.1]

Seven has done research on the Astral Gates, and he credits Professor Dynamo as an influence in his work. He also tells Dynamo that he has loved someone.2]

Seven's goal of "apotheosis"3] means the glorification of a subject to divine level,4] implying that Seven wants to become a god, or gain godlike powers.

On the run from the OSIC, Seven currently lives in New York's old subway tunnels. He tells Mo & Krill he has "particular needs" regarding the creation of new tunnels.1]

The New York Oracle reporter Allie Kincaid is writing a story on Seven.5]6]

## 角色趣闻

- Seven during Deadlock Pre-Alpha (Neon Prime) was named Gigawatt.

- Seven is the only character who parries with his back.

- Seven's clothes has numbers that form a Gorowase, a form of Japanese wordplay with numbers. 4279 can be read as Shi (4) Ni (2) Na (7) Ku (9) = Shininaku (死になく), or "Become unkillable" (rough translation).

- Seven's name may be a reference to Numerology, in which the number 7 is associated with spirituality and the search for knowledge.16]

- Seven is currently the only hero with a limping animation which plays when he is low on health.

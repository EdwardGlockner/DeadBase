Metal Skin is a Tier 3 Vitality Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

- Metal Skin does not block Paradox's Kinetic Carbine, since it deals Spirit Damage.

### Weapon damage abilites

The damage of all these abilities is blocked by Metal Skin.

| --- | --- |

| Venator | Gutshot |

## Trivia

- Metal Skin shows a VFX of the alchemical symbol for Mercury when activated. Mercury, or quicksilver, is the only metallic element that is liquid at standard temperature.

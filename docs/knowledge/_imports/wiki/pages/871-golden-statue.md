Reference extract:

Golden Statues are small breakable gold objects found in various set locations around the map. When broken, they have a chance to drop a pick-up that grants a permanent stat buff.

- Sinner's Sacrifices

- Mid-Boss

- Soul Urn

- Eternal Gift (Street Brawl)

Buffs received from these sources are random and follow the same level scaling as the statues.

Note: Powerups also grant buffs, but unlike statue buffs those are temporary, have a different scaling, and scale linearly instead of having fixed values.

## Buffs Granted

### Permanent buffs granted

| Stat | LVL 1 (0 min) | LVL 2 (10 min) | LVL 3 (30 min) |

| Max Ammo | +3% | +5% | +7% |

| Cooldown Reduction | +0.5% | +0.75% | +1% |

| Weapon Damage | +3% | +4% | +6% |

| Max Health | +15 | +20 | +30 |

| Spirit Power | +2 | +3 | +4 |

Icefrog is a Valve developer. He is best known for his work as the lead developer of Dota 2.

Icefrog was reportedly involved with early development of Neon Prime and Deadlock.1]

In a January 2024 interview, Deadlock artist Vasili Zorin said that Icefrog was working in the game at the time.2]

Icefrog's name was noticed by players on the Six Heroes update Steam post, confirming his involvement on Deadlock.

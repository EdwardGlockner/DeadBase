Ethereal Shift is a Tier 4 Spirit Item.

Ethereal Shift has an active ability which can be used to become Ethereal and nearly immobile for a short duration. Afterward, the activating hero gains bonus Movement Speed, Spirit Power, and Spirit Resist.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

## Notes

- During ethereal shift, caster can only float around slowly at 3 m/s. Caster does not fall if mid-air.

- Casting ethereal shift does not reset jump/dash, even if mid-air when cast.

- Casting ethereal shift cancels any abilities currently being channeled.

- During ethereal shift, caster can still turn their camera and character.

- During ethereal shift, the caster's allies can still target them with abilities and items like Rescue Beam or Bebop's Grapple Hook, allowing for heals and/or repositioning.

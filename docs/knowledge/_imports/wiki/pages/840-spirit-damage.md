Spirit Damage is a form of damage dealt by most abilities and some items. It is modifiable by obtaining Spirit Power, and scales with the respective ability or item's Spirit Scaling. Certain items will deal additional Spirit Damage if a condition is met.

Continue Dismiss

Attacks from Walkers, Shrines and Patrons deal Spirit Damage.

## Sources

There are several items that deal Spirit Damage, but called in a different name or applied in a specific manner. (Not a comprehensive list)

- Alchemical Fire deals increasing Spirit Damage per second in an area.

- Mystic Shot makes the player's bullet deal 40 x1.2 Spirit Damage every 9 seconds.

- Tesla Bullets deals 33 x0.19 Shock Damage on hit and on jump.

- Torment Pulse deals 25 x0.23 Pulse Damage every 1.4s (scaling with Spirit Power).

- Toxic Bullets deals 1.9% x0.005 Bleed Damage based on current health per second.

See also Spirit Power for a list of items that grant Spirit Power.

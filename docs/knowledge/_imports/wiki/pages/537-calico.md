Calico is a Hero in Deadlock, a former monster hunter turned hired assassin. Possessing her cat, Ava, Calico uses the shadows to her advantage to surprise her foes, slinging Gloom Bombs and attacking with a Leaping Slash for damage and healing. By Returning to the Shadows, she can turn any fight to her advantage, disappearing after a fight or engaging again when the enemy least expects her.

As a contract killer, Calico is glad to finally be earning significant money by assassinating other people. Despite the good that the Baxter Society did by slaying monsters, it simply didn't pay well enough for her.

Calico was added to Hero Labs on October 24, 2024, and officially released on January 17, 2025.

## Trivia

- Calico was called Nano in Neon Prime.

- At one point in development, Calico's gameplay style was inspired by Dota's Broodmother.

- Calico was an arms dealer in her original background.1] Her background was changed when she was officially released on January 17, 2025.

- Ava is a Sphynx cat. Ironically, she's not a Calico cat, as her skin has no spots of different colors. (Despite the lack of fur, Sphynx cats can have Calico markings.)

- "Playing With Ava in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Calico in the Hideout.

- When in the Hideout's Atrium, switching to Ava and then running near the smaller version of her will cause the kitten to follow you for the duration of the spell.

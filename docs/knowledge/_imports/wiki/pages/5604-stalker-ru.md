Дрифтер is a Tier 2 Оружие Item that improves sneak attacks from behind the enemy heroes. It also reduces footstep sound distance and provides bonus health.

Attacking enemies from behind with your weapon or melee opens a wound dealing spirit damage over time and reducing their bullet resist. When nearby an enemy with a wound, heroes will gain increased move speed and be able to see the wounded target through any walls.

It can be purchased for 1,600 from the Shop.

## Notes

- Вязкус' Удар из лужи can proc Backstabber's passive, but you must be standing behind the target - regardless of where the Удар из лужи hits.

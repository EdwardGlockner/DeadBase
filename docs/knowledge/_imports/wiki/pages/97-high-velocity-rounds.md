High-Velocity Rounds is a Tier 1 Weapon Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

It is a component of Armor Piercing Rounds, Express Shot, Opening Rounds, and Sharpshooter.

March 6, 2026

- No longer grants +5% Fire Rate

- Now grants +8% Weapon Damage

- Bullet Velocity increased from +50% to +60% (these changes affect upgrades)

November 21, 2025

- Bullet Velocity increased from +45% to +50% (affects upgrades)

Celeste is a Hero in Deadlock. It's not every day that Coney Island's biggest star, The Dazzling Celeste, performs on the streets of Manhattan. Her show begins with Light Eater, where a deep breath lit by her blazing baton coats enemies in front of her, and grants increased damage against them. Following that up, she envelops herself in the prismatic shield of her Dazzling Trick, which explodes and silences nearby enemies who break it. Try not to get distracted by her gliding gracefully through the air, or by the dance of her Radiant Daggers, as you'll regret being underneath them when they rain down from above. As for her memorable final act, the Shining Wonder will make her allies go OOH and her foes go AGGHHH!

Celeste was released on February 9, 2026 as the fifth winner of the Old Gods, New Blood update contest.

## 武器

Carny Life's projectile flies in an arc and bounces on the first surface it hits. If there's an enemy in range at a narrow angle (~45º) from the shot, the projectile flies in their direction. If shot directly at an enemy, the projectile can ricochet to a second enemy at close distance and aligned at a narrow angle. It has no damage falloff.

Celeste cannot buy 裂光射击.

## Background

### Lore

Celeste's existence is questioned by skeptics who don't believe unicorns are real.1]2]3] Some of them believe that she glued a horn to her head.4] Only her closest friends know her true identity.5]

Celeste hates hurting other people, expressing remorse when killing enemies.6]7]

Celeste has frequently caused accidents when performing or training her acts.8]9] She used to close kids' birthday parties with her Light Eater ability until she faced multiple lawsuits.10] She also took Shining Wonder out of her act after causing too many concussions.11]

The Paradox Organisation have tried to steal Celeste's horn.12]

It's implied that Lenny is the friend who sold his soul.13]

## Trivia

- Celeste was named Pepper during early development.35]

- Celeste has unique poses for different directions of her dash.

- Celeste's Shining Wonder is nearly identical to Lich's ultimate ability from Dota 2 and is similar to the "Skipshot" ability of the unreleased hero Boho.

- Celeste had the most votes from a single player in the Old Gods, New Blood hero contest, at 813.36]

- Chickenwing and Ted were both teased 3 months before Celeste's backstory was revealed, in a joke post by developer Hopoo.37]

- "Cleaning Up Glitter in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Celeste in the Hideout.

- Celeste's appearance alludes to tattooed ladies who worked as sideshow attractions in the 19th and 20th centuries.

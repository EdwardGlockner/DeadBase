This article is part of the Deadlock Movement Wiki.

Status effects are buffs/debuffs applied to you that change properties of either you or your enemy. These usually buff attributes or reduce attributes (e.g. dash distance).

Below are a list of all the movement relevant status effects in the games:

- Movement Slow

- Stun

- Displace

- Immobilize

- Purge

Character-specific Status Effects:

- Petrify

- Tether

- Time Stop

- Sleep

- Hex

## Trivia

- Kali's previous model served as the base for Ivy, Vyper and Fathom, who reuse some of his animations. Vyper's files also replaced some of Kali's original files.

- Kali's model was removed in the Six New Heroes update.

- Kali was voiced by Phil Morris, who also voices Infernus.

- According to Neon Prime dialogue, Kali was a fighter in the Bear Pit, who at some point retired before making a less-than-triumphant return, being injured by Bebop.

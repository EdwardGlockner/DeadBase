Mystic Burst is a Tier 1 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

It is a component of Tankbuster.

- The additional damage, and the ability which triggered the additional damage, are applied to the target as separate instances of damage. The additional damage from Mystic Burst is dealt after the original spell damage.

Superior Cooldown is a Tier 3 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200. Its component, Compress Cooldown, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

It is a component of Transcendent Cooldown.

## Notes

- Because the cooldown refund when sold is based on the current cooldown, buying then selling this item in shop allows the player to accelerate their cooldowns proportional to the amount of time before it was refunded.

Damage per second, or DPS, is a function of a hero's Bullet Damage and Fire Rate. It refers to the amount of damage per second the hero's weapons will deal if continuously fired, and is calcuated by the amount of bullets shot per second multiplied by the bullet damage.

Values referenced from Hero Comparison Table.

Note: Due to a bug, The Doorman's and Paige's weapons have incorrect bullets per second and DPS values listed in the game UI. The values listed here are accurate.

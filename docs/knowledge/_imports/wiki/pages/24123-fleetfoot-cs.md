Fleetfoot is a Tier 2 Zbraň Item that grants Bullet Resist and increased Slide Distance. Its passive effect removes the Movement Speed penalty while shooting, allowing players to maintain speed while firing. Its active ability causes the user to immediately gain increased Move Speed and Slow Resistance for a short duration.

Fleetfoot is a Tier 2 Zbraň Předmět that can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

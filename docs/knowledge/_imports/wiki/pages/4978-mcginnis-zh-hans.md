## 背景故事

### Lore

McGinnis' first name is Margaret, but she goes by Maggie.1]

麦金尼斯被描述为「恶名昭彰的厌世者」。2]

Maximilian Fairfax "paraded McGinnis incessantly" to the New York elite when he first discovered her as a genius teenage inventor.3]

McGinnis was worried that she would lose her job when Fairfax lost the sovereignty of the Island of Cadenza to the US Government.4]5]

麦金尼斯喜欢喝啤酒。3]

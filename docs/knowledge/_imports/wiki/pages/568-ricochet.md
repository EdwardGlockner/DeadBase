Ricochet is a Tier 4 Weapon Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

## Notes

- Bullet based procs and effects are applied to ricocheted shots (with some exceptions such as Seven's Power Surge).

- Bullets do not bounce off of objectives.

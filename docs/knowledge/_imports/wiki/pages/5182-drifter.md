Drifter is a Hero in Deadlock. Something ancient has arrived in New York... a nameless horror known only as the Drifter. Relentless as time, as certain as death, once Drifter sets his eyes on a victim there's nothing they can do to stop him. They can try to run, but his Bloodscent drives him forward, leading him to isolated prey. Even if they manage to escape it doesn't matter, as Stalker's Mark allows Drifter to appear right behind his enemies before lunging in for the kill with a vicious Rend. Soon Eternal Night comes, and a faint whistling in the breeze will be joined by a chorus of screams.

A feral vampire with ties to Louisiana, Drifter has open contempt for the rules of vampire society, leaving a trail of carnage wherever he goes.

Drifter was released on August 27, 2025 as the fifth hero of the Six New Heroes update (fourth winner of hero vote).

Crimson Grasp deals the same amount of bullet damage to a target regardless of how many pellets hit per shot. It deals no damage beyond its max range.

- Stalker's Mark has no range limit, allowing Drifter to hit enemies across the entire map.

- Damage is based on target's current health, not their max health.

- Damage is divided up into 0.5s intervals, starting immediately as the projectile connects. (This works out to approximately the same overall damage per second stated in the tooltip, just dealt smoother.)

- Stalker's Mark can be applied to the Rejuvenator, allowing Drifter to teleport to it.

- If the duration expires during the cast time, Drifter will not teleport to the target.

## Trivia

- Drifter was one of the heroes in a private build who was accidentally leaked on stream in May 2025.2]

- Drifter leaves claw marks when sliding on a wall.

- Drifter's forward air dash and wall jump animation resembles the pounce animation of the Hunter, a recurring enemy in Valve's Left 4 Dead series. He also previously had other animations similar to the Hunter's attack and run.

- "Prowling in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Drifter in the Hideout.

- During the 2025 winter event, one of three Christmas songs would be whistled instead; Joy to the World, Twelve Days of Christmas, and God Rest Ye Merry, Gentlemen.

- Drifter has a variety of symbols on his right hand.

- Lash calls Drifter a "murder hobo",4] a term commonly used to describe RPG players who kill NPCs indiscriminately. Drifter's voice actor Matthew Mercer is well-known as the Dungeon Master of the Dungeons & Dragons webseries Critical Role.

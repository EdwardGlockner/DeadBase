Slide Bunnyhopping is a type of bunnyhopping that utilises sliding. This is the most efficient way for most people to bunnyhop.

Slide Bunnyhopping does not consume any stamina.

Slide Bunnyhopping consists of sliding then jumping; when doing certain actions, a flag can be added to your character that enables Slide Bunnyhopping. These actions include;

- Slide Conservation

- Dash Jumping

Any other action will lock you out from jumping during the slide (e.g. Dash Sliding will not allow you to jump). When sliding in a Slide Bunnyhop, sliding for less than 0.5 seconds at a time will inflict a speed penalty onto you, decreasing speed dramatically. For this reason, it is recommended to slide for more than 0.5 seconds at a time.

The first slide in a Slide Bunnyhop does not have this property applied to it, so instantly jumping for the first one is viable.

To execute a Slide Bunnyhop, first slide. When sliding, jump while holding the slide key. When you touch the ground again, you should slide on the ground again. You can repeat this as long as you have the necessary speed to slide (376 u/s).

Vortex Web is a Tier 4 元灵 Item that provides Ability Range.

It has an Active ability in which the player throws a squid that pulls enemies to the center of its effect radius and applies a 减速魔咒 on them.

It can be purchased for 6,400 from the Shop (its component, 减速魔咒, makes up 1,600 of the cost).

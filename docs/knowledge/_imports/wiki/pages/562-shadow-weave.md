Shadow Weave is a Tier 3 Weapon Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

## Notes

- Fleetfoot

- Restorative Locket

- Heroic Aura

- Untoggling Blood Tribute while Shadow Weave is active triggers the Ambush but keeps Stealth.

- Charging up Paradox's Kinetic Carbine, throwing Haze's Sleep Dagger, and casting Rem's Tag Along do not break Stealth.

- Sinclair's ability to redirect a Vexing Bolt and swap places with a Spectral Assistant do not break Stealth.

- Targeted abilities such as Mo & Krill's Combo and Curse's active effect still work as normal on heroes in stealth.

- The increase in fire rate after ending stealth is gradual, increasing to to the full 30% after about half a second.

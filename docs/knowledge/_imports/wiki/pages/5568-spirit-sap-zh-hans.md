Spirit Sap is a Tier 2 元灵 Item that provides Bonus Health. Its active ability allows the user to target an enemy hero and reduce their Spirit Resist and Spirit Power for a short duration.

Spirit Sap is a Component of 沉默之潮.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

Enchanter's Emblem is a Tier 2 Vitality Item that provides Spirit Resist and Out of Combat Health Regen to the purchasing hero. It was previously named Enchanter's Barrier.

Its passive effect grants the user additional Spirit Power and Cooldown Reduction when above 65% Health.

It can be purchased for 1,600 from the Shop.

Melee Lifesteal is a Tier 1 Живучесть Item that provides Melee Damage. In addition, melee attacks will heal the player, but this heal is less effective against non-heroes.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

Melee Lifesteal is a component of Витальный удар.

## Notes

- Striking multiple targets with one attack will not increase healing gained.

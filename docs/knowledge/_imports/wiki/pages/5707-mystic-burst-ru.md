Mystic Burst is a Tier 1 Спиритизм Item.

It has a passive which causes additional Spirit Damage to enemies who receive a single instance of ability spirit damage above a certain threshold from the purchasing hero.

Mystic Burst is a component of Гроза танков.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

## Notes

- Heroes with Spirit Lifesteal will heal from Mystic Burst's passive effect triggering.

- A small lightning shock visual is applied to the enemy hero when it is hit by Mystic Burst's passive effect.

- Damage amp and spirit resist reduction will not help you in achieving the damage threshold to trigger the passive.

- Damage reduction and the target's spirit resistance will not prevent the passive from triggering when it otherwise would.

- Vindicta uses assassinate on an enemy Warden. The baseline Assassinate on its own is not enough damage to kill the Warden, but the increased damage (due to the additional damage from triggering Mystic Burst) is enough to secure the kill. Since the additional damage from triggering Mystic Burst is applied after the Assassinate, it is technically the item's damage which secures the kill, not the Assassinate. Thus, Vindicta will not receive bonus souls from Assassinate's passive.

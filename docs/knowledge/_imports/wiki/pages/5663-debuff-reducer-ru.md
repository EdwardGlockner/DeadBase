Debuff Reducer is a Tier 2 Живучесть Item that reduces the duration of all negative effects applied to you.

It also provides Bonus Health to the purchasing hero.

Debuff Reducer is a Component of Debuff Remover and Неудержимость.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

Spirit Armor was a Tier 2 Vitality Item that provided Spirit Resist and Spirit Power to the purchasing hero.

Spirit Armor was a component of Improved Spirit Armor before it was removed. Improved Spirit Armor was renamed to Spirit Resilience.

It was able to be bought for 1,250 from the Shop.

Vampiric Burst is a Tier 4 活力 Item that provides Bullet Lifesteal, Bullet Resist and Bonus Health to the purchasing hero.

Its active ability grants the user Bullet Lifesteal, Fire Rate and Ammo.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400, with its Component, 子弹回复, making up 1,600 of the cost.

McGinnis is a Hero in Deadlock. She wields a machine gun and her abilities revolve around the usage of structures like turrets and walls, which she can create to support allies and hold down positions to the benefit of her team. If the situation goes awry, she can launch a powerful barrage of rockets at her enemies.

A talented engineer who started her career from a young age, McGinnis isn't fond of theoretical work, thus spending most of her time working in a mechanics shop, often meddling with the supernatural. While the ethics of brainstorming the occult war machine are grey at best, McGinnis isn't that fussed so long as she gets her paycheck

## Background

### Lore

McGinnis' wish is unknown, although it's implied that she's using the ritual as a chance to test some weaponry.1]2] In The Binding of Oathkeeper, McGinnis helps Lady Geist due to the chance to "harness the power of a God".3]

McGinnis' first name is Margaret, but she goes by Maggie.4]

Maximilian Fairfax "paraded McGinnis incessantly" to the New York elite when he first discovered her as a genius teenage inventor.5] McGinnis was famously known as a "kid genius".6]7]

As the head engineer of Fairfax Industries Military R&D department, McGinnis brainstorms the occult war machines.8]9] While it's unknown where McGinnis' inventions have been used, it's said that she may be resposible for millions of deaths.10]11] As a "notorious misanthrope", McGinnis doesn't really care about the deaths she's caused or the sins of Fairfax Industries.12]13]14]

While McGinnis doesn't love working for Fairfax Industries, she's content with being a "wage slave" since she gets paid a fortune.13]15] McGinnis was worried that she would lose her job when Fairfax lost the sovereignty of the Island of Cadenza to the US Government.16]17]

McGinnis is aware and makes it known that Spectral Wall usually backfires on her team.18]19]20]

McGinnis' equipment and weaponry is powered by souls.21]22]

McGinnis likes to drink beer.5]

## Character interactions

- In The Binding of Oathkeeper, one possible storyline includes McGinnis building a new ward for Dame Geist.

- McGinnis consistently mistakes Kelvin's name for "Kevin".23]24] All of McGinnis' pings for Kelvin include a variant where she calls him "Kevin".

- It's possible this is referring to Calico's old lore as a weapons dealer, as Calico used to use turrets while she was in Hero Labs.

- McGinnis wants to talk to Surin and "fix him".26]27]

- McGinnis is "not a fan" of Cadence.28]

- McGinnis wants to sample Myxo' goo.29]

- McGinnis doesn't frequent Infernus' bar, Jezebel's, because she finds it "too fancy". She does, however, want to go when infernus offers her a free drink.30]

- McGinnis is impressed by the craftsmanship used to create Bebop and shows interest in hiring his creator, Ms. Shelly.31]

- McGinnis offers help to remove Seven's headgear.32]

- McGinnis offers to upgrade Dynamo's body.33]

- McGinnis appreciates what Ivy has done for New York.34]35]

- McGinnis makes fun of Grey Talon and Infernus for not using guns.36]37]

- Pocket loathes McGinnis due to her work for Fairfax Industries.38]39]40]

- Seven criticizes McGinnis for her lack of creativity.41]

- The Doorman calls McGinnis "the brightest mind humanity has to offer".42]

- Mina and Wraith call McGinnis "Fairfax's pet genius".43]44]

- Paradox is interested in McGinnis' research and the secrets she holds for Fairfax.45]46] McGinnis has been trying to keep Paradox out of her lab.47]

## Trivia

- In Neon Prime, McGinnis was named Forge. She had a relationship with Astro (Holliday).48]49] She trained fighting with Lash in his dojo.50]51]

- McGinnis' design is similar to popular depictions of the American World War II cultural icon Rosie the Riveter - notably the red headband, blue shirt, jeans overalls, red socks and bulky physique.

- McGinnis and Bebop are currently tied for the highest base ammo count at 66.

- In The Binding of Oathkeeper artwork, McGinnis has a mermaid tattoo in her left shoulder.

- McGinnis' Heavy Barrage sound effects were changed to sound less like a real air raid siren.

- "Tinkering in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as McGinnis in the Hideout.

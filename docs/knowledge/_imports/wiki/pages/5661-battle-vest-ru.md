Battle Vest is a Tier 2 Vitality Item that provides Bullet Resist and Out of Combat Health Regen to the purchasing hero. It was previously named Combat Barrier.

Its passive effect grants the user additional Weapon Damage and Fire Rate when above 65% Health.

It can be purchased for 1,600 from the Shop.

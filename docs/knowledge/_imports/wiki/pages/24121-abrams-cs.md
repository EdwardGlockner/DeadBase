Abrams je Hrdina v Deadlocku. Drsný, nekompromisní bojovník zaměřený na střety s početnějšími skupinami nepřátel. Vyniká vysokou výdrží, silným poškozením a schopností vysávat život, díky čemuž dokáže zůstat v boji déle než většina ostatních. Díky silnému plošnému omráčení a ničivému nárazu ramenem dokáže Abrams oddělit křehčí cíle od zbytku týmu a připravit tak své více útočné spojence na jejich rychlou likvidaci.

Jako schopný a proslulý newyorský detektiv řešil Abrams případy sahající od únosů až po rituální vraždy. Jednoho dne se na jeho stole objevil záhadný svazek (Tom), doplněný vzkazem ‚Nedovol jim ho získat‘ napsaný onyxiovou krví. ## Background

### Lore

Abrams je Ixian.3] He was born in Ixia and seems to speak their native language.4]5] Unlike Infernus, Abrams does not demonstrate control over fire.

Abrams' goal in the ritual is to destroy the Tome.6]7]

Abrams expresses little control over the Tome.8]9] The Tome often tries to fly away, which is weaponised by Abrams and allows him to cast Seismic Impact.10]11]

Abrams likes to drink whiskey.12]13] He also frequents Jezebel's.14]15]

Outside of his case with the Tome, Abrams has also been investigating the Paradox Organisation.16] He once worked alongside Venator on a case in the past.17]

Since his car was firebombed, Abrams has been forced to use the bus to get around.18]

## Character interactions

- In one of the endings of The Binding of Oathkeeper, Abrams moves to Lady Geist's apartment.19]

- Abrams is surprised that Infernus has never visited Ixia.22]

- Abrams is friends with Celeste and seems to know she's secretly a unicorn.23]

- Abrams is sceptical that the Patrons will follow through with fulfilling his wish.24]25]

- Abrams plays in Wraith's weekly poker game alongside Mo & Krill.26]27] Wraith calls Abrams "Abe".28]

- Warden wants the tome to be destroyed.35]

- Grey Talon says his "stepdaughter" is Ixian, like Abrams.3] However, it seems that the intended term was "daughter-in-law", as evidenced by Talon's background and Abrams' response.

- Victor asks Abrams for help with uncovering his origins.36]

- Silver is old friends with Abrams, referring to him as "Abe".13] According to Silver, they used to make "bad decisions" before Abrams became "boring".37]

## Trivia

- Abrams was named Atlas in Neon Prime, and also known as Bull. The icon for Infernal Resilience previously had a bull design.

- Haze may call Abrams a "juggernaut".38] Samoa Joe voiced the character Juggernaut in the "Call of the Bladeform Legacy" arcana voice pack in Dota 2, and later in Dota Underlords.

- Abrams' Tome contains a ripped page matching Oathkeeper's sign.39]

- The first version of Abrams' Tome in-game used textures from TF2's Bombinomicon.

- In The Binding of Oathkeeper artwork, Abrams has spikes on his back.

- Abrams was the only hero who had unique animations in the shop menu screen. These animations have been removed.

- Abrams was made slightly taller in an unlisted patch change around November 2024.

- Abrams' updated model was first leaked in the 2024-12-06 Update, and later removed. Compared to the final model, this version had slightly different facial hair and still had his old book model.

- "Investigating the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Abrams in the Hideout.

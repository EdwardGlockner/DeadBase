Street Brawl is a fast-paced, 4v4, combat-focused game mode in Deadlock, offering a short game format designed for quick sessions. Street Brawl removes the standard soul economy entirely, placing players in a best-of-five series of skirmishes with the ability to obtain a fixed amount of items each round.

Before each round, players will be presented with three sets of three items to choose from. Players can choose to reroll one set of items each round. Players who queued for Street Brawl with the random hero option will be given an additional reroll for the first three rounds. Street Brawl has exclusive legendary items not available in standard matches. Additionally, both normal and legendary items can appear as "enhanced" versions, with improved numerical stats. If a player is presented with an item that normally builds into an item they have already chosen, they will be given another item of a lower tier as a bonus.

Having access to the same number of items reduces the likelihood of one particular player snowballing ahead of others, and players do not need to make decisions about what objectives to focus on. Each round, all abilities start ready to use, except for ultimate abilities, which start on a heavily reduced cooldown.

Street Brawl matches do not affect the player's matchmaking rating in standard mode.1]

Street Brawl was added in the Old Gods, New Blood update.

### Items

- Range and radius for all items is reduced by 10%

- Alchemical Fire: Damage reduced by 30%

- Cursed Relic: Duration reduced from 3.25s to 2.75s

- Golden Goose Egg: Disabled in this mode

- Mystic Expansion: Radius bonus reduced from +20% to +16%

- Greater Expansion: Radius bonus reduced from +30% to +25%

### NPCs

Denizens do not spawn in Street Brawl.

Troopers spawn faster and have starting Spirit Resist and increased Spirit Resist during overtime, and deal more damage to structures.

Structures have faster and increased resist decay over time.

Guardians have Backdoor Protection.

### Other differences

Players cannot pause in Street Brawl.

Healing in base is twice as fast as standard play.

There is no item limit. If a player gets more than 12 items, the older items are no longer visible in the UI but still equipped.

## Legendary Items

There are 17 Legendary items exclusive to this mode.

### Weapon

- Haunting Shot: Your next bullet deals additional Spirit Damage based on the enemy's current Health. This bullet is larger and pierces through enemies, applying a debuff which reduces the enemy's healing, movement speed, and damage outputs.

- Infinite Rounds: Your bullets have a chance to become unavoidable, piercing through enemies and ignoring their Bullet Resistance. You have infinite Ammo.

- Runed Gauntlets: Your next Melee pierces through parry. Landing a heavy melee reduces your existing Cooldowns.

### Vitality

- Celestial Blessing: On use, applies a cleansing effect to your whole team, replenishing their health and stamina while increasing their movement speed.

- Cloak of Opportunity: Occasionally block the next debuff that would apply movement lock, stunned, immobilized, chained, or slept to you, instead granting a temporary barrier and movement speed, as well as a temporary Unstoppable effect.

- Electric Slippers: While sliding, evade bullets and gain increased fire rate while dealing periodic Spirit Damage to nearby enemies.

- Eternal Gift: Every 2 seconds, provides a random permanent stat buff to your hero, while also occasionally dramatically reducing your next respawn timer.

- Nullification Burst: On use, removes any positive buffs on nearby enemies while blocking them from using any stamina or healing for a short duration.

- Seraphim Wings: Massively reduces your gravity, granting you large amounts of damage amplification and damage reduction while airborne. Also passively grants improved air control and allows for an additional air dash or air jump before landing.

- Shadow Strike: Any stamina use causes you to become temporarily invisible. Dealing damage with a melee attack while invisible applies periodic spirit damage while stealing bullet and spirit resistances.

### Spirit

- Frostbite Charm - Imbue an ability to grant it cooldown reduction and spirit power. When the imbued ability damages a target, it freezes them, dealing bonus spirit damage.

- Mystic Conduit - Passively provides a significant increase to ability range and cooldown reduction for self and nearby allies. Dealing high damage to an enemy Hero within a short period of time heals you and nearby allies.

- Mystical Piano - On use, strikes the target area after a short delay with a large piano, stunning and reducing the stamina of any enemies hit. For a short duration after the stun, enemies are dazed.

- Omnicharge Signet - Imbue an active non-ultimate ability to grant it charges. Already charge-based abilities gain even more charges.

- Prism Blast - On use, become untargetable and invincible for a short duration while damaging lasers rotate around you.

- Shrink Ray - On use, significantly reduces self or target ally hero's model size for a duration while granting significantly increased movement speed.

- Unstable Concoction - On use, grants massively increased movement speed, health, spirit power, and weapon damage for a short duration before you explode, instantly dying but also dealing large area damage based on your max health and stunning enemies.

## Strategy

Since Troopers don't drop Souls in Street Brawl, players are less encouraged to hit them. However, pushing waves is still valuable to gain heal from Banner Troopers and pressure the enemy's objectives.

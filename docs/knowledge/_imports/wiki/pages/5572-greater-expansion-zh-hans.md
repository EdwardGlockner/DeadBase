Greater Expansion is a Tier 3 Spirit Item that grants increased Spirit Resist and Ability Range to the purchasing hero. It was previously named Improved Reach.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200, with its Component, 秘术扩张, making up 800 of the cost.

Crushing Fists is a Tier 4 Weapon Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400. Its component, Melee Charge, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

## Affected Abilities

Some abilities scale with melee damage and are affected by this item's increase to Melee Damage.

| Hero | Ability | Base Damage | Light Melee Scaling |

| --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Bebop | Exploding Uppercut | 0.01 | ×1 |

| Bebop | Grapple Arm | 0 | ×0.7 |

| Billy | Bashdown | 0 | ×0.9 |

| Calico | Leaping Slash | 10 | ×0.8 |

| Drifter | Rend | 0 | ×1.2 |

| Silver | Boot Kick | 0 | ×0.9 |

| Silver (Transformed) | Go For The Throat | 0 | ×1.5 |

| Silver (Transformed) | Mauling Leap | 0 | ×1.5 |

| Viscous | Puddle Punch | 20 | ×0.6 |

| Yamato | Flying Slash | 0 | ×1 |

Several abilities use heavy melee scaling and will benefit from Crushing Fists' unique passive effect as well.

| Hero | Ability | Base Damage | Heavy Melee Scaling | Notes |

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Billy | Bashdown | 0 | ×0.5 | Only becomes Heavy Melee on T3 upgrade. |

| Drifter | Rend | 0 | ×0.55 | Only becomes Heavy Melee on T3 upgrade. |

| Viscous | Puddle Punch | 40 | ×0.6 | Only becomes Heavy Melee on T3 upgrade. |

Additionally, melee abilities also apply 1 stack of Crushing Fists' second unique passive effect, increased to 2 stacks for abilities that deal heavy melee damage.

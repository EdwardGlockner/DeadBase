Escalating Resilience is a Tier 3 Оружие Item that grants increased Max Ammo, Bonus Health, and Weapon Damage. Its passive effect grants the user additional Bullet Resist every time their bullets hit an enemy hero, up to a maximum.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200, with its Component, Увеличенный магазин, making up 800 of the cost.

## Notes

- Passive Bullet Resist Stacks can only be gained once per shot, meaning spread fire weapons like Абрамс, Карман, and characters using Веерный выстрел can't gain several stacks in one shot. This also includes characters like Вязкус and Ямато, who have alt-fire modes on their weapons that cause explosive damage.

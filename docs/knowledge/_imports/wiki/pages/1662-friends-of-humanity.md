The Friends of Humanity are a "neo inquisition" political group founded by the descendants of John Hathorne.1]2]

Their leader is also named John Hathorne, described as a "political firebrand"3] and a "controversial speaker".4]

## Summary

Vindicta wants to kill John Hathorne and eradicate the Friends of Humanity.5]6]7] She has already shot John's brother.8] Grey Talon suspects the group's members were behind the murder of his family, and is similarly pursuing them.9]10]

Hathorne has survived an assassination attempt.4] if Vindicta doesn't complete the ritual, she plans to assassinate him during his next public appearance at Columbia University.11] Haze warns Vindicta that murdering Hathorne will "galvanize" the Friends of Humanity.12]

Mo & Krill don't like the Friends of Humanity and support Grey Talon's actions.13] Infernus similarly supports Vindicta.14]

The Friends of Humanity have rallied in New York to protest against the US Government's aid to Ixia, amid the Ixian War.2]

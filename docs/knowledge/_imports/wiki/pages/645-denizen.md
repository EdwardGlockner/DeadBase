Denizens, also known as Neutral Creeps or Jungle Creeps, are a type of unit that are not controlled by any player. They are aligned to neither of the teams, and offer an alternative source of Souls.

Mid-Boss is a unique boss Denizen that spawns in the middle of the map. It spawns in at 0:00, and its first respawn occurs 7 minutes after its first death. Subsequent respawns occur after 6 minutes, then 5 minutes. It is very hard to kill by a single player, requiring at least 2-3 other players to assist early on. The Mid-Boss is a relatively easy fight mechanics-wise, but a difficult one based on stats. As it has an infinitely regenerating shield, it takes a lot of hits to deal damage to its large health pool. This camp is indicated by a yellow crest in the middle of the Minimap.

### Cockroaches

Cockroaches spawn in small groups in the tunnels inside Vents. They spawn in immediately and always give 1 soul when killed. Cockroaches will respawn 30 seconds after being killed. Unlike Denizens, they respawn independently of one another and can roam in a small area from their spawnpoint. Cockroaches do not attack Heroes. Rem's Lil Helpers can be sent to kill cockroaches, unlike Denizens.

## Souls

See: Souls#Denizens (Jungle Creeps)

Denizens drop varying amounts of Souls depending on their size and the overall duration of a match. The larger the Denizen, the higher the total Soul bounty they drop. If multiple players attack a Denizen, the Souls will be split evenly between all allied players who helped kill the Denizen. All Souls dropped by Denizens are Unsecured Souls.

The Denizen Soul bounty increase per minute is equal to 1.08% of the base Soul value. The starting value is from the start of the match, not when the Denizens are spawned.

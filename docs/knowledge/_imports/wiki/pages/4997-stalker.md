Stalker is a Tier 2 Weapon Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

- Stalker's "Close Range" is affected by Ability Range.

- Stalker procs off Melee Damage.

## Ability Triggers

Stalker can trigger off abilities that deal weapon or melee damage if the caster is within close range when the ability deals damage.

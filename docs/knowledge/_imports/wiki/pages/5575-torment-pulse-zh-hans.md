Torment Pulse is a Tier 3 元灵 Item that provides Bonus Health and Spirit Power to the purchasing hero. It periodically (every 1.5s, scaled with cooldown) deals Spirit Damage to the 2 closest enemies.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

## Notes

Torment Pulse can be useful for constantly activating ability and item effects that depend on dealing spirit damage, such as 粉碎护甲 and 伤上加伤.

## Trivia

- When controlling 遥控夜枭, Torment Pulse ceases to damage enemies based on its proximity to 灰爪’s body, and instead triggers based on the owl position. Although the pulses are happening at the owl location, the lightning VFX visually starts from the stationary body of Grey Talon.

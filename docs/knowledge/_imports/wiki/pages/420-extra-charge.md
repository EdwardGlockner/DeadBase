Extra Charge is a Tier 1 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

It is a component of Rapid Recharge.

## Notable interactions

- If Sinclair copies Vindicta's Assassinate with Extra Charge, they will start with 2 charges. After using Assassinate once, they can keep the copy indefinitely as long as they have one or more charges left.

Stamina Mastery is a Tier 3 Живучесть Item that gives Stamina, Stamina recovery, and Air Jump/Dash distance as well as letting the user do a second air dash and air jump. It was previously named Superior Stamina.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200 (its Component, Добавочная выносливость, makes up 800 of the cost).

## Notes

- Stamina Mastery no longer grants a bonus wall jump after the 2025-04-04 Update wall jump rework.

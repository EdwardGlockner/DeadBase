## Audience Participation

Audience Participation(by default, 4) is Sinclair’s fourth and ultimate ability. Upon activation on a target, it copies their ultimate ability for 10s. Upgrading Audience Participation upgrades the copied ultimate.

If there is tech for a character's ultimate, Sinclair can execute it too with Audience Participation. This includes:

- All ZMC

- All ability HMC

## Spectral Assistant Teleport Momentum Conservation

Spectral Assistant(by default, 2) is Sinclair’s second ability. Using it spawns an assistant, which you are able to teleport to at any time during the duration of Spectral Assistant.

To execute, cast Spectral Assistant, then teleport to it. Whatever momentum you had before the teleport will be taken with you.

Capacitor is a Tier 4 Оружие Item.

It has a passive ability that shocks enemy targets; and an active ability that deals damage, applies slow and removes all non-ultimate buffs on the affected enemy players.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400. Its Component, Тесла-пули, makes up 3,200 of the cost.

Hunter's Aura is a Tier 3 Оружие Item that grants Bonus Health. Its passive effect reduces all nearby enemy heroes Bullet Resist and Fire Rate. While there is only one player nearby, this effect is tripled.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

## Notes

- Passive Radius is affected by Ability Range.

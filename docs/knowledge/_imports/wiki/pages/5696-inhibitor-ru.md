Inhibitor is a Tier 4 Живучесть Item that provides Weapon Damage and Bonus Health to the purchasing hero.

Inhibitor has a passive effect causing bullets fired, on hit, to reduce the target's damage and Healing.

Inhibitor's effect appears as a downward red arrow in the player's back and cause the player's model to lose color saturation.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

## Notes

- Inhibitor's Damage Reduction Debuff effect will apply to enemy players with Unstoppable activated.

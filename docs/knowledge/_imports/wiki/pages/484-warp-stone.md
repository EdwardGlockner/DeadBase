Warp Stone is a Tier 3 Vitality Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

## Movement

When activated, Warp Stone teleports you forward in the direction of the caster's camera, emitting a distinctive sound as you warp. Warp Stone only allows you to warp to positions that have a line of sight from where you start from; you are unable to warp through objects or walls. Alongside this, using Warp Stone immediately stops the caster's velocity on all axes.

When performing on the ground, you will be in the air for a very short amount of time; this allows you to execute actions that are available while in the air.

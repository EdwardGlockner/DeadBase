Tankbuster is a Tier 3 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200. Its component, Mystic Burst, makes up 800 of the cost.

- Spirit Resist on target does not prevent Tankbuster from triggering. For example, an ability that deals 170 damage, when striking a target with Spirit Resilience will still trigger Tankbuster.

- Most other sources seem to not affect the trigger, but more testing is required to confirm that.

- The triggering of the additional damage is entirely unaffected by the target's barriers (or lack thereof).

- The additional damage, and the ability which triggered the additional damage, are applied to the target as separate instances of damage. The additional damage from Tankbuster is dealt after the original spell damage.

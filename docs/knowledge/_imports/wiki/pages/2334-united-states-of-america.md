The United States of America is a country on Earth in which the Cursed Apple is located.

## Description

The United States is composed of 52 states. The 50th State is the Island of Cadenza,1] the 51st is Puerto Rico,2] and the 52nd is South Ixia.3] Other known states include New York, Wyoming,4] Massachusetts,5]6] Illinois,7] Pennsylvania8] and Wisconsin.9]

There are at least 2 known Astral Gates located in US territory: New York and Puerto Rico.

The OSIC is an organization within the US government.

The US government is sending aid to Ixia as part of their military campaign, drawing opposition from the Friends of Humanity.10]

The US government is negotiating a sale of a part of Wyoming with the Djinn.4]11]

## Trivia

- Due to the divergences in the Deadlock timeline, it is currently unknown if Hawaii and Alaska are US states, as they joined the Union in the year 1959 in the real world.

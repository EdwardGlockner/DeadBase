Rebuttal is a Tier 1 Живучесть Item that provides Bonus Health and a shorter cooldown for your Parries. Its passive allows for successful Parries to Heal you for the damage parried and return that damage to the target. In addition, the user will be granted increased melee damage for a short time.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

Lifestrike is a Tier 3 活力 Item that makes your Melee attacks heal a significant amount of health on hit, as well as slowing the enemy. Lifestrike also grants increased Melee Damage and Bonus Health.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200, with its Component, 近战疗法, making up 800 of the cost.

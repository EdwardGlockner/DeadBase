## Branching Timelines

Due to the multiple choice aspect of the Deadlock visual novels, there are multiple potential timelines of events that happened shortly before and after the ritual.

## Trivia

- Some posters on the map used to show specific dates, such as a Jazz Festival poster dated 1927 and a Rescue Beam poster dated 1939. Those posters have either been edited or removed from the map over time.

- The synthetic fiber Kevlar, which the item Diviner's Kevlar is named after, was invented in 1965.

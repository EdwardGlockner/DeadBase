Fairfax Industries is an arms manufacturing corporation in Deadlock. They produce weapon items.

They are currently under sanction by the Environmental Protection Agency for improperly disposing of spiritual waste.6]

Various Fairfax-branded crates can be found across the Cursed Apple. Some stores have curtains with an "FF" logo.

A statue of Walter Fairfax, presumably an ancestor of Pocket and Maximilian, can be found in the garden behind the Baroness Hotel.

Fairfax Industries has a publishing arm called Fairfax Books. Dynamo carries one of their books in his bag.

Fairfax Industries has made a map of New York's subway lines, which can be seen in the subway tunnels.

### Eldritch Tactical Solutions

Eldritch Tactical Solutions, or ETS, is a Paranormal Military Company and a subsidiary of Fairfax Industries. Its workers are known as "soldiers of fortune". The New York Oracle states that it has been accused of committing war crimes by previous employees.7]

Pocket wants to dismantle ETS.8]

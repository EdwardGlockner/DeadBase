The Paradox Organisation is a guild of thieves who want to show the world that there's nothing they can't steal.

The members of Paradox work mostly independently, gathering together a few times a year to discuss their thefts.6]1] The thefts of each Paradox member is "based on individual interest and challenge".7]

The origins of the Paradox Organisation are unknown. Their oldest known theft seems to be one of Johann Geist's paintings, which happened 50 years before the events of the game.8]9]

## Thefts

The Paradox Organisation has had several artifacts on its radar. Some thefts have been successful, some have yet to be committed, and some have failed.

- Paradox has successfully stolen The Ghent Altarpiece, the Tome of Anubis, some form of blackmail on Fairfax,10] a hellenistic Bust of Homer,11] one of Johann Geist's paintings,12]9] Booth's Deringer,13] and the location of Seven's safehouse.14]

- The Paradox Organisation has shown interest in robbing Kelvin's home during the ritual. Paradox, the playable hero, has shown personal interest in Abrams' tome,15] Holliday's revolver,16] McGinnis' research and secrets she holds for Fairfax,17]18] Nashala,19] rare books Paige may have,20] Pocket's suitcase,21] and Yamato's sword.22]

- The Paradox Organisation has tried, and failed, to break into the Baxter Society's Manor House, although they haven't given up trying.23] They have also been unsuccessful at breaking into the Baroness Hotel.24] The Paradox Organisation keep trying to steal Celeste's horn.25]

## Trivia

- The Paradox Organisation is wary of Abrams.26]

- Paradox holds Kelvin in high regard.27]

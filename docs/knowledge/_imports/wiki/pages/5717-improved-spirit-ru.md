Improved Spirit is a Tier 2 Спиритизм Item that provides Spirit Power and Out of Combat Regen to the purchasing hero.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600, with its Component, Добавочный спиритизм, making up 800 of the cost.

Improved Spirit is a component of Безграничный спиритизм.

Mercurial Magnum is a Tier 4 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400. Its component, Quicksilver Reload, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

## Notes

- Mercurial Magnum is a Charge-Up item, thus not affected by Cooldown Reduction.

- Mercurial Magnum will always give the full Spirit damage on bullet hit bonus, regardless of charge-up.

- The passive buff has a fixed duration of 12 seconds.

The Paradox Museum is a pop-up gallery showcasing everything the Paradox Organisation steals.

## Summary

The Paradox Organisation steals priceless artifacts to be showcased at the Paradox Museum, with the intention of showing that anything can be stolen by Paradox.1] Once a sufficient number of artifacts are gathered, the museum is open to the public for a short amount of time.2]

The Paradox Museum isn't exclusively for art: it's also used to showcase stolen state secrets, celebrity paternity tests,1] and forms of blackmail on renowned individuals.3]

As a pop-up gallery, the Paradox Museum has no set location.1] Where Paradox stores their artifacts while the Museum is closed is unknown.

## Trivia

- Characters that have visited the Paradox Museum include but aren't limited toCalico,4] Dynamo5] and Lady Geist.6] Vyper has also visited the museum, but was kicked out.7]

- The items that are known to be showcased at the Paradox Museum include The Ghent Altarpiece,3] a hellenistic Bust of Homer,8] one of Johann Geist's paintings,9]10] the Tome of Anubis (whichRaven allegedly also has),3]11] and some kind of "blackmail on Fairfax".3]

- Calico and Dynamo both show adoration for the Paradox Museum.5]4] Lady Geist, on the other hand, describes it as "a multi-million exhibition with the emotional depth of a 10th grader writing "I will rage against conformity" in a poetry unit of Remedial English."12]

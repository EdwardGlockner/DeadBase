## 背景故事

### Lore

As a second generation Ixian immigrant,1] Infernus was born and raised in New York and has never visited Ixia.2]

He works at a bar called Jezebel's, with his boss Hank. Jezebel's, is under threat of being closed by the Alchemy Bureau for code violations.3]

## 角色互动

- Infernus' friends and regular patrons, like 亚伯兰, 灵魅 and 盖斯特夫人, call him Fern.4]5]3] Abrams calls Infernus "his bartender".6]

- 岚梦 has made a deal with Infernus.7]8]9]

- 灵魅 and Infernus have worked together in the past.10] He plays in Wraith's weekly poker game.11]

- 灵魅, 蝰邪 and 柒 tease Infernus for leaving his criminal life behind and becoming a bartender.12]13]

- Infernus wants to go on a date with 哈雷黛.14]

- 盖斯特夫人 likes Infernus' recipe of gin and tonic.15]

- 劳什 has an open tab at Jezebel's.16]

- Infernus wants to watch Cadence's set after the ritual.17]

- 麦金妮 doesn't frequent Jezebel's because she finds it "too fancy".12]

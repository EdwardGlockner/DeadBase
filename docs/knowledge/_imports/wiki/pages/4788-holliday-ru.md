Holliday is a Hero in Deadlock. She is a sheriff, relying on her aim with her trusty heirloom revolver and a litany of abilities to take down her enemies. With a Crackshot of her pistol, she can slow and damage her foes from a distance before launching an explosive Powder Keg with her Bounce Pad or a melee attack. To isolate her foes, she can wrangle them with her Lasso and drag them into the attacks of her allies.

Once a sheriff content with the small-town life, this would all change when a travelling musician known simply as "The Troubadour" began a gruesome killing spree; but when she tracked down and confronted him, he managed to flee. Intending to track him down, Holliday spent six months investigating and deduced that the killer's next target was New York. She's going to kill that evil son of a bitch.

Holliday was added to Hero Labs on October 24, 2024, and released officially on January 17, 2025.

## Background

### Lore

Holliday's visual novel will tell the story of her hunt for the Troubadour.

Holliday is the granddaughter of Wild West gunslinger Doc Holliday.1]2]3] She inherited her revolver from her grandfather.4]

Holliday had a sister.5] It is implied that she was murdered by the Troubadour.

Holliday's hometown is Macomb, Illinois.6]7] She doesn't like big cities like New York or Chicago.8]

Holliday has been married more than once.9]

## Character interactions

- As a sheriff, Holliday is antagonized by outlaw characters like Тень and Ямато.

- In Holliday's visual novel, Captain Murphy's receptionist believes Holliday wants to have an affair with Murphy, who is married. Пелена and Ямато tease Holliday about the rumor.6]

- Инфернус wants to go on a date with Holliday.10]

- Хлыст attempts to flirt with Holliday.6]

- Holliday mistakes Вязкус' home, The Deep, for the American Deep South. She asks if he's from Mobile, Alabama.6]

- Holliday tells Cadence to "get a real job".11]

- Holliday finds Ворон handsome.12]

- Holliday likes Серый Коготь due to their shared goal of revenge.6]

- Калико compares Holliday's introverted personality to Серый Коготь's.6]

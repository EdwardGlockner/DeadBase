Vampiric Burst is a Tier 4 Vitality Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400. Its component, Bullet Lifesteal, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

Abilities that deal weapon damage also provide heal from Bullet Lifesteal. Here are all of the relevant abilities.

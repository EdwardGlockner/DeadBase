Blackmore Academy is a school for the children of America's wealthiest families.

Blackmore Academy is an upper-class school, exclusively for wealthy or exceptionally talented students.6]7] Krill descibes Blackmore Academy as "the breeding ground for Manhattan's finest narcissists".8]

Graves and Apollo are each other's lab partners.9]

Blackmore Academy students seem to have the day off on the day of the ritual.10]

Blackmore students are implied to frequent Jezebel's, even though they legally can't drink alcohol.11] Infernus regularly has to kick them out of the bar,12] which once happened to Mina on her 16th birthday.13]14]

Some Blackmore students play cards at Wraith's.15]

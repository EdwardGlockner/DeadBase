Lightning Scroll is a Tier 4 元灵 Item that provides Bonus Health and Sprint Speed. The purchasing hero's Spirit Damage will also cause enemies hit to have their Movement Speed be reduced.

Lightning Scroll has a passive effect that causes any damage done by your ultimate to apply bonus Spirit Damage and a brief Stun after a short delay. This can only be triggered once per target per ultimate, and cannot be used with ultimates that have charges (such as 薇妲's 狙击.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400 (its Component, 秘术缓速, makes up 1,600 of the cost).

## Notes

- 口袋's ultimate does not trigger Lightning Scroll, but they can still buy it.

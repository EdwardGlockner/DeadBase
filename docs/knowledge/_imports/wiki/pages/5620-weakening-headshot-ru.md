Weakening Headshot is a Tier 2 Оружие Item that grants Bonus Health. Its passive causes headshots to reduce the Bullet Resist of Heroes hit.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

Weakening Headshot is a Component of Калечащий выстрел в голову.

The Hideout is a personal, interactive hub that functions as the main menu and social area in Deadlock. Within this space, players can freely move around, prepare for matches, and invite friends to join them. Various terminals located throughout the Hideout provide access to the game's core menus, including Play, Watch, and Learn.

For training, the Hideout includes a dedicated practice range called The Atrium, designed for testing hero movement and abilities without a loading screen. Players can access the more extensive Sandbox mode through a Teleporter found within this practice area, or through the Play menu.

To the left of the main hall is an open doorway leading to a two-part training area.

### The Curiosity Shop

The antechamber to the practice range features a fully-stocked counter for The Curiosity Shop. While players can access the item shop at any time with a dedicated keybind.

### The Atrium

The main practice area is a large, industrial-looking room with scaffolding and graffiti. It is designed for basic ability and damage testing.

- Bots: The range contains one friendly and one enemy bot. These bots are static and do not perform any actions, serving as simple targets.

- A large, red-buttoned machine allows players to toggle fast cooldowns and infinite stamina by meleeing it.

- When in a party, a crystal appears that allows players to swap teams.

- Teleporter to Sandbox: At the back of the range is a teleporter that allows players to instantly load into the full Sandbox mode for more advanced training options.

- Kicking the ball into the teleporter 3 times will spawn a rabbit. The rabbit has the same behavior as the cat. If the cat and the rabbit come into proximity of each other, and aren't already chasing one of the aforementioned objects, the cat will chase the rabbit around the room.

- Shooting the owl makes it disappear and spawns a new owl, as well as a second one, increasing the number of owls by one every time all owls are shot. It also shows a counter of how many owls were shot by the player in the current session.

## Customization

The Hideout is planned to be customizable with items unlocked by the player. The first two unlockables are:

- A snow globe above the fireplace for players who engaged in the Winter 2024 event challenges.

- A Merry Midtown Mayhem Souvenir for players who participated in the Winter 2025 event.

## Trivia

- On 20th January 2026, the Old Gods, New Blood update's heroes were teased with various objects scattered around the Hideout (such as Apollo's sword, Rem's pillow and Venator's thurible).

- The clock, located above the elevator in the hideout, displays the player's local system time while playing solo. However, upon joining a party, it switches to reflect the local time in New York City, New York (EST/EDT), the real life location of The Cursed Apple. The clock also flashes and dings every 60 seconds. On the hour, it also chimes to match the current hour.

Escalating Exposure is a Tier 4 元灵 Item that provides Spirit Resist to the purchasing hero, along with reducing the enemy Spirit Resist when the purchasing hero deals Spirit Damage. Additionally, it has a passive that applies a stacking Spirit Amp that increases your Spirit Damage to the target.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400, with its Component, 秘术脆弱, making up 1,600 of the cost.

## Notes

- Vindicta has 5 stacks of Escalating Exposure on an enemy Warden, and she uses Assassinate on him. The baseline Assassinate on its own is not enough damage to kill the Warden, but the increased damage (due to the 5 stacks) is enough to secure the kill. Since the damage amp from Escalating Exposure is applied after the Assassinate, it is technically the damage amp which secures the kill, not the Assassinate. Thus, Vindicta will not receive bonus souls from Assassinate's passive.

- This means regardless of your own stack count, your Geist's 100 damage bombs will only apply 108 damage (Mystic Vulnerability's 8%) and will not be affected by your stacks.

- This still applies even if the teammate also has escalating exposure.

- The user that most recently started applying stacks' icon will be the only one shown to the entire team.

- The user with stacks applied to them will only see the combined total of Escalating Exposure stacks on them, not each player's individual count.

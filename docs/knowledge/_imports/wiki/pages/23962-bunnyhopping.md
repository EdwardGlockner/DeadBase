Bunnyhopping is a movement technique used in video games where the player times jumps immediately upon landing to create a rhythmic hopping motion similar to a rabbit’s movement. The technique originated in early first-person shooters such as the Quake series.

In Deadlock, bunnyhopping exists in two forms:

- Slide Bunnyhopping

- Raw Bunnyhopping

Reference extract:

Bullet Damage is a stat that heroes start with and can obtain more through items.

A permanent Bullet Damage buff can be obtained from Golden Statues.

Values referenced from Hero Comparison Table.

Notes:

- The following table considers the value of individual bullets for burst fire and spreadshot weapons. See "Base Bullet Damage based on Boons" below for a comparison based on bullets per shot.

- Drifter's spreadshot weapon has the exception of always dealing the same amount of damage to a target, regardless of how many bullets hit per shot.

Plated Armor is a Tier 4 Живучесть Item that provides Bonus Health. Its passive provides a chance for a bullet fired at the user to deflect, avoiding all damage, and a chance to prevent on-hit effects from bullets.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

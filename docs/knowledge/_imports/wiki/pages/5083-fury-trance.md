Fury Trance is a Tier 3 Vitality Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200. Its component, Bullet Lifesteal, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

## Notable Interactions

- Fury Trance has strong synergy with Haze, as it can be used after casting Bullet Dance to increase her Fire Rate, and/or after isolating a target with Sleep Dagger.

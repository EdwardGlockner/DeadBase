Disarming Hex is a Tier 3 Спиритизм Item that provides Bonus Health and Sprint Speed. Its Active ability allows the user to Disarm an enemy hero and reduce their Bullet Resist for a short duration.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200, with its Component, Ржавый ствол, making up 800 of the cost.

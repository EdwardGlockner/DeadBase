The Bear Pit is an underground arena of blood sport in New York where spectators can bet on prize fights.

## Summary

Known fighters include Lash and Bebop, who are rivals. Lash calls himself a "champion" of the Bear Pit.1]

Lash has lost to Bebop in the Bear Pit once,2] while Bebop lost to Lash at least once afterwards.3]4]

Wraith and Krill are prominent gamblers in matches, who are both known to often bet money on Bebop.2]5] Once, Mendoza told Wraith that Lash was going to "take a dive" in a Bear Pit fight, which Lash proceeded to win, causing Wraith to lose "a lot of money".6]

Bebop is fighting in the Bear Pit to earn money for Ms. Shelly's medicine.7]8] He intends to retire once he completes the Ritual and gets his wish.5]

Celeste's friend Blockhead Lenny once fought in the Bear Pit, but lost to Lash.9]

Fights in the Bear Pit seem to allow the use of magic.10]

## Trivia

- In Neon Prime, Kali was a former champion of the pit who came out of retirement, only to lose to Bebop. Kali sustained injuries severe enough to send him into an almost 2-decade hiatus afterwards.11]

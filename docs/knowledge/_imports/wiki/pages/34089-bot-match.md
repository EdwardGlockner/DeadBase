Bot Match is a gamemode in Deadlock in which the player plays with and/or against AI-controlled bot heroes.

## Overview

Bot matches are available for both Standard mode and Street Brawl, in easy, medium, and hard difficulties, and in both online co-op and single player custom games. Bot heroes are chosen randomly based on Deadlock's team composition algorithm.

Some heroes don't have AI bot support, and thus don't spawn in bot matches (unless they are player-controlled).

Bots have passive Souls generation and can buy items anywhere on the map.

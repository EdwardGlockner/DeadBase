Unstable Rift is an Objective in Deadlock.

| Source | Soul Value | Soul Gain (/min) | Notes |

| --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Unstable Rift | 1482 | +222 | Starts scaling at minute 13. Split equally among the team |

When a team wins the rift:

- Rift Troopers will spawn in that lane for the winning team. They are larger, colored black, have +100% health and damage, 25% Melee Resistance, and 30/35/40/45% Spirit Resist compared to normal troopers, increasing by +20% (additively) for each consecutive rift, up to 160%. Rift Troopers don't drop souls when killed. They spawn at a minimum of 5 troopers，every 0.5 seconds..

- All souls are distributed instantly and equally to all team members.

If the rift has been contested for over 50s, a warning shows up. After 60s of being contested, all souls will be released as 13 Soul Orbs to be claimed by either team. The orbs float for several seconds, and if unclaimed, they disappear and the souls are lost.

### Comeback reward

If the trailing team wins the rift, they get a soul bonus that scales based on game time and the team's soul deficit. They also get up to +9 Rift Troopers (14 total).

Improved Spirit is a Tier 2 元灵 Item that provides Spirit Power and Out of Combat Regen to the purchasing hero.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600, with its Component, 灵力扩增, making up 800 of the cost.

Improved Spirit is a component of 灵力无边.

Majestic Leap is a Tier 3 Vitality Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

The minimum angle that you can leap at is 30°. When leaping, it will launch you at a peak of 1063 u/s upwards and outwards. You can choose to descend after a second in any direction and any angle under 45°.

During the duration of which you are airborne, you have 100% air control; this allows for better air strafing and turning. This state and the ability to quickly descend ends immediately upon riding a Zipline or landing.

When landing, your velocity will be reduced to ~500 u/s. It is possible to cancel your descent and this slowdown by dashing or jumping. This is called Majestic Leap Cancelling.

## Notable Interactions

- Majestic Leap has good synergy with heroes who can use abilities to float in the air, such as Pocket's Barrage (which also doesn't interrupt leap's velocity momentum) and Bebop's Hyper Beam.

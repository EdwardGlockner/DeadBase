Spirit Lifesteal is a Tier 2 Живучесть Item that provides Spirit lifesteal and Bonus Health to the purchasing hero.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

Spirit Lifesteal is a Component of Кровопийца and Насытитель.

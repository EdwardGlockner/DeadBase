## Bounce Pad

Bounce Pad is Holliday's second ability. In it, she places down a bounce pad that acts similarly to the air vents found throughout the map, which allies and enemies can use. Holliday's and her teammates first use of a bounce pad will give 100% air control and allow for an explosion upon landing, dealing damage. If Bounce Pad is maxed to T3, (only) Holliday will also apply 0.7s Stun to enemies in Stop Zone. Subsequent uses will not give this buff.

When spawned in, the bounce pad will appear 200 units in front of Holliday1] after 0.2 seconds. The bounce pad lasts for 22 seconds, after which it will despawn.

Holliday's Bounce Pad will send at an initial vertical velocity of 900 u/s2], and initial horizontal velocity of 750 when you have any horizontal movement3].

On the first bounce, Holliday gains 100% air control until she lands on the ground, which allows her to maneuver and strafe easier in the air. Holliday still experiences the standard air drag of 0.2, although strafing will significantly eliminate effective air drag.

When placed standing directly in front of a wall, it will place the bounce pad vertically. Holding no movement inputs while doing this will let you stand on top of the vertical bounce pad; this enables you to look into the wall and input back and jump at the same time to get an instant wall jump into bounce pad, generating lots of momentum.

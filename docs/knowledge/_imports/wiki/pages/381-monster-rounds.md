Monster Rounds is a Tier 1 Weapon Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

It is a component of Cultist Sacrifice.

## Notes

- Monster Rounds applies additional damage to Non-Player Characters. This includes Troopers, neutral entities like Denizens and Mid-Boss, as well as lane structures and team objectives such as Guardians, Walkers, Shrines, and the Patron.

- It applies additional damage against Graves' Ghouls and McGinnis' Mini Turrets.

- The bonus Bullet Resist has no effect against Walkers, Shrines, the Patron, Graves' Ghouls or McGinnis' Turrets, since they deal Spirit Damage.

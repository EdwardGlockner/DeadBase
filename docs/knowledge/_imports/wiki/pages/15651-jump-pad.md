Jump Pads, also known as Bounce Pads, Launch Pads or Air Vents, are structures on the Map that launch a player in the air in a specific direction, indicated by the wind above the pad.

## Overview

The landing location is usually indicated by a white graffiti on the ground. The player can air strafe by looking and moving to a different direction mid-jump.

Taking damage in the first 0.9s after using a Jump Pad will stun the player until 0.2s after landing, apply a 30% slow for 2s, and deal 15% Max Health damage.

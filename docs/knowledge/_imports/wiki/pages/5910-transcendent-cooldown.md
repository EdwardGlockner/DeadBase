Transcendent Cooldown is a Tier 4 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400. Its component, Superior Cooldown, makes up 3,200 of the cost.

## Notes

- When bought, Cooldown Reduction is applied retroactively to abilities already on cooldown.

- It reduces all item cooldowns, for both passive and active effects.

- It does not affect Charge-Up items, such as Tankbuster and Mercurial Magnum.

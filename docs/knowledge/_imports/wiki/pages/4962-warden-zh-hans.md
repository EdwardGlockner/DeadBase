## 背景故事

### Lore

Warden hides his true motives from the Patrons, despite their telepathic abilities.1]2] As such, the Patrons probably don't know that Warden is working against their goals.

A concept sketch for Warden's upcoming redesign can be seen in one of Yamato's concept artwork released by Yoshi.3] The Shopkeeper may ask Warden if he would be interested in selling his cape, despite Warden's current model not having one.4] The Newscaster voice line for Warden's Winter skin challenge also makes a reference to his upcoming redesign, asking "What will he look like next?"5]

## 角色互动

- Despite being driven by a single-minded goal of protecting humanity from supernatural forces, Warden is sympathetic to some supernatural beings like 青藤 and 薇妲.6]7]

- Warden says he looked up to 灰爪 and apologizes for killing him.8]9]

- 亚伯兰 is scared by Warden's cape (in his upcoming redesign).10]

- 哈雷黛 says that Warden looks like he's wearing a Halloween costume and calls him a "creepy homeschooled kid".11]

- 劳什 refers to Warden as "creepy" and "vomitus".12]13]

## 角色趣闻

- Warden was named Murphy in Neon Prime. This name was reused for the new character of Captain Murphy in Holliday's visual novel.

- Warden's current model uses a modified version of Lash's head model. This is referenced in a line where Warden asks "Do I look like Lash?"

- Warden is currently one of two heroes whose Bullets per sec is directly affected by Spirit Power, the other being Vindicta.

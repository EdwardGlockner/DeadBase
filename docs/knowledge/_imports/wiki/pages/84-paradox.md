Paradox is a Hero in Deadlock. She wields time manipulation with potent disruption and slowing abilities. With a charged Kinetic Carbine, she can threaten her enemies at any range while damaging and slowing them with thrown Pulse Grenades and well-placed Time Walls. At just the right moment, Paradox can activate her Paradoxical Swap to change places with her target, dropping them into a dangerous situation amongst her team.

Paradox is both the name of this Hero and the name of their Thieves' Guild, specializing in stealing the untouchable and breaking into ultra-secure facilities. Their motives are unclear, except for a certain level of delight that its members seem to take in snubbing the world's biggest corporations and governments.

- Deals 50% damage to objectives.

- "Time slow" here means Movement Slow. Dashes are not affected.

- The grenade starts pulsing if it hits an enemy mid-flight: heroes, troopers, objectives, or denizens. In that case it doesn't stick to the target but stays in the air where it made contact. It passes through allied units without hitting them.

- There is no stacks limit and no cooldown between stacks. Casting grenade twice with Echo Shard allows double stacking.

- The charge-up happens over 2.5s.

- Like the damage, the duration of Time-Stop for the target also scales with charge time, with minimum being 0.25s.

- Carbine's damage scaling is based on Weapon Damage of a full burst shot (5 * bullet damage = 34).

- Casting Kinetic Carbine does not break stealth. Shooting Carbine's shot does break it.

- Despite saying "Move Speed is increased while charging", the bonus is only given after Carbine is fully charged.

- It works as a flat multiplicative modifier on top of all other move speed buffs and debuffs and is not part of common stacking algorithm.

- Heavy melee distance is increased.

- Dash distance seems to be unaffected.

- Reload animation gets completed quicker, but it is only a visual/audial indicator and the actual reload time is unaffected.

- Carbine shot is immune to Bullet Evasion.

- Carbine is affected by Disarm.

- Shooting objectives with Carbine displays "Too far" even in close range. This is a visual bug, the damage still happens.

- Carbine has complicated interactions with many items, see more in Kinetic Carbine Interactions.

### Paradoxical Swap

PARADOXICAL SWAP

x1.1

150

0%

x0

- Paradoxical Swap does not interrupt channeled casts like Dynamo's Singularity.

- The position swap lasts ~1.25s. Neither Paradox nor her target(s) are able to perform any actions during this time.

- Once connected, the swap cannot be interrupted and can go through walls.

- Both Paradox and her target(s) are vulnerable to damage during the swap.

- Swap damage is applied after the swap ends. T1 barrier is activated when the swap begins.

- The T3 upgrade also unlocks an Alternate Cast that lets the player swap a single target.

- The projectile continues even if it gets cleansed and will still swap when it hits another enemy along its path.

- The original target of Paradoxical Swap doesn't move.

- Any other nearby targets affected by the swap area of effect will be swapped.

- Paradox will move to the original target's position.

## Kinetic Carbine Item Interactions

Due to unique nature of the ability (charging a weapon damage into a single shot of spirit damage) Kinetic Carbine has many unique interactions.

### Weapon/Spirit Items

Weapon Damage items provide their bonus to Carbine.

Conditional Weapon Damage items apply their bonuses to Carbine if the condition is satisfied at the moment of the shot. For example, having Point Blank will make Carbine use its damage bonus if the shot target is in close range.

Spirit Power items do not affect Carbine in anyway since it scales only off weapon damage.

### Resist Reduction Items

Bullet resist reduction items do not affect Carbine's damage.

Spirit resist reduction items affect Carbine's damage.

### On-shot Items

- Restorative Shot.

- Mystic Shot.

- Weakening Headshot and Crippling Headshot. Crippling Headshot's Spirit Resist reduction applies after the damage.

- Hollow Point's bullet resist debuff.

- Headshot Booster and Headhunter.

- Spirit Shredder Bullets and Spirit Rend.

- Point Blank slow.

- Tesla Bullets and Capacitor.

- Armor Piercing Rounds' pierce.

- Lucky Shot's extra damage.

- Ricochet's ricochet.

- Siphon Bullets' health steal.

- Buildup items like Toxic Bullets.

- Split Shot allows Paradox to fire 5 Carbine shots at once. However, it will only affect the first enemy hit.

- Express Shot gives the smaller "Secondary Fire Weapon Damage" bonus to Carbine shot and immediately shoots a regular bullet with the bigger "Weapon Damage" bonus.

### On-spirit-damage items

Any items that trigger on spirit damage work with Carbine. For example, a single shot of carbine can trigger Tankbuster, Spirit Burn and Mystic Slow.

### Other interactions

Kinetic Carbine applies a unique multiplicative modifier for its move speed bonus that has several interactions with items that move the player:

- Majestic Leap performs the leap quicker.

- Magic Carpet gets increase to its flying speed.

- Ethereal Shift gets increase to its "floating" speed.

Since casting Carbine does not break stealth it creates a unique interaction with items that give Stealth:

- Shadow Weave

- Veil Walker

## Trivia

- Paradox was named Chrono in Neon Prime.

- Paradox is voiced by Elle Newlands, who also voiced Horizon in Apex Legends. Both characters are Scottish and use technology of spacetime manipulation.

- Paradox wears a blue rose in her lapel, which do not exist in nature, and are only possible through genetic modification. In art, blue roses are a symbol for mystery and the unattainable.

- "Scheming in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Paradox in the Hideout.

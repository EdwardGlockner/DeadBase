The denizens of the Deep know Fathom's true name.1]

## Character Interactions

- 卡厉可 says the Baxter Society would eventually come after Fathom.3]

## Trivia

- "Slork" has been adopted as a nickname for Fathom by some players in the Deadlock community.4]5] This is alluded to in-game in the voice line where Fathom mentions his "true name".1] The dev Kaori has also acknowledged "Slork" on Discord,6]7] and adopted a profile picture with the caption "Team Slork".8]

- Fathom's weapon used to be called Poison Sprayer.

- Fathom is the only hero who has an idle animation in the hero selection screen.

- Fathom has a mention of Hudson Bay.9] However, judging from the context, it was likely intended to be the Hudson River.

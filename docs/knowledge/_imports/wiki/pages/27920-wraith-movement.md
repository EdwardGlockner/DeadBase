## Telezip

Project Mind is Wraith’s second ability. Project Mind allows her to teleport to a location within a radius of 25m, which can be upgraded using one Ability Point to 40m. Momentum from the initial teleporting point is not carried over; once teleported, you drop straight down.

Telezip allows you to buffer riding the Zipline. This can allow you to make a better and more efficient escape.

Telezip also allows you to move quicker after a teleport. Project Mind normally cancels all momentum at the exit point, but by riding the transit line, you can immediately drop off and utilise the air drag buff. This can be used to escape or initiate a fight.

To execute, use Project Mind near a transit line, and while teleporting towards it, hold your Jump key (by default, SPACE) to instantly ride when actionable.

Pure Damage is a damage type from Abilities and Items that isn't considered Spirit, Weapon, or Melee Damage, and thus cannot be mitigated by Damage Resistance or Barriers.

## List of Pure Damage Items

| Name | Cost | Category | Notes |

| --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Blood Tribute | 3,200 | Weapon | Self Damage part |

| Siphon Bullets | 6,400 | Vitality |  |

| Focus Lens | 6,400 | Spirit | Post-mitigation damage amp |

- Armor Piercing Rounds and Tankbuster don't deal Pure Damage (white numbers), but their damage bypasses Weapon and Spirit Resistances, respectively.

## Notes

- Pure Damage does not trigger Mystic Reverb. If an ability has bonus Pure Damage like the blessed stakes of Venator's Ira Domini, it will not be used to calculate the damage of the reverb.

- Their behavior was similar, bypassing resistances, however some interactions were different, for example Lady Geist's Soul Exchange worked with Mystic Reverb.

The Baxter Society is a well-known international monster hunting organization.

Instead of hunting all magical creatures, the Baxter Society specifically hunts monsters that prey on humanity.1] This can extend to hunting humans, as Grey Talon mentions that the society likely would have hunted Lady Geist if they knew about her deal with Oathkeeper.2] It's also mentioned that, while they currently aren't hunting him, the Baxter Society would likely hunt down Seven.3] The Baxter Society have been hunting Drifter for years, but have been unsuccessful at killing him.4]5]6]

The members of the Baxter Society don't get paid to hunt monsters. The lack of payment was why Calico left the society to pursue the well-paid life of an assassin.7] It's implied that Calico left the society before Shiv joined.8]

Grey Talon, while retired, is still heavily respected by the Baxter Society.9] His dedication to the society resulted in books being made about his life and earning him a reputation as a "legend".10]11] His dedication to his work, however, meant he was often away from home, which meant he missed watching his son grow up.12] Refusing to make the same mistake for his grandkids, Grey Talon retired after roughly 40 years of service.13] When he retired, Grey Talon was said to have more confirmed kills than anyone else in the Baxter Society.14]

Shiv was chosen by Grey Talon as his replacement.15]16] Due to his criminal background, the other members of the Baxter Society don't trust or respect Shiv.17]18] Shiv worries that the Baxter Society would eventually turn on him.19] Shiv has been sent to the ritual on behalf of the Baxter Society,20]21] and hopes that he can prove his worth to them and Grey Talon by hunting the Patrons.22]23]

Holliday is disappointed that the Baxter Society isn't hunting the Troubadour. Shiv says the Society is dealing with their own problems, which could potentially be more dangerous than the Troubadour.24] He also invites Holliday to join the Society if she manages to kill the Troubadour.25]

The Baxter Society owns a manor house, which seems to be their main base of operations. Once, a member of the Paradox Organisation tried to break into the manor house, but was caught. Paradox wants Grey Talon to get her into the manor house.26]

At some point, the Society had to intervene at the Baroness Hotel, which The Doorman was grateful for.27]

The Baxter Society have a friendly rivalry with the Venators of St. Benedict.28]

According to Grey Talon, members of the society who are killed in action are often found skinless in a retention pond in Portugal or Denmark.29]

Billy believes the Baxter Society never hunted any real monsters.30]

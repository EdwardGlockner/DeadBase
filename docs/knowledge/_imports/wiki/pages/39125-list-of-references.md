List of references to other media in Deadlock, and references to Deadlock in other media.

- Bebop's Grapple Arm ability being depicted as a Hook is a reference to Dota 2, where hero Pudge has a functionally identical ability called Meat Hook.

- Warden's backstory shares themes with Arc Warden from Dota 2, who similarly wants to stop the conflict between the Ancients at any cost.

### Other media

- Shiv carries an amulet on his hip with a design similar to the blood key from the movie Demon Knight.1] The amulet glows red when Shiv is at full Rage.

- The Teleporter's appearance is similar to the TARDIS from Doctor Who, which is a time machine disguised as a 1960s British police phone box.

- Seven's character select line "With these hands I will destroy, and with these hands I will rebuild" is a quote from the song The Good Doctor by The Protomen.2]

## References to Deadlock in other media

### Media made by Valve Corporation

- Team Fortress 2 community map Freaky Fair, released in the 2024 Scream Fortress update, has a parody of the Curiosity Shop called the "Upgrade Station", with Merasmus as the Shopkeeper.

- Counter-Strike 2 community map Warden features a graffiti of Warden.3]

- Counter-Strike 2 community map Dogtown has arcade cabinets and plushies referencing Deadlock and other Valve games.

### Other media

- Skullgirls Mobile features a variant of the character Black Dahlia called "Locked 'N' Loaded", with a color palette and playstyle based on Lady Geist and using one of Geist's select quotes as a caption.

- In Slay the Spire 2, the boss Doormaker was inspired by The Doorman.4]

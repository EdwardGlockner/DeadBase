Bullet Evasion is a stat that prevents a percentage of incoming bullets from hitting the target.

## Evasion Immunity

Bullets with the following effects cannot be prevented by Evasion.

- Парадокс's Кинетический карабин

- Мистический выстрел

- Удачный выстрел

## Sources of Bullet Evasion

- Пелена's Танец пуль T3 gives her 60% Bullet Evasion for the duration of the ability.

- Мираж's Tornado gives him 25% Bullet Evasion for 3.5s after emerging from the Tornado, with an added 20% Evasion at T3.

- Латная броня gives 30%% Bullet Evasion and 50%% prevention of bullet effects on hit.

All characters have the ability to Sprint. Sprinting in Deadlock is a passive action — no further action is required to sprint. When sprinting, you instantly accelerate up to +2.5 m/s of your base sprint speed1], then gain 0.6 m/s every second2] above that threshold. Sprinting characters can be identified by the grey speed lines coming off them.

Every character has a base sprint speed of 1.6 m/s except:

- Bebop – 4 m/s

- Kelvin – 1.1 m/s

- Lady Geist – 2.4 m/s

- Lash – 2.1 m/s

- Paige – 3.5 m/s

- Victor – 1.1 m/s

When damaged by an enemy hero or non-hero, sprint will be disabled for 3 seconds.3]

You cannot sprint while firing or zooming with alt-fire. Upon firing, the character will slow to around ~70% of their base walk speed until the character stops firing.

- With the Weapon Item Fleetfoot, they will move at their base walk speed if they are firing.

- Ivy’s Kudzu Connection, provides Fleetfoot's passive to both her and the tethered teammate.

Jumping is a fundamental part of movement in Deadlock. Jumping allows you to reposition to higher places. In Deadlock, you can jump on the ground and in the air. A jump in the air is called an Air Jump or a Double Jump.

Jumping on the ground does not use stamina. Ground jumping sends you at a peak speed of 295 u/s and at a max height of +45 units. Jumping in the air, or Air Jumping, costs one bar of stamina. For every hero, at the start of each game you are only permitted one air jump. This can be expanded to two with the Vitality Item Stamina Mastery ( 3,200).

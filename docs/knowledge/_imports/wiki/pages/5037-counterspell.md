Counterspell is a Tier 3 Vitality Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

- Counterspell can prevent Lash's Death Slam if the player parries at the moment they would be pulled.

- Counterspell can be used on The Doorman's Hotel Guest.

- Counterspell can be used on Celeste's Shining Wonder to avoid the initial damage. However, the orb will continue to linger on the player and can bounce onto other heroes, and back.

- Counterspell can prevent Build-Up effects such as Mina's Love Bites and stacking effects such as Haze's Fixation, if activated either before the build-up is applied or when it triggers. If build-up/stacks have already been applied, it does not prevent the build-up itself.

- Counterspell can prevent Mirage's Djinn's Mark before it's applied to the player. However, it does not prevent it from stacking or triggering once it has been applied.

- Counterspell can block instant abilities such as Mo & Krill's Combo if the enemy tries to use the ability during the target's parry window, similarly to Unstoppable.

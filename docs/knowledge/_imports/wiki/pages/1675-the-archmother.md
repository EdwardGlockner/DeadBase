— Old Gods, New Blood Update

The Archmother is a Patron, as well as the name of her Team. Her followers are The Archmother's Disciples. The Hidden King is her rival.

The Archmother's herald is voiced by Debra Wilson.1]

The Doorman refers to the Archmother as City Mother.8] The bank behind Archmother's Yellow Walker is called "City Mother Bank".

The Hidden King's herald calls the Archmother a "tyrant".9]

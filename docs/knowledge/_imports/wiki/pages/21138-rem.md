Rem is a Hero in Deadlock. An accidental stowaway from the world of dreams, Rem has found himself stranded in the city that never sleeps. Pillow fights are Rem's preferred form of conflict, tossing his favorite pillow at enemies to knock them back when they're getting too close. His friends, however, he likes to keep nearby - leaping to their aid when things get dicey and taking a quick nap on their shoulder. His Lil Helpers are curious creatures, taking interest in everything the streets of New York have to offer, and cheering on his allies as they go. Ultimately when Naptime hits, no one will be able to keep their eyes open.

A sleepy child from a world of dreams, Rem and his little helpers found themselves stranded in New York. Searching the five boroughs for a way home, Rem seeks to summon the Patrons so he can finally return to his family.

Rem was released on January 26, 2026 as the first winner of the Old Gods, New Blood update contest.

Long Night has a subtle "homing" effect, in which the projectiles bend towards the enemy when close to Rem's crosshair. This is more noticeable when landing headshots, and is offset by Rem's outgoing headshot reduction.

## Trivia

- Rem has a secret room in the Hideout that can be accessed by entering the vents behind the news computer or the large statue in the Atrium, then climbing over the boiler machine in the backroom.

- Rem was originally leaked in November 2025 with a screenshot of the character's select screen.1] On 20th January 2026, Rem was teased in the Hideout with a pillow in a chair.

- Rem has unused "sleepy" voicelines for every other hero in the game, presumably meant for the clone ability.

- Rem's name references Rapid Eye Movement, a unique and active phase of sleep in which most dreaming occurs.

- Rem's hybrid anatomy of bird and mammal resembles that of a wingless Griffin or Keythong in ancient mythology.

- Rem has a line where he dreams about mangos,2] which are the favorite fruit of developer Icefrog and an item in Dota 2.3]

- "Having Sweet Dreams in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Rem in the Hideout.

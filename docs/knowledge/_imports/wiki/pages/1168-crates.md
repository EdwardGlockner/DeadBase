Crates (or boxes) are breakable objects scattered around the map that, when broken, have a chance to drop Unsecured Souls. Crates can be broken by shooting, punching, using certain abilities, dashing, or sliding through them.

Crates have the Fairfax Industries logo.

- There are 518 crates on the map

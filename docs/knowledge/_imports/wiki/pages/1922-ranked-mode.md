For the current rank system, see Skill Rating.

Ranked mode was a game mode in Deadlock for competitive players to compete with other players of similar skill levels. By winning matches and performing well, players could earn badges for their profile depending on the highest rank they achieve. Medals were updated once per week, requiring at least 7 games played within that week to receive a rank.

Ranked mode was added to the game on October 10, 2024, and was removed on November 21, 2024. It was replaced by the Skill Rating system.

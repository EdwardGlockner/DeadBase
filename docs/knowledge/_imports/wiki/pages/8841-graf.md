Information

Graf is an unreleased hero in Deadlock.

In the game files, there's a reference to a model called "graffiti_girl" under Graf's name, indicating that she could be a graffiti artist.

- Graf has the lowest health in the game at 480hp with no boons (100hp less than Mina), and 1472 with max boons (8hp less than Mina).

- Besides "graffiti", the name "Graf" could also mean an earl/count of German nobility, or a slang for "paragraph".

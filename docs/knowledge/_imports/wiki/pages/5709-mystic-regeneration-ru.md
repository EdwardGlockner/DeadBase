Mystic Regeneration is a Tier 1 Спиритизм Item. It has a passive effect that grants the player bonus Health Regeneration when they deal Spirit Damage to enemy heroes.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

It is a component of Сияющее восстановление.

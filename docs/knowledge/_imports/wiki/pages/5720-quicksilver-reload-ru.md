Quicksilver Reload is a Tier 2 Спиритизм Item. Its passive component Imbues the hero's ability with bonus Spirit Damage when hitting an enemy. Additionally, when the ability is used, the hero's weapon is instantly reloaded and gains bonus Fire Rate.

Quicksilver Reload is a Component of Ртутный Магнум.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

- Инфернус with multiple Catalyst charges

- Пелена who benefits from maintaining Fixation stacks on targeted enemies

- Виндикта Ворон-фамильяр has a short cooldown after second upgrade

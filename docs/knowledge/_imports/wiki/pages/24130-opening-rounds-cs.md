Opening Rounds is a Tier 2 Zbraň Item that grants increased Spirit Power. Its passive causes the user's attacks to deal bonus Weapon Damage to enemies who are above 50% Max Health.

Opening Rounds is a Tier 2 Zbraň Předmět that can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600. Its component, Vysokorychlostní zásobník, makes up 800 of the cost.

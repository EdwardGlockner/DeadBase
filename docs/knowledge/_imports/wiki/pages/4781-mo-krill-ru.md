Mo & Krill are Heroes in Deadlock. Fighting together, they are a disruptive and mobile pair that can burrow around the battlefield and leave their enemies in ruins. Beginning encounters with Burrow can let Mo & Krill pop out on unsuspecting enemies, attacking them with Scorn to damage and Sand Blast to disarm them. With the support of their team, they can Combo one targeted enemy to pin their target in place while the rest of their allies unleash their attacks.

Members of The Tunnel Rats, Mo & Krill are smugglers and illicit traders who make the underground of New York their home. Rival gangs that try to exploit these tunnel systems for their own gain will rapidly discover how protective and deadly this pair of smugglers can be.

## Background

### Lore

Mo is a mole man. Krill's species is currently unknown, but he has monkey-like characteristics. McGinnis refers to Krill as a rat.1]

Mo's full name is Maurice. Krill also calls him Momo.

Mo and Krill have been forced to live in the margins of society.2] They don't pay taxes.3] They want to achieve the respect and glory they believe is owed to them.4]

Mo practices pottery, and Krill is writing a memoir.5] Mo wants to audit pottery classes at Columbia University.6]

Mo & Krill don't like the Friends of Humanity.7]

Mendoza was a client of Mo & Krill.8]

## Character interactions

- Mo & Krill protected Карман and they regard each other as friends.9]10]11]

- Mo & Krill are in Тень's weekly poker game and owe money to her. If they're on the same team, Wraith may say she called a favor to have them fighting on her side. They regard each other as friends.12]13]

- After killing Mo & Krill, Ямато may say she wants to turn them into a coat and a hat, respectively.14]

- Mo & Krill watch Бибоп's fights in the Bear Pit. They know Miss Shelly and have tried to help her.12]

- Krill has read Кельвин's books and found them "riveting".12]

- Семь hides in Mo & Krill's subway tunnels, without their permission.12]

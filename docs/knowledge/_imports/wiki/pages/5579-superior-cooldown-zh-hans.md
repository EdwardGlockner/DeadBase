Superior Cooldown is a Tier 3 Spirit Item that provides bonus Health Regeneration and reduces the cooldown of all abilities of the purchasing hero.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200 (its Component, 冷却压缩, makes up 1,600 of the cost).

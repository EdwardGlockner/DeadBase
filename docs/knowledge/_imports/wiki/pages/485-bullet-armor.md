Bullet Armor was a Tier 2 Vitality Item that provided Bullet Resist and Weapon Damage to the purchasing hero.

Bullet Armor was a component of Improved Bullet Armor before it was removed. Improved Bullet Armor was renamed to Bullet Resilience.

It was able to be purchased for 1,250 from the Shop.

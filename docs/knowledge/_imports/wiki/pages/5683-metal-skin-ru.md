Metal Skin is a Tier 3 Живучесть Item that provides Bullet Resist to the purchasing hero.

Its active ability grants the user immunity to bullet damage whilst slowing their movement speed and shortening their dash distance for a short duration. Attacks can still hit the user and proc effects such as Замедляющие пули.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

## Notable Interactions

- Metal Skin prevents the bullet damage from Пелена's Танец пуль, but the user will still take the added spirit damage from Пристальный взгляд stacks.

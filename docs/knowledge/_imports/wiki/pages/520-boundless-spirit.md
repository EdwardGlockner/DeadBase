Boundless Spirit is a Tier 4 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400. Its component, Improved Spirit, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

## Notes

- It's applied to all sources of Spirit Power granted to the player at all times, including temporary sources.

- It stacks multiplicatively with abilities that also grant bonus % Spirit Power, such as Rem's Tag Along (T3) and Kelvin's Ice Path (T3).

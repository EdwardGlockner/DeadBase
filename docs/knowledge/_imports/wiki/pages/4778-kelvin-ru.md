Kelvin is a Hero in Deadlock. He is a dedicated, undead scientist with a bevy of freezing abilities to slow his enemies and heal allies. He can conjure an Ice Path under his feet for faster movement for himself and his team, throwing Frost Grenades and firing his Arctic Beam at nearby enemies to set them up for his companions' attacks. Tactical usage of his Frozen Shelter ice dome can protect and heal allies while trapping hapless opponents.

As an adventuring scientist, Kelvin was particularly excited for rumours of an 8th Astral Gate opening in the Arctic, developing an expedition to hunt for it. However, something went wrong with the expedition, vanishing into the snow and leaving Kelvin's frozen, animate body to be discovered over a year later.

## Background

### Lore

Kelvin exhibits a boastful personality.1]2]

Kelvin has done an expedition to Ixia.3]

Kelvin writes books about his adventures.4]5]

Kelvin likes to drink single malt Scotch.6]7]

## Character interactions

- Other heroes such as Макгиннис and Динамо will sometimes call him "Kevin" instead of Kelvin.8]9]10]

- A likely possibility is that Seven is Kelvin's old friend Edrick, who "vanished". Леди Гайст says Edrick "caused a scene" at one of Kelvin's fundraisers.17] This is supported by one of Seven's lines to Kelvin that mirrors one of Kelvin's lines about Edrick.18]19]

- Семь believes Kelvin has a bigger ego than Хлыст.20]

- Леди Гайст funded Kelvin's Arctic expedition.21]

- Динамо, Ms. Shelly, Мо и Мелкий and Плющик's brother have read stories about Kelvin's adventures.4]5]

- Kelvin admires Мо и Мелкий's friendship.5]

- Kelvin is one of Парадокс's targets.5]

## Trivia

- Kelvin is the base unit for temperature of the International System of Units and the Kelvin scale is an absolute temperature scale that starts at the lowest possible temperature, 0 K, which is equal to -459.6° Fahrenheit or -273.1° Celsius.

- Kelvin narrated an old version of the tutorial.22]

- Kelvin may reference a shadowline when upgrading his Ice Path.23]

- Kelvin's suit in Neon Prime was used as the base for Viscous' current model.

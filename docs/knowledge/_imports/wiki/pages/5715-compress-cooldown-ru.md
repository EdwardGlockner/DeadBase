Compress Cooldown is a Tier 2 Spirit Item which gives Cooldown Reduction to an Imbued ability of the purchasing hero. It was previously named Improved Cooldown.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

Compress Cooldown is a component of Превосходная перезарядка умений.

## Notes

- If Compress Cooldown is imbued on Синклер's Участие аудитории, it does not reduce the cooldown of copied ultimates, only the default cooldown of the ability.

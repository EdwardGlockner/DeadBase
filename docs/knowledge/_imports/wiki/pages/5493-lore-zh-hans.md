Lore is the story of the world of Deadlock and its characters.

The Lore of Deadlock is pieced together from various sources: Character backgrounds, voice lines, the environment, and the visual novels.

Main article: The Library

Visual novels are planned to be the main narrative medium to expand the world of Deadlock. The first one, The Binding of Oathkeeper, was leaked in an early build. There are at least three other novels planned starring Ivy, Holliday and Infernus, and featuring various other heroes as side characters.

Split Shot is a Tier 2 Weapon Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

- Split Shot affects Grey Talon's Rain of Arrows, allowing him to fire 25 arrows at once. It doesn't affect Charged Shot.

- Split Shot affects Paradox's Kinetic Carbine, allowing her to fire 5 Kinetic Carbine shots at once. However, it will only affect the first enemy hit.

- The Doorman and Paige can gain stacks by hitting multiple players with the same shot. It also removes the 50% post-pierce damage reduction for Doorman's bullets.

- Split Shot has no effect on Silver (Transformed).

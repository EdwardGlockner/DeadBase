Slowing Hex is a Tier 2 Spirit Item.

Slowing Hex has an Active ability which can be used against a target enemy, slowing the target's movement and dashes and Silencing the target's movement-based items or abilities.

Slowing Hex is a component of 雷织漩涡.

It can be purchased at the Shop for 1,600.

## Notes

- 蝰邪's 蛇影疾行 appears to go on cooldown when affected by Slowing Hex, however it does not seem to disable any of its effects.

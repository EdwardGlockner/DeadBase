Stamina is a stat that heroes start with and can obtain more through items. Stamina dictates the amount of dashes a hero can use. Stamina regenerates back over time, taking 5 seconds for most heroes without items to regenerate 1 bar.

## Dash Speed

Dash Speed is a stat that determines how fast a hero performs a dash. These are also commonly referred to as Dash Buckets, in which the fastest Dash heroes are in "bucket 1" and the slowest ones are in "bucket 3".

All dashes cover the same distance (10m for ground dashes, 8m for air dashes), but take different durations. Ground dashes are slower than air dashes, but cover more distance. The "Dash Speed" stat uses the ground dash speed.

### Stamina Disable

- Capacitor disables Stamina of its target for 3s.

- Fury Trance disables Stamina of its user for 6.5s.

- Entangling Bola disables Stamina of its target for 1.5s, with +0.75s at tier 3.

### Map spawns

- Powerup runes can provide a temporary Movement buff that provides from +2 to +4 Stamina, +20% to +50% Stamina Regeneration Rate, and +40% to +80% Zipline Speed (the buffs grow stronger as the game progresses) for 160 seconds.

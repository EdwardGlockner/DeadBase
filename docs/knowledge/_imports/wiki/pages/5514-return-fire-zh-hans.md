Return Fire is a Tier 2 活力 Item that provides Bullet Resist to the purchasing hero.

Its active ability automatically fires a bullet towards any attacker who damages you for a short duration.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

## Notes

- Example: If an enemy deals 100 Bullet Damage while you have 50% Bullet Resist, you reflect 60 damage but only take 50

- Works even with 100% damage immunity (e.g., 铜皮铁骨 reflects full damage while preventing damage to the user)

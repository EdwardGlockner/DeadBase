Spirit Rend is a Tier 3 Оружие Item that grants Bonus Health, while also carrying over the Spirit Resist debuff and Spirit Lifesteal against enemies granted by Душегубные пули. It was previously named Spellslinger Headshots.

Additionally, headshots landed on enemy heroes apply up to 4 stacks of a Spirit Resist debuff on top of the previous debuff. All stacks are refreshed with each new stack.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200. Its Component, Душегубные пули, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

## Notes

- Spirit Rend's Spirit Resist and Spirit Lifesteal debuff can only be activated through bullet damage.

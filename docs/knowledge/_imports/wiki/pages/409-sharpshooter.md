Sharpshooter is a Tier 3 Weapon Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200. Purchasing one of its components, Long Range or High-Velocity Rounds, discounts the cost by 1,600 or 800 respectively. Purchasing both components discounts the cost by 2400.

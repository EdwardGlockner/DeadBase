The Curiosity Shop is the place players go to purchase various items from the Shopkeeper.

### Active Items

When buying an Active Item, players are prompted to place it into one of 4 ability slots in addition to their normal item slots. Players can only have a maximum of 4 Active Items in their inventory at a time, even if they have open item slots available to purchase more. Once a player has 4 Active Items, no more Active Items can be purchased until an Active Item is sold.

### Shop Bonus

When buying items, the player gains bonus stats related to the item's category: Weapon Damage for Weapon, Health for Vitality, and Spirit Power for Spirit.

## Shop Locations

### Base Shop

The base shop can be found at spawn. Unlike other shops, the buying radius extends to the whole area of the spawn room. Players can only buy items at their own base's shop.

### Side Shop

By every lane there is a side shop available, allowing quick purchases without leaving the lane. However, if the lane's Guardian is destroyed, the side shop closes and will not sell any items for the rest of the match. Players can only buy items at their own team's side shops; with the exception of Rem, who can send his Lil Helpers to purchase queued items from any shop on the map (with the exception of either team's Base Shop).

### Secret Shop

Both sides have their own secret shop, which can be found in an underground tunnel accessible by stairs or an open manhole. They are located midway between Broadway Street and the outside lanes. From the player's perspective, the secret shop on the right side of the map (Green for Hidden King and Yellow for Archmother) is in their teams' side. Both teams can access and make purchases from either secret shop.

## Lore

The Curiosity Shop's item catalogs are provided by Fairfax Industries, Mystic Pharmaceutical Stores, and the Curiosity Catalog.

## Trivia

- The side shops had wheels and tracks leading to each team's base, indicating that at one point in development they were supposed to move along the lanes. This was changed after the Map Rework update.

- Before the October 2, 2025 update, Secret Shops temporarily closed when players from both teams were nearby.

- The shape of the shop's minimap icon is more reminiscient of its Neon Prime design.

- The Team Fortress 2 community map Freaky Fair, released in the 2024 Scream Fortress update, has a parody of the Curiosity Shop called the "Upgrade Station", with Merasmus as the Shopkeeper.

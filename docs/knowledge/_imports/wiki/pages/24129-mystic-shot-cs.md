Mystic Shot is a Tier 2 Zbraň Item that grants increased Spirit Power. It states that its its passive effect causes the next bullet fired to deal additional Spirit Damage. The actual effect of the item sometimes creates an additional bullet instead that mimics the current Hero's weapon but deals Spirit Damage.

Mystic Shot is a Tier 2 Zbraň Předmět that can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

## Mechanics

The item provides a passive effect that creates an additional bullet when the hero shoots their weapon, and the additional bullet will mimic the bullets created by the current weapon. The Mystic Shot bullet appears as a glowing purple version of the hero's regular bullets.

If the hero uses an alternate-fire such as Viscous Deep Sea Interpreter's alternate fire, the Mystic bullet will appear the same but cannot deal damage in an area. Instead, it only deals the Spirit damage to the closest enemy to impact.

If the hero fires multiple bullets at once such as Abrams shooting Case Closed, the Mystic bullet fires directly towards the cursor without spread. On the other hand if the weapon shooting has inherent spread such as McGinnis continuously shooting Services Rendered then the Mystic bullet will follow the trajectory of another bullet and not always directly towards the cursor.

The additional Mystic bullet cannot deal critical (headshot) damage and does not get applied by Ricochet. Because the Mystic bullet hits before regular bullets, Crippling Headshot's Spirit Resist reduction is applied after Mystic Shot's damage even if the bullet is a headshot.

A Mystic bullet cannot miss due to evasion.

## Notes

- The additional spirit damage occurs before the bullet damage. This is relevant for items such as Drtič balistické zbroje, since the spirit damage will activate the negative bullet resist effect, amplifying the bullet damage.

- The bonus spirit damage is able to activate the Mystická dávka. However, this requires some additional spirit, since the base mystic shot damage is below the threshold to activate mystic burst. Similarly, mystic shot is able to activate the effect of Mystická bomba, but requires significantly more spirit to get to the threshold spirit damage for activation.

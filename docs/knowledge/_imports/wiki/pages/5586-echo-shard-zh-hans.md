Echo Shard is a Tier 4 元灵 Item that grants Cooldown Reduction, Fire Rate, Spirit Resist and Bullet Resist.

Echo Shard has an Active which can be used to reset the cooldown of the imbued ability.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

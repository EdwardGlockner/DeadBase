Scourge is a Tier 4 元灵 Item that provides Bonus Health and Cooldown Reduction.

Its Active ability gives a friendly target (can be self-cast) Spirit Resistance and an aura around them that does damage to enemies for a percentage of the enemies' max health.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

The Outer Planes refer to planes of existence beyond the known universe.

Some Outer Planes are connected to Earth by Astral Gates.

## Summary

The Outer Planes include Ixia1] and the Patron's plane.2] Some souls of humans move to an Outer Plane after death.3]

The US is currently sending aid to the Outer Planes due to the military campaign in Ixia.4]1]

The Doorman is an extraplanar being and an "old friend" of the Patrons.5]6]

An exhibit at the Met is due to open up showing off artifacts from the Outer Planes.7]

The New York Astral Gate has led to extraplanar invasive species appearing in the city.8]

Astral Plane Canned Mille-Pattes Dazzle ("mille-pattes" is French for "centipede") are advertised as The World's Most Popular Delicacy.

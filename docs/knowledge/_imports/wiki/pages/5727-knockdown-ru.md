Knockdown is a Tier 3 Спиритизм Item that provides Bonus Health to the purchasing hero.

Knockdown has an active ability that knocks down and Stuns the target after a delay, temporarily preventing them from taking any action. This effect is increased on a target that was airborne. It appears as a large anvil above the target player's head.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

## Notes

The bonus stun duration scales based on the target's height when hit, reaching its maximum +1.5s duration against targets 30m or higher in the air.

## Notable Interactions

- Knockdown is a strong counter to Heroes with flying abilities, such as Виндикта and Серый Коготь, and channeled abilities, such as Семь's Грозовое облако and Пелена's Танец пуль.

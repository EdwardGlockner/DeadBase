Extra Stamina is a Tier 1 Vitality Item that provides Stamina, Stamina Recovery and Fire Rate to the purchasing hero.

Extra Stamina is a component of Превосходная выносливость, Кинетический рывок and Колдовской порыв.

It can be purchased for 800 from the Shop.

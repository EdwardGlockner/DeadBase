Quicksilver Reload is a Tier 2 元灵 Item. Its passive component Imbues the hero's ability with bonus Spirit Damage when hitting an enemy. Additionally, when the ability is used, the hero's weapon is instantly reloaded and gains bonus Fire Rate.

Quicksilver Reload is a Component of 水银重弹.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

- 炽焱 with multiple Catalyst charges

- 岚梦 who benefits from maintaining Fixation stacks on targeted enemies

- 薇妲 渡鸦仆使 has a short cooldown after second upgrade

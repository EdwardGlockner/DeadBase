Items are in-game equipment that provide Heroes with bonus attributes and special abilities.

## Item Types

There are three types of items:

- Weapon: Focus on improving base weapons, damage, and bullet effects.

- Vitality: Focus on improving survivability, defense, and movement.

- Spirit: Focus on improving abilities and spirit power.

Spending Souls on items of each type provides corresponding stats bonuses. The strength of the bonus is dependent on the total cost of the items of each type owned by the player, up to 28,800 Souls.

At 4,800, the player gains a significant investment bonus.

## Active Items

Active items provide additional, temporary effects when activated by a key press. Upon being purchased, they must be bound to one of four individual keys for activation. As a result, a player cannot hold more than four active items at a time.

A player can change the assigned slot for an active item by holding "Tab" or "Alt" and then clicking and dragging the item between slots.

## Cosmetic Items

Cosmetic items are a unique type of active item that are only available during Seasonal events and specific periods. These items are globally unlocked for all players, don't take an item slot or provide bonus stats, and use a unique active item slot ("E" key).

These items can be tested outside their events by using the "giveitem" Console command in the Hideout, Sandbox, or cheat-enabled custom games.

- Snowball (cosmetic_item_snowball)

- Hero Vote Posters (cosmetic_item_voting_poster)

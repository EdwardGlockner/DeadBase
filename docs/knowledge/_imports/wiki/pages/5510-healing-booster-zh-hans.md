Healing Booster is a Tier 2 活力 Item that provides extra Health Regen to the purchasing hero.

Its passive effect grants the user increased effectiveness of Healing.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600, with its Component, 加速回复, making up 800 of the cost.

Healing Booster is a Component of 治疗律动.

## Notes

- Healing Booster increases the effect of healing applied to allies as well as the caster, but owning Healing Booster does not increase the healing received from an ally. Moreover, it also does not affect any healing from health regen.

## 背景故事

### Lore

This section contains spoilers for The Binding of Oathkeeper.

Jeanne "Oma" Geist (married name) was born in France, in 1815. She married German artist1] Johann Geist. Jeanne and Johann had a son named Walter in 1850, and Johann died in 1880.

Circa 1899, when Jeanne was around 84 years old, the first Maelstrom happened, bringing various supernatural forces to Earth, and the spirit Oathkeeper was one of them. Jeanne made a pact with Oathkeeper to regain her youth in exchange for draining the life of others. She was initially reluctant, but accepted the deal. However, with the help of Walter, she made a ward to keep Oathkeeper under control.

Walter had a son whom he named after his father, Johann "Jr." Johann Jr. became a doctor, and after Walter died, he did the maintenance of his grandmother's ward. Jeanne and Walter had "a terrible sense of humor", according to Johann Jr.

Now, 50 years after her initial pact and on the eve of the second Maelstrom, Geist has to decide how she'll deal with Oathkeeper, and whether she'll continue to kill others to stay young or accept her natural aging and eventual death.2]

## 角色趣闻

- Geist is a german word that can be translated as "ghost", "spirit" or "mind". Lady Geist is French, but she uses her late German husband's last name.

- Lady Geist is currently the only hero who holds her gun in the left hand, as Oathkeeper is in her right arm in-game. However, in a concept art and in The Binding of Oathkeeper artwork, Oathkeeper is in her left arm.

- Lady Geist was named Spectre in Neon Prime, and later renamed to Ghost.

- Lady Geist has the lowest health regen in the game at 1.

- There are two work-in-progress Lady Geist models in the game files, labeled Ghost_test and Ghost_v2. These models feature different face designs and a hairstyle closer to her portrait art.

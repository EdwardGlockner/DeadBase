Spirit Snatch is a Tier 3 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200. Its component, Spirit Strike, makes up 800 of the cost.

## Notes

- Hitting multiple heroes in one swing will steal from all struck heroes.

- When a marked target dies, the buffs are instantly lost.

## Notable Interactions

- Indomitable will prevent the player from being teleported to The Baroness if hit with The Doorman's Hotel Guest.

- Indomitable is not triggered by Apollo's Itani Lo Sahn or Dynamo's Singularity. This is likely unintentional, as both abilities trigger Reactive Barrier.

- Indomitable is triggered by and will fully prevent the drag-down effects of Abrams' Seismic Impact and Ivy's Stone Form.

- While performing a similar effect, Phantom Strike's pull does not trigger Indomitable.

- Indomitable will prevent players from being stunned after being parried.

- Indomitable will prevent players from being stunned after getting hit by bullets while on a zipline. They will still fall off the zipline.

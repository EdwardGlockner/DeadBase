A Glide Wall Jump is a Superglide performed directly next to a wall. This allows you to combine the initial impulse from a Mantle Glide with a Wall Jump. Just like a Superglide, this also carries more speed due to the lower friction coefficient in the air.

Glide Wall Jumps allow you to easily gain height and reach otherwise unachievable routes. A Glide Wall Jump can also be used in a route to reduce the amount of stamina used. They are extremely useful for escaping enemies, as they are hard to chase after when performed correctly. Just like Superglides, a sideways Glide Wall Jump can be performed by holding left or right while Glide Wall Jumping.

To execute, start a Mantle Glide near a wall. As soon as you become actionable, quickly jump. If performed correctly, you will follow the path of the wall jump while benefiting from the Mantle Glide's burst of speed.

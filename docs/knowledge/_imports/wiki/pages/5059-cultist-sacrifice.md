Cultist Sacrifice is a Tier 3 Weapon Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200. Its component, Monster Rounds, makes up 800 of the cost.

- They can both also be consumed for the buff and bonus souls.

- The bonus Bullet Resist has no effect against Walkers, Shrines, the Patron, Graves' Ghouls or McGinnis' Turrets, since they deal Spirit Damage.

- Cultist Sacrifice's active counts as Spirit Damage. This means it can activate on-hit spirit damage procs such as Spirit Burn.

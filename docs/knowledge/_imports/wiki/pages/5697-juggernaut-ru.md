Juggernaut is a Tier 4 Живучесть Item that provides Slow Resist, Move Speed, Melee Resist, and Health Regen. Its passive causes enemies that shoot the user to have their Fire Rate slowed.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400, with its Component, Скоростная стойкость, making up 1,600 of the cost.

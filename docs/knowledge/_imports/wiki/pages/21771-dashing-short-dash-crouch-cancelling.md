Short Dash Crouch CancellingAlso known as SDCC, Dash Cancelling. Name by LȊGHTBRINGER. is the act of cancelling a dash by crouching, shortening the dash. SDCC costs one bar of stamina.

To SDCC, initiate a dash, then during the dash animation, quickly press the crouch key.

SDCC is more situational than other universal movement techniques, but is useful nevertheless. It is performed nearly the exact same as a Dash Slide, but duck is only temporarily held. SDCC is particularly helpful during laning to quickly Light Melee enemy troopers to confirm and deny the souls.

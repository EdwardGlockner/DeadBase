青藤是一位 Deadlock 中的英雄。她是由石像鬼蜕变而成的义警，拥有强大的辅助与干扰能力。青藤可与队友建立生命链接共享治疗与增益，同时投掷葛根炸弹生窒息藤蔓区，或化身为石像鬼形态击晕敌人并自我疗伤。凭借独特的飞行能力，她可携带队友或炸弹抵达战场任意位置——将目标空投至敌群中心引发爆炸，或实施战术救援。

青藤曾是一座无生命的石像鬼，直到被召唤苏醒以保护某个女孩及其家人免受门多萨集团的侵害。她誓言守护新家庭——阿若悠一家，并踏上瓦解该集团的义警之路。经过18年的斗争，她最终击败了这个犯罪组织，如今正在西班牙东哈勒姆区之外寻找新的人生目标。

## 背景故事

### Lore

Ivy's sister, Sophia Arroyo, is studying at university under Dynamo,1] and works with Wraith to pay for her tuition. Carlos Arroyo is a 1st-grade teacher2] who's running an election campaign for mayor of New York.3] Ivy also has a little brother called Manny,4] who's a fan of Kelvin.5]

Ivy's visual novel will show her efforts to get a job and support the Arroyos.

Ivy is referred to as "the savior of Spanish Harlem",6]7] but she doesn't like the nickname.8]

Ivy's efforts to save Spanish Harlem were reported by news in Illinois, due to public interest.9]

## 角色互动

- After dedicating most of her life to destroying the Mendoza Syndicate, Ivy is naturally distrustful towards other criminals, especially 灵魅 10] and 大和,11] who are members of criminal organizations. Ivy warns Wraith not to approach Spanish Harlem.12]

- With Sophia being a university student under 奇能, Ivy worries her fighting Dynamo during the ritual1] would preconceive him against Sophia.13]

- Ivy expresses admiration for 希弗14] and calls him a "shampoo ad with knives".15]

## 角色趣闻

- Ivy was named Tengu in Neon Prime.

- Several of Ivy's current animations are derived from those of Kali, an unreleased hero.

- Ivy's gun was originally called Cat Dad's Fancy.

- Ivy's gun is inspired by the German MP 18 submachine gun modified to resemble a crossbow.

- Some of Ivy's voice lines were changed in the 2024-12-06 Update to make her use of Spanish more accurate.

- Ivy has the Roman numeral IV carved on her back.

- Ivy's wings have a pattern resembling church windows.

- Ivy has the lowest melee damage in the game.

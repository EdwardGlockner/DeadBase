Reference extract:

Components in Deadlock are items required to purchase upgraded "parent" items. The pricing system automatically applies discounts when components are obtained, whether directly or through other parent items.

For example, Титанический магазин normally costs 1600 because it includes the 800 cost of its Увеличенный магазин component. If you already own Extended Magazine, Titanic Magazine's price drops to just 800 for the upgrade itself.

This component ownership affects all related parent items. Since Extended Magazine is also required for Растущая стойкость, owning Titanic Magazine will make Escalating Resilience cheaper too (reduced from 3200 to 2400).

Units, also known as NPCs or Non-Playable Characters, are any entities in Deadlock which are not directly controlled by the players.

## Structures

Structures or Objectives are large Units belonging to each team. They do not respawn.

- Guardians

- Walkers

- Shrines

- Patron

## Creeps

Creeps are Units that respawn, either in waves or after a specific time.

- Troopers

- Denizens

- Mid-Boss

## Hero-Created Units

Units created by Heroes.

- Graves' Ghouls

- McGinnis' Mini Turrets

- Rem's Lil Helpers

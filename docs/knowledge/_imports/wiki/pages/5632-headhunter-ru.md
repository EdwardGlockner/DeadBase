Headhunter is a Tier 3 Оружие Item that grants increased Weapon Damage and Bonus Health. Its passive effect causes headshots to deal bonus damage, heal the user and briefly grant them bonus movement speed.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200 (its Component, Усилитель выстрелов в голову, makes up 800 of the cost).

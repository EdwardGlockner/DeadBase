Debuff Reducer is a Tier 2 Vitality Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

It is a component of Spellbreaker and Unstoppable.

## Interactions with Crowd-Control

Debuff Reducer reduces the duration of stuns and similar effects inflicted by the following abilities:

- Abrams: Shoulder Charge, Seismic Impact

- Apollo: Disengaging Sigil, Riposte

- Bebop: Exploding Uppercut, Grapple Arm (cast microslow + T1 debuff), Hyper Beam (effect is negligible)

- Billy: Bashdown, Blasted

- Celeste: Light Eater, Dazzling Trick, Shining Wonder

- Drifter: Rend (T3 debuff), Stalker's Mark

- Dynamo: Kinetic Pulse

- Graves: Jar of Dead (T2 slow), Grasping Hands, Borrowed Decree

- Grey Talon: Rain of Arrows (T1 slow), Spirit Snare, Guided Owl

- Haze: Sleep Dagger, Fixation

- Holliday: Powder Keg, Crackshot, Spirit Lasso

- Infernus: Napalm, Afterburn, Concussive Combustion

- Ivy: Entangling Thorns, Stone Form, Air Drop (T2 slow, T3 silence)

- Kelvin: Frost Grenade

- Lady Geist: Life Drain, Malice, Soul Exchange (cast microslow + T2 silence)

- Lash: Ground Strike (T2 displacement + slow), Flog (T1 slow), Death Slam

This effect helps mitigate crowd-control by shortening stuns' durations, allowing the hero to recover more quickly.

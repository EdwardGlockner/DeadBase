Death occurs when a hero takes sufficient damage to be reduced to zero health. Upon dying, the player's screen is shaded gray, a death timer is displayed over their hero's icon, souls are rewarded to heroes who earned a kill or an assist, and other hero or situation-specific events may occur, as detailed below.

Revive Tokens are obtained from the Crystal that drops upon killing the Mid-Boss. The Crystal contains a total of 3 Revive Tokens.

Revive Tokens can only be claimed by a Heavy Melee, and only 1 is granted per punch.

Revive Tokens are consumed upon a players death, and any further deaths from that player will not consume more. Once all the Tokens are consumed, the entire team will lose access to the mid-boss buff.

Killing a player with a Rejuvenator credit does not appear as a kill in the HUD and does not grant any souls, but it does trigger "on hero kill" effects such as the cooldown reset of Shiv's Killing Blow or the stack buildup of Grey Talon's, Mo & Krill's and Vindicta's ultimates. The same happens when triggering Victor's Shocking Reanimation.

## Trivia

- Soul Rebirth was an item that made the player respawn 4s after their death, in the location where they died. It also increased the regular respawn time, when the player died while Soul Rebirth was on cooldown. This item has been removed from the game.

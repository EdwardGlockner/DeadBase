Reference extract:

Extra slots are three item slots that are unlocked after certain objectives are completed over the course of a match.

## Unlocking

| Position | Objective |

| --- | --- |

| Bottom Left | Unlocked after destroying one enemy Walker |

| Top Right | Unlocked after destroying two enemy Walkers |

| Bottom Right | Unlocked after destroying three enemy Walkers |

— Soul Urn

The Soul Urn, also known as Spirit Urn, Soul Jar or Spirit Jar, is an objective that spawns at the 10-minute mark and respawns in 5-minute intervals.

Players can pick up the urn and deliver it to its destination at the side lanes of the map. Doing so grants the courier Souls and 4 Golden statue buffs.

- Going through the Doorman's Doors drops the urn at the entrance door.

- Mirage - Traveler

- Rem - Tag Along

- Sinclair - Spectral Assistant teleport

- Viscous - Goo Ball

Melee attacks (light or heavy) and Melee Damage abilities from enemy Players (or NPCs), and any stun source will force the courier to drop the urn.

Once every 6s, the Urn will say a voice line that can be heard by nearby players.

Note: The urn's soul value is determined at the moment it spawns. Holding onto the urn for longer durations does not increase its value.

### Anti-stalling mechanics

If the urn hasn't been delivered within 45s of being picked up, it will start decaying in value over another 45s, until its value reaches zero and it expires. This is indicated by the "delivering the urn" text turning red and counting down.

## Reward

The Soul Urn's base soul bounty is 250 Souls and increases by 70 Souls per minute of game time. This value is "locked in" at the minute the Urn spawns.

The souls are released as Soul Orbs and can be denied. If not denied, all souls go to the courier. If an ally secures an orb, they share the orb value with the courier.

The courier also receives 4 random Golden Statue buffs.

### Comeback bonus

If the courier is on the trailing team, they get a soul bonus that scales based on game time and the team's soul deficit. It uses a growth rate of 170 Souls per minute.

Approximate Formula: Bonus = 170 × Minutes × Deficit %

## Lore

The Urn is sentient and says its job is to keep New York clean by capturing rogue spirits, which should then be released in another location.

The Urn nags heroes to be delivered quickly and believes its work is underappreciated.

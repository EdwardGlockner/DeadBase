Kinetic Dash is a Tier 2 Zbraň Item that grants Stamina and Stamina Recovery. Its passive effect causes a hero's next Dash Jump to grant bonus Fire Rate and Ammo until they next reload.

Kinetic Dash is a Tier 2 Zbraň Předmět that can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600. Its component, Dodatečná výdrž, makes up 800 of the cost.

Deadlock is a third-person shooter MOBA developed and published by Valve Corporation. It was first publicly announced on August 23, 2024, though it initially gained public attention after being leaked by playtesters earlier that year.1] The official website is https://www.playdeadlock.com/. The game can only be played by receiving an invite from a Steam friend who already has the game. The game is still in development and is played through a Closed Beta2] Early Development Build.1]

While Deadlock hasn't been officially mentioned or promoted by Valve beyond its Steam page, various Valve employees, contractors and voice actors have acknowledged their work on the game on social media, and it was mentioned in the Half-Life 2 anniversary documentary on November 2024.6]

## Invites

Any player with access to Deadlock can invite an unlimited number of Steam friends.

Here are recommended resources to get an invite:

- Deadlock Steam forums

- Reddit r/DeadlockTheGame invite megathread

- Deadlock Community Discord invite thread

An invite can take minutes to hours to be processed, and has to be accepted by the player. If the invite notification doesn't appear after a few hours, check your Steam playtest invites. If a player has refused an invite in the past, they may be unable to receive a new one.

### Official forums

Once a player has joined the game, they can click the "give feedback" button in the main menu to create a forum account and join the official Discord server. Please note that the Discord link is one use only.

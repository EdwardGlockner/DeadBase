Melee Damage is a stat that Heroes start with and determines how much damage a hero's Melee Attack deals.

Melee damage scales with Boons and Items. It also scales with the Weapon Damage stat at a rate of 50%. Most abilities with weapon damage effects also affect melee damage, but there are exceptions such as Metal Skin.

Most heroes have the same base melee damage and scaling, with a few exceptions (see table below).

The Bermuda Triangle was never a myth, it was a shadowline nexus deep underneath the Atlantic Ocean. For the most part, the things that escaped this astral gate were benign (like Nessie), but now something dark and predatory known only as The Adversary hunts the seas... and ocean denizens are scared to death. One of those inhabitants, a psychic sea anemone, wants to do something about it. Summoning an ectoplasmic goo body to traverse the surface, Viscous is desperately trying to summon the Patrons in order to keep The Deep safe from the horror that lurks below.

Unlike Viscous, the other denizens of the Deep find legs "unnerving".13]

### Personality

Viscous is innocent and optimistic, eager to learn about the surface world and make new friends. He likes to compliment others,14]15] although he's "socially awkward" and unfamiliar with the customs of human society.16] Viscous likes people-watching,17] and enjoys violence, which surprises him.18]19] Viscous commonly refers to people as "surface dwellers".20]21]

Despite his upbeat nature, Viscous takes his mission to stop the Adversary very seriously.22]23] He believes that others underestimate him and ignore the problems of the Deep.24]25]26] The other heroes don't know or don't take the threat of the Adversary seriously.27]

Viscous often speaks in a very literal way.28]29]30] The concept of sarcasm seems to be new to him.31] Viscous can't read and doesn't have a mouth or nose (though he's likely referring to his goo body, since sea anemones do have mouths).32]33]14]

### Appearance

Viscous' current model is a modified version of Kelvin's Neon Prime model. He has a green goo body shaped like an astronaut suit, with black shoes and gloves, and a black patch on his right shoulder. His head is a green sphere with a black neck connection and two misshapen black dots for eyes (the right one is slightly bigger). A black stick "skeleton" with ball joints can be seen inside his body. His weapon is a futuristic two-handed gun, similar to Kelvin's but black.

Viscous has had two proposed redesigns in the files, one in which he had a large body with a coral arm and an octopus-like creature inside his head, and another in which he was characterized as a tourist wearing shorts, sunglasses, an orange cap and a portable camera. His current portrait artwork and icon depict Viscous with a bulky featureless goo body, more similar to his current model.

### Information

The Cube is a cube made of protoplasmic goo.34] The Cube nullifies all pain, even for characters like Victor.35] Viscous doesn't question how the Cube works, even when it can heal non-organic bodies like Bebop's.36] Viscous spends time in the Cube whenever he feels sad.37]

## Character Relationships

### The Adversary

The Adversary is massacring the denizens of the Deep,3] and Viscous believes that, if he can't stop the Adversary, it's only a matter of time before the Adversary starts killing everything on the surface.22]23] Viscous has entered the ritual to stop the Adversary, hoping to get rid of it to save the Deep.1]

### Haze

Haze takes Viscous and his problems seriously.25]

Haze wants to know more about the Adversary in case more creatures like it come through the Deep's astral gate.38]

## Character Interactions

- Viscous offers the Cube to Ms. Shelly and Grey Talon.39]37]

- Viscous likes Holliday's hat and wants to wear it.15]

- Viscous thinks Seven and Warden are scary.40]41]

- When Seven learns about Viscous' mission, he admits there's more to Viscous than he expected.22]

- Abrams,42] Bebop, Holliday, Lash, Mina, Mo & Krill and Paradox refer to Viscous as "goo boy".43]

- Grey Talon offers to have The Baxter Society help Viscous defeat the Adversary should they fail the ritual.44]

- Holliday mistakes Viscous' home, The Deep, for the American Deep South. She asks if he's from Mobile, Alabama.45]

- Lady Geist and McGinnis want to salvage some of Viscous' goo for themselves.46]47]

- Calico is interested in Viscous' suit.48]

- Paige wants to take Viscous home and give him a hug.49]

- Infernus advises Viscous to decline Wraith's offers to play cards.50]

- Apollo accidentally invites Viscous to one of his father's galas.51]

## Removed Lore

Viscous seemingly used to have a different backstory. According to old Patron lines, Viscous was shunned by humanity52] and seen as "a mistake" and "an abomination".53] Viscous was ambitious54] and wanted his name etched in history, instead of "remembered with the fondness of a bus driver he couldn't bother to learn the name of".55]

### Removed character interactions

- Fathom was another hero from the Deep, like Viscous. It's unknown how Fathom and Viscous were related, as they didn't have voice lines for each other.

### Neon Prime

In Neon Prime, Viscous looked the same as the first version of his current model, but he had an icon in which he was depicted with a pufferfish inside his head.

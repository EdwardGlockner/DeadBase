## 背景故事

### Lore

Mo is a mole man. Krill's species is currently unknown, but he has monkey-like characteristics. McGinnis refers to Krill as a rat.1]

Mo's full name is Maurice. Krill also calls him Momo.

Mo and Krill have been forced to live in the margins of society.2] They don't pay taxes.3] They want to achieve the respect and glory they believe is owed to them.4]

Mo practices pottery, and Krill is writing a memoir.5] Mo wants to audit pottery classes at Columbia University.6]

Mo & Krill don't like the Friends of Humanity.7]

Mendoza was a client of Mo & Krill.8]

## 角色互动

- Mo & Krill protected 口袋 and they regard each other as friends.9]10]11]

- Mo & Krill are in 灵魅's weekly poker game and owe money to her. If they're on the same team, Wraith may say she called a favor to have them fighting on her side. They regard each other as friends.12]13]

- After killing Mo & Krill, 大和 may say she wants to turn them into a coat and a hat, respectively.14]

- Mo & Krill watch 比波普's fights in the Bear Pit. They know Miss Shelly and have tried to help her.12]

- Krill has read 开尔文's books and found them "riveting".12]

- 柒 hides in Mo & Krill's subway tunnels, without their permission.12]

Blood Tribute is a Tier 3 Weapon Item that provides bonus health and a life-drain Active. Activating this item will continually drain the triggering hero's health to provide Debuff Resist as well as improving Fire Rate and Move Speed. During this drain, all healing received will be reduced.

Unlike most active items, Blood Tribute has no cooldown or duration limit and instead can be toggled on and off indefinitely. The health drain cannot kill its user.

It can be purchased for 3,200 from the Shop.

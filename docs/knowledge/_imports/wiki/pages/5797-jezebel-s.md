Jezebel's Billiard Hall is a sketchy yet popular bar located in New York.

Jezebel's is described in The New York Oracle as a place where people can bet their souls over a game of pool.2]

Shortly before the events of the game take place, Jezebel's is under threat of getting shut down by the Alchemy Bureau due to a large number of infractions, including gambling souls without a license, distributing spirits from Ixia without a permit, and not having the Hex & Alchemy Bureau authorize a ward at the bar's entrance. Infernus and Hank are given 2 weeks to resolve the infractions.3]

### Hank

Hank is a portly, middle-aged man who owns Jezebel's.4]5] He's worried about the bar's infractions and is willing to "make a deal with the devil himself" in order to save his bar.6]7]

The threat of Jezebel's being shut down and what it means for Hank is why Infernus is participating in the ritual.8]9] Since Infernus doesn't want Hank to worry about him, he hasn't told Hank about his involvement with the ritual.10]

Infernus mentions that Hank "saved his life".11] The implication seems to be that Hank helped Infernus move on from his criminal youth and turn over a new leaf as a bartender.12]

Hank is also implied to be friends with Celeste,10] Lady Geist,7] and Mo & Krill.13]

In one pathway in The Binding of Oathkeeper, Lady Geist pawns off Oathkeeper to Hank on the promise of getting Hank the resources needed to save Jezebel's.7]

## Trivia

- The full name of Jezebel's seems to be "Jezebel's Billard Hall".2]

- McGinnis describes Jezebel's as "too fancy".14]

- Lady Geist and Krill offer to help prevent Jezebel's from closing.6]13]

- Viscous asks Infernus why the bar isn't called "Hank's" instead of Jezebel's. Infernus answers that it's a long story.31]

- During the festive season, Jezebel's has a promotion of a free cup of hot chocolate for every game of pool.32]

The Arroyos are

's surrogate family that live in Spanish Harlem.

Ivy is reluctant to leave the Arroyos on their own for too long.4]5] This could be due to the fear that the Arroyos will be hurt if she's away from them.6]7]8]

The Arroyos eat Mofongo, a traditional Puerto-Rican dish.2]9]

The Arroyos are likely Catholic, based on Ivy's reaction to killing Venator.10]11]

There are four known Arroyos: Carlos, Maria, Sophia and Manny.

### Carlos Arroyo

Carlos Arroyo is the father of the family.

Carlos works as a first-grade teacher12] and a community organiser for Spanish Harlem, however he's also running for mayor of New York.13] In one pathway in Ivy's visual novel, Ivy supports Carlos' political campaign and participates in the ritual to help him become mayor.14]

Infernus is voting for Carlos to become mayor.15] Billy thinks Carlos is a fraud who uses Ivy for publicity and to increase the property value of Spanish Harlem.16]17]

Carlos is worried about Ivy participating in the ritual.18] He dislikes Fairfax Industries19] and isn't thrilled about Ivy fighting against the OSIC during the ritual.20]

Ivy idolises Carlos.21]22] Carlos tells Ivy that it takes more muscles to frown than to smile.23] He also says that "you can make opportunities, but you can't make people take them".24]

### Maria Arroyo

Maria Arroyo is the mother of the family.

Not much is known about Maria, however she does have a date planned with Carlos at the Baroness Hotel.25] Ivy also doesn't want her to know about Venator's involvement in the ritual.11]

### Sophia Arroyo

Sophia Arroyo, also known as Sophie, is the elder daughter of the Arroyos. Based on her being a child when Ivy was brought to life, Sophia's age is likely in her mid-twenties.

Unknown to Ivy and the other Arroyos, Sophia works at Wraith's casino, helping Wraith as her bookmaker.26]27] In one pathway in Ivy's visual novel, Ivy discovers Sophia's secret job.28] While the ending of that pathway is unknown, it likely results in Ivy participating in the ritual to keep Sophia safe.29]

Sophia attends Columbia College as one of Dynamo's students.30] Sophia speaks highly of Dynamo31]32] and Dynamo thinks she's a great student, but he is worried about her due to Sophia sleeping during lessons due to working long shifts at her job.33] Dynamo is unaware that Sophia works for Wraith.

Sophia tells Ivy that, if she gets mad, she should hold her breath and count to four.34]

Sophia was the Arroyo who named Ivy.2]

Sophia listens to Cadence's band.35]

### Manny Arroyo

Manny Arroyo is the younger son of the Arroyos. Manny's age is unknown, however he's implied to still be in school.36]

Manny seems to be a bookworm, as he loves reading about Kelvin's adventures37] and gets taken to libraries by Ivy for Paige's Story Time.38]

## Trivia

- Spanish Harlem, also known as East Harlem or El Barrio, is a real New York neighborhood with majority Hispanic population.

- The Arroyos seem to be spelt "The Arroyo's" instead of "The Arroyos", based on Ivy's background.2]

- The Doorman says that one of the Arroyos will die in 5 years, no matter what Ivy does.7] This could be a bluff, but it's unknown.

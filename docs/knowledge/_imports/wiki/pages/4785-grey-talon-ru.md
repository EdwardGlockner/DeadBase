Grey Talon is a Hero in Deadlock. He is a clever, long-range combatant that uses his powerful bow in combination with traps and mystical flight to pin down and destroy his foes. With the ability to charge his arrow shot for powerful effect, Grey Talon can harry his foes at a distance before launching a guided Spirit Owl to finish them off.

A founder of the monster hunting organisation The Baxter Society, Grey Talon happily retired after 40 years of service to support his extended family. He was living well until he was alerted that that his son and daughter-in-law had died in a mysterious house fire. Now, he hunts the perpetrators of the arson, bow in hand.

## Background

### Lore

Grey Talon believes the Friends of Humanity are behind the murder of his family.1]2]

Grey Talon chose Shiv as his replacement in the Baxter Society.3]4]

Grey Talon's son was named Nathan.5]

Grey Talon has visited South Ixia and said it was "beautiful".6]

## Character interactions

- Grey Talon and Виндикта have hunted the Friends of Humanity together and regard each other as friends.7]8]9]

- Grey Talon says his "stepdaughter" is Ixian, like Абрамс.10] However, it seems that the intended term was "daughter-in-law", as evidenced by Talon's background and Abrams' response.

- Пелена knows Grey Talon. She refers to him by his first name and knew his late son, Nathan.5]

- Ямато respects Grey Talon for his age and skill.11]12]

- Дозорный says he looked up to Grey Talon and apologizes for killing him.13]14]

- Калико compares Холлидей's introverted personality to Grey Talon's.15]

- Калико calls Grey Talon "a relic of a bygone era",16] but also says she will mourn his death.17]

- Холлидей likes Grey Talon due to their shared goal of revenge.18]

- Калико, Макгиннис and Гадюка comment on Grey Talon's health, due to his age.18]19]

## Trivia

- Grey Talon's current model is inherited from his design in Neon Prime, in which he was called Orion.

- Grey Talon's bow was originally called Koschei.

- Gray Talon is currently the only hero whose Bullet Damage is directly affected by Spirit Power.

- Grey Talon has been affectionately nicknamed "Grandma" by the playerbase.

Mystic Reverb is a Tier 4 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

- Mystic Reverb can be imbued on Lady Geist's Soul Exchange, but it has no effect.

- Spirit Snatch applies after the melee damage and does not allow melee abilities to use Mystic Reverb.

- However, Quicksilver Reload and Mercurial Magnum grant bonus spirit damage to the imbued ability's first hit of weapon or spirit damage and can activate the debuff.

- The 50% additional damage only applies to the first instance of damage for abilities that deal more than one instance, such as Abrams' Siphon Life or Calico's Gloom Bombs

- The debuff can store damage from items like Quicksilver Reload or Tankbuster on the imbued ability.

- The 50% extra damage is calculated based on pre-mitigation damage, but can then still be mitigated by spirit resistance when it triggers.

## Known Bugs

- The initial reverb damage can kill the primary target, but is sometimes non-lethal to secondary targets, leaving them with at least 1 hp remaining. This seems to mainly happen against Target Dummies in Sandbox

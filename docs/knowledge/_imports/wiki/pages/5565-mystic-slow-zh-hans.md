Mystic Slow is a Tier 2 元灵 Item that provides Bonus Health and Sprint Speed to the purchasing hero. If a target of the purchasing hero takes Spirit Damage, they have their Movement Speed reduced.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

Mystic Slow is a component of 雷鸣卷轴.

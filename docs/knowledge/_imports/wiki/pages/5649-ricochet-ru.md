Ricochet is a Tier 4 Оружие Item that grants increased Fire Rate. Its passive causes bullets to ricochet to 2 nearby enemies (13m), dealing 65% damage. Any bullet based procs or effects will also be applied to ricocheted shots.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

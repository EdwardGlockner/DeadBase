Metal Skin is a Tier 3 活力 Item that provides Bullet Resist to the purchasing hero.

Its active ability grants the user immunity to bullet damage whilst slowing their movement speed and shortening their dash distance for a short duration. Attacks can still hit the user and proc effects such as 减速弹.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

## Notable Interactions

- Metal Skin prevents the bullet damage from 岚梦's 子弹之舞, but the user will still take the added spirit damage from 锁定 stacks.

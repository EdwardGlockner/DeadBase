Mercurial Magnum is a Tier 4 Spirit Item that provides Ammo and Spirit Power.

Its passive Imbues the hero's ability with bonus Spirit Damage when hitting an enemy. Additionally, when the ability is used, the hero's weapon is instantly reloaded, gains bonus Fire Rate, and deals bonus spirit damage with their bullets.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400, with its Component, Ртутная перезарядка, making up 1,600 of the cost.

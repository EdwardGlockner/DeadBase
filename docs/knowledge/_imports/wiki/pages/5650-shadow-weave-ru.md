Shadow Weave is a Tier 3 Оружие Item that provides Out of Combat Regen and Sprint Speed to the purchasing hero.

Shadow Weave has an Active ability that grants the hero Stealth. Attacking or using an ability ends Stealth and temporarily grants bonus Fire Rate and Spirit Power.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

- The increase in fire rate after ending stealth is gradual, increasing to to the full 30% after about half a second.

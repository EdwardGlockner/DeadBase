While dismounting using crouch from a Transit Line, a small 0.14 second (or 9 ticks) window opens where you can execute a Small Zip Jump. Jumping during this window results in a regular jump from the Transit Line with the added benefit of no air drag from the crouch dismount.

This is very useful in conjunction with Zipline Momentum Conservation for rollouts; the extra height unlocks new routes that would not be possible otherwise.

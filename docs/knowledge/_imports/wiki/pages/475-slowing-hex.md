Slowing Hex is a Tier 2 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

It is a component of Vortex Web.

- Movement Silence removes the knockback movement from Shiv's alt-fire.

## Hero abilities silenced by Slowing Hex

- Abrams: Shoulder Charge, Seismic Impact

- Apollo: Disengaging Sigil, Flawless Advance, Itani Lo Sahn

- Billy: Rising Ram

- Calico: Leaping Slash, Ava, Return to Shadows

- Drifter: Stalker's Mark (only second activation (teleport) gets silenced)

- Dynamo: Quantum Entanglement

- Grey Talon: Rain of Arrows

- Holliday: Bounce Pad

- Infernus: Flame Dash

- Ivy: Air Drop

- Kelvin: Ice Path

- Lash: Grapple

- Mina: Sanguine Retreat

- Mirage: Dust Devil

- Pocket: Flying Cloak (only second activation (teleport) gets silenced)

- Rem: Tag Along

- Shiv: Slice and Dice, Killing Blow

- Silver: Boot Kick

- Silver (Transformed): Mauling Leap

- Sinclair: Spectral Assistant (only second activation (teleport) gets silenced)

- Vindicta: Flight

- Vyper: Slither

- Wraith: Project Mind

- Yamato: Flying Slash

### Notes

- The Doorman's Doorway and Mo & Krill's Burrow are not affected by Slowing Hex.

- Slowing Hex does not disable passive effects such as Vyper's Slither (though it appears visually as though it is disabled).

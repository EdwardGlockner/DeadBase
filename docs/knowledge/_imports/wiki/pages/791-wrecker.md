Information

— Lady Geist

Wrecker is an unreleased Hero in Deadlock, previously available in the Hero Labs gamemode. Stunning enemies with his Wrecking Ball, Wrecker will Consume them to satisfy his hunger, then spit out their remains in a Bio Blast. His foes will suffer devastating damage once he charges his Overload.

Employed by Eldritch Tactical Solutions, Wrecker is a monstrous creature who made a reputation as "The Butcher of Ixia" for his dirty work in extraplanar wars.

Wrecker was added to Hero Labs on October 24, 2024, and removed from the game when the mode was discontinued on August 18, 2025.

## Backstory

### Lore

Wrecker speaks in a different language and has a limited English vocabulary. He is described by other heroes as a "gremlin"1]2] and a "purple thing with eyes".3] This could be a hint at his final design.

He is known as The Butcher of Ixia,4] and fought in the fields of North Ixia.5] Wrecker's military company has been accused of commiting war crimes.6]

Wrecker's background suggests that World War I and World War II didn't happen in the Deadlock timeline.

## Character Interactions

- Pocket doesn't like Wrecker and wants to dismantle Eldritch Tactical Solutions.7]8]

- Seven believes Wrecker is an extra-planar being.9]

- Wrecker calls Abrams "blue meat"10] and Mo & Krill "mole meat".11]

## Trivia

- Their temporary model appears to incorporate the mesh for the Clockwerk's Helm, a promotional cosmetic for Team Fortress 2. The Clockwerk's Helm is in turn based on the hero Clockwerk from Dota 2.

- Wrecker's current model and animations are based on Paradox's model.

- Wrecker's weapon was originally called Demolition Hymn.

- "Paranormal Military Companies" are a reference to real-world Private Military Companies, also identified as PMC.

— Mirage

Raven is an unreleased Hero in Deadlock, a Russian operative seeking to re-corporealize Catherine the Great by using cunning and tactics to outwit his opponents. Raven Blindsides his enemies with concussion grenades, launching into the air with an Umbrella maneuver to continue blasting and shooting his target with a bonus for shooting them in the back. Against multiple foes, Raven activates his Revelation relic to punish them for looking in his direction, Cursing those who look too long in the process.

As the Spymaster of the Soviet Empire, "The Raven" has sought every scrap of ancient writing, artifacts, and history in an effort to give the specter of Catherine the Great a body once more. In the Cursed Apple, the Patrons offer him the best chance to achieve his goal.

Raven was added to Hero Labs on December 6, 2024, and removed from the game when the mode was discontinued on August 18, 2025.

## Backstory

### Lore

Raven carries a canopic jar, used by ancient Egyptians to store the soul of the dead in the mummification process.1] It is used in his ultimate.

Raven mentions Rasputin2] and the Romanovs.3]

Raven hopes the OSIC will be cooperative to his plans.4]

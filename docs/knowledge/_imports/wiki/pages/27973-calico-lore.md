Reference extract:

Once a decorated member of The Baxter Society, Calico left the august order once she realized that killing monsters for free was less profitable than killing people for money. Now an in demand contract killer, Calico is living her best life ending someone else's.

Calico has been an assassin for at least 5 years, as she was hired to kill Pocket 5 years before the events of the ritual.9] Pocket survived the assassination attempt due Calico's bullet missing their heart.10] It's implied that this failed assassination earned Calico a reputation for "sloppy work" amongst other assassins, which she wasn't aware of.11]

Now, Calico is being hunted by the International Police,12] Scotland Yard,13] and the OSIC.14]15] While she has successfully evaded all of them so far, Calico has seemingly chosen to participate in the ritual to gain power and protection.1]2] She also intends to "show off her product" by killing a Patron.16] Calico has been waiting to summon the Patrons for a long time.17]

Calico is consistently called "the black sheep" of the Baxter Society.18]19] The Baxter Society doesn't seem to be after Calico.20]

Calico has a son, who makes "terrible decisions" according to her.21]

Though Calico's origin is unknown, she has a Received Pronunciation British accent.

### Personality

Calico is a snob. She views herself as "superior" and classy and finds it important to surround herself with "disposable" people.22]23]24] Calico believes that her allies should listen to her and do as she says.25]26] A few characters view Calico as egotistical,27]28] although Calico thinks that she's just honest with herself.29]

Calico is very knowledgeable about a variety of topics, including the Deep,30]31] the Troubadour,32] and Oathkeeper.33] She's implied to know something about Victor's origins.34]

Calico doesn't seem to have loyalties to anyone,35] and criticizes Warden for his "blind loyalty".36] However, there is an implication that Calico does slightly miss the life she once had, as her "heart is cold, but it is not dead".37] It's implied that Calico has dreams of her time at the Baxter Society.38]

### Appearance

Calico is a slender black woman, with yellow eyes hidden by her round glasses with white lenses, and black hair tied in a bun, with short curls behind her ears. She wears a purple jacket with shoulder pads and a long square coattail over a white shirt, baggy dark purple pants, heeled purple shoes and belt, and a purple glove in her left hand. She has a prosthetic right arm which she uses as her weapon. Her accessories are a pair of circular golden earrings and a large purple skull-shaped pin in her chest.

She carries Ava in her left shoulder, a purple Sphynx cat with full yellow eyes.

## Character Relationships

### Ava

Ava is Calico's Sphynx cat familiar.39]

Calico adores Ava and refers to herself as "mummy" when talking to Ava.40]41] She threatens anyone who may want to hurt or steal Ava.42]

### Pocket and Maximilian Fairfax

Calico was the assassin sent to kill Pocket 5 years before the events of the ritual.9]

Calico enjoys "tying up loose ends" when she kills Pocket in-game.43] She also mentions that Maximilian Fairfax will "have questions",44] lining up with Pocket's belief that Maximilian Fairfax was the one who sent Calico to kill Pocket.

Pocket, naturally, dislikes Calico for almost killing them.45]46]

### Grey Talon

Calico used to work with Grey Talon in the Baxter Society.

While Grey Talon is disappointed in Calico for becoming an assassin,47] he doesn't resent her and was content to leave her alone.20]48]49]

Calico views Grey Talon as a "relic of a bygone era",50] but also says she will mourn his death if she kills him during the ritual.51]

### Venator

Venator knew Calico from when she was in the Baxter Society, but loathes her now that she's an assassin.52]

Venator believes that, for the right price, Calico would be willing to kill Ava.53]

When killing Calico, Venator mentions that it was "for Grey Talon".54] He also references the 30 pieces of silver that Judas received for betraying Jesus when killing Calico, presumably referring to Calico leaving the Baxter Society.55]

### The Doorman

The Doorman is familiar with Calico and likes her.56]57] He talks with her about "professional interests in town", likely meaning that Calico told him about her job as an assassin.58]

The Doorman loves Ava and, in the event that Calico will be unable to take care of Ava anymore, says he'll treat her "like a queen" at the Baroness Hotel.59]60]

## Removed Lore

When Calico was first added to Hero Labs on October 24, 2024, she originally had a different background:

Granted the dubious honor of being named Time Magazine's most dangerous woman on the planet, Calico is an enigmatic occult arms dealer that is willing to fund any and all sides of a conflict if the price is right.

Calico's lore was updated when she officially officially released on January 17, 2025 to her current lore.

### Neon Prime - Nano

In Neon Prime, Calico was originally a character named Nano.

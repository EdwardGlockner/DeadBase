Sinclair is a Hero in Deadlock. With their trusty magic wand, they can shoot Vexing Bolts to surprise enemies and use magic to turn them into Rabbits. The Spectral Assistant amplifies their damage and can serve as a fast escape route. Sinclair can even turn their enemies' abilities against themselves with their surprising final trick, Audience Participation.

Henry and Savannah were two young stage magicians in love, who bound their souls to each other for eternity. However, their relationship was strained after a tragic on-stage accident claimed both of their lives. Now, under the shared title of Sinclair, they seek a way to be separated and find peace in the afterlife.

Sinclair was added to Hero Labs (as Magician) on October 24, 2024, and officially released on January 17, 2025 (as THE MAGNIFICENT SINCLAIR).

### Audience Participation

AUDIENCE PARTICIPATION

12s

40%

- The "Upgrade" effect determines what Tier level the copied ultimate will be. (The ultimate that is copied will be restricted to however many upgrade Tiers Sinclair has unlocked.)

- Sinclair has a 1s delay to use the target ultimate after copying.

- Sinclair can use Audience Participation even if the target enemy hasn't unlocked their ultimate yet.

- Dynamo's Singularity

- Haze's Bullet Dance

- McGinnis' Heavy Barrage

- Mo & Krill's Combo

- Rem's Naptime

- Seven's Storm Cloud

- Warden's Last Stand (while channeling)

- If Sinclair copies an ultimate but does not use it, Audience Participation uses its default cooldown of 85s (with the exception of Vindicta's Assassinate).

- Imbuing Audience Participation exclusively affects the ultimate-copying ability, and not the copied ultimate itself. For instance, imbuing Compress Cooldown only reduces the default cooldown after the copying duration expires, and imbuing Mystic Expansion only increases the range from which ultimates can be stolen.

- Sinclair only copies the properties of the ultimate, which can make it weaker compared to the original hero. For example, when copying Haze's Bullet Dance, they will deal less damage due to their lower fire rate and lack of the Fixation passive ability.

- Sinclair gets permanent bonus health from kills or assists with Mo & Krill's Combo, permanent bonus spirit power from kills with Grey Talon's Guided Owl, and Bonus Weapon Damage from kills with Vindicta's Assassinate.

- Audience Participation has instant cooldown if Sinclair kills an enemy with Shiv's Killing Blow T3.

- If Sinclair copies Vindicta's Assassinate, they start with one less charge.

- Sinclair gets Shiv's Rage passive if they copy Killing Blow, dealing bonus damage when at full Rage. The Rage lasts until Sinclair either loses Killing Blow or Rage goes down to zero. However, if Sinclair copies Killing Blow again, they will still have the remaining built-up Rage from the last copy, allowing them to reach full Rage faster.

- If Sinclair copies Silver's Lycan Curse, they can transform instantly with no build-up. However, likely due to a bug, Sinclair will not gain the upgrades for two specific Silver (Transformed) abilities if their base counterparts are currently deployed: a deployed Vexing Bolt results in an un-upgraded Go For The Throat, and a deployed Spectral Assistant results in an un-upgraded Mauling Leap.

## Trivia

- Sinclair changes voices on death, depending on which ghost is controlling the body. It starts as Henry's voice, then changes to Savannah's when Henry dies and vice-versa.

- The weapon was previously named Legerdemain and Magic Wand.

- Sinclair's current design is based on one of the scrapped designs for Wraith. It is also used in a Metal Skin advert.

- Sinclair's lead developer is Hopoo,5] best known as the co-creator of the Risk of Rain series.

- The Spectral Assistant currently uses a modified version of Lady Geist's original model prior to the Six Heroes update.

- Sinclair's bunny uses the same model as Arnabus the Fairy Rabbit from Dota 2.

- Savannah's voice actor, Andia Winslow, also plays the role of Allie Kinkaid in Holliday's visual novel.

- "Working Magic in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Sinclair in the Hideout.

- When copying Yamato's Shadow Transformation, the description shows "Become infused with Sinclair's shadow soul." instead of 'Become infused with Yamato's shadow soul."

- If a hero has a voice line warning against a specific enemy ultimate, that voice line is triggered if the enemy Sinclair copies and uses that ultimate.

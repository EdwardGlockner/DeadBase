## Sky Lift

Storm Cloud(by default, 4) is Seven’s ultimate ability. In it, he channels an expanding storm cloud that zaps opponents in its radius.

Normally, when channelling Storm Cloud, you are lifted ~60 units upwards. By dropping down from the transit line during the two second air drag buff then using Storm Cloud, you are able to very quickly rise upwards.

This allows you to essentially get the benefits of Majestic Leap on Seven without spending 3,200.

To execute, use duck to drop down from the transit line, then within two seconds, use Storm Cloud.

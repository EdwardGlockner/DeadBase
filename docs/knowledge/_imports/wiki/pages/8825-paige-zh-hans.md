## 角色趣闻

- Paige was one of the heroes in a private build who was accidently leaked on stream in May 2025.9]

- Paige was originally named Bookworm, and later renamed to Patience during development.

- Paige mentions real life literature such as A Midsummer Night’s Dream by William Shakespeare, Wuthering Heights by Emily Brontë, as well as referencing Arthurian Legend and Hercule Poirot, a fictional detective by author Agatha Christie.10]11]12]

- Paige's ability VFX uses text from the book Sir Gawain and the Green Knight.

- "Reading in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Paige in the Hideout.

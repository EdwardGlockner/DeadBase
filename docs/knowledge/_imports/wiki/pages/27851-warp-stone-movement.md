## Warp Dashing

When emerging from Warp Stone, you are fully actionable and (typically) in the air. You can Air Dash after a Warp Stone to create tricky movement for your opponent.

Warp Dashing costs one bar of Stamina.

Warp Dashing can be used to confuse your opponent or turn corners. This can look to observers as if you have disappeared entirely.

Since Warp Stone kills all prior velocity, you can air dash the full distance without it fighting against prior velocity.

To execute, use Warp Stone and after you emerge, Air Dash in your chosen direction.

## Warp Dash Jump

Dashes open a window to Dash Jump. You do not have to be grounded or even still dashing after the initial press to dash jump.

The Jump part of a dash jump sets your velocity to a set amount (810 u/s). Normally, it is irrelevant, since it’s just the speed a regular grounded dash goes.

When dashing then using Warp Stone, the window for dash jumping is still available. The resulting dash jump is a normal dash jump, and has the normal amount of speed, but happens after Warp Stone.

The direction that you dash in before warping is the direction of the dash jump; this allows you to create tricker movement.

This allows you to maintain speed after Warp Stone. Warp Dash Jump is particularly good for disengaging and escaping.

To execute, dash forward and use Warp Stone. After the warp, jump in the dash jump window. You should do a regular dash jump afterwards.

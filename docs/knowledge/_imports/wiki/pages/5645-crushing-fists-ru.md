Crushing Fists is a Tier 4 Оружие Item that increases Heavy Melee Distance, Melee Damage, and Bullet Resist, while also carrying over the Bonus Heavy Melee Damage passive from Удар с зарядом.

Melee damage will restore ammo and builds up Stacks which weaken the target's Bullet Resist. Once maximum stacks on a target is reached, they will be Stunned.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400. Its Component, Удар с зарядом, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

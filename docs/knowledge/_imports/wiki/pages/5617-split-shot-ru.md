Split Shot is a Tier 2 Weapon Item with a multi-shot active effect. Activating this item will enable a 5-shot multishot from your weapon for a short time, adding a stacking weapon damage buff if multiple enemy heroes are hit in a single attack.

Targets may only be hit once per multishot.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

## Notes

- Multishots can hit units as well, but they do not grant stacks.

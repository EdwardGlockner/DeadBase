Counterspell is a Tier 3 活力 Item that lets the player parry the effect of enemy abilities and items.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

## Notes

- Counterspell prevents all damage from a Walker stomp, but does not prevent the stun.

- Counterspell can prevent 劳什's 死亡猛击 if the player parries at the moment they would be pulled.

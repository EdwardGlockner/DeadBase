A lot of people spend their whole lives wondering who they really are. For Victor, this existential question is made more complicated as he woke up on a mortuary table with no knowledge of his creator, why he was made, and whom he is made of. Now searching the five boroughs for answers, Victor is hoping to find someone who can identify any part of him.

Now, Victor has travelled a long way to New York in order to find answers there.7]

Victor is in constant chronic pain,3] with the only relief being from The Cube.8] His body contains embalming fluid.9] It's implied that Victor's physical condition is deteriorating, and his left arm keeps falling off.10]5]

Victor has heterochromia, likely due to his eyes belonging to different people.

### Personality

Victor is brooding and has existential dread.11]12] He thinks of himself as a "grotesque mirror of humanity".13] Victor says he's working on being less moody,14] but brushes off Kelvin's advice to not squander his unnatural life.13] He's often seen by the other heroes as sad or pitiable.15]

Despite being in constant pain,3] Victor is a grounded and logical person.16]17]18] He doesn't enjoy hurting people in the ritual, but believes he has no choice if it means getting answers from the Patrons.19]20]21]

Victor's willing to do anything for answers on his origins,22] and feels the need to honor the people that his body parts come from.5] Victor doesn't know what he's going to do once he's found answers as he hasn't given it any thought.23]

Victor feels like he doesn't belong in his own body, as he can't tell where he ends and others begin.24] He feels that some of his body parts are mismatched.25] Victor dreams when he sleeps, and he believes that his dreams belong to him rather than his body parts.26]

### Appearance

Victor is a tall muscular man with long black hair, a brown left eye with a dilated pupil, a blue right eye, dark eyeliner, and stitches in his mouth corners. His stitched-up body parts are pale white and gray, with the exception of his blue right forearm and hand with elaborate tattoos. He has large scars over his torso and back, and smaller markings over his body. He wears stitched-up black pants with lowered suspenders and black boots. He has large metal bolts in his back, neck and left arm, which is also covered in barbed wire. When he dies with Shocking Reanimation, his body parts float apart and come back together. His weapon is a nail gun. He's considered attractive.27]28]29]30]

## Character Relationships

### Victor's creator

Victor wants to learn who his creator was and why they made him.

Victor isn't proud of his creator, and has a low opinion of them.31]32] He worries that he may have been built by a serial killer, and intends to stop them if so.33]34]

Victor believes he was made for a reason.35]

### Kelvin

Victor and Kelvin hoped that they could stick together, as they're both walking corpses.36]37]

Kelvin tells Victor to make the most of his unnatural life, suggesting that Victor travels, writes a book, or finds love. Victor brushes off the advice.13]

## Character Interactions

- Ivy attempts to ask Victor out to dinner.34]

- Victor often asks other characters for any leads on his origins. He asks Mo & Krill if he can search the New York sewers to see if any body parts down there match his own.38] He also asks Dynamo where Columbia College gets their cadavers for their medical program.39] Victor asks Abrams if any of his past cases included "a mad genius who lost one of his creations".40]

- Victor asks Abrams for help with uncovering his past.41]

- Victor wishes Pocket luck with their problems.42]

- Lady Geist calls Johann her son. It's unclear if this is a mistake or a lie by Lady Geist to hide her age.

- Infernus tells Victor to focus on his future instead of his past.43]

- While on the enemy team, Graves tells Victor that he should have come to her for help.46]

- Bebop, Grey Talon, Kelvin, Krill, Mirage and Vindicta wish Victor luck in uncovering his past.15]

- Seven believes Victor doesn't know a thing about "real" suffering.47]

- Yamato is offended by Victor's existence.48]49]

- Haze suggests Victor looks into working with the OSIC once he's found answers on his origins.23]

- Bebop relates to Victor and invites him to see Ms. Shelly.50]

- The Doorman has invited Victor to the Baroness Hotel.51]

Magic Carpet is a Tier 4 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

## Dismissing the carpet

Casting an ability, using an item, and attacking with a weapon are considered "actions" and immediately dismiss the carpet. Some abilities that can linger and deal damage after a hero uses Magic Carpet also count as an "action". For example, an enemy hero walking over a Grasping Hands line dismisses a flying Graves' carpet.

As a rule of thumb: A hero dealing single-instance damage will dismiss their carpet and most effects that deal Damage over Time will not. This rule is similar to damage that knocks heroes off of Ziplines but without damage from player-created Units.

The following abilities are notable exceptions:

- Mirage: Djinn's Mark

- Graves' Jar of Dead, Grasping Hands (Ghouls), and Borrowed Decree

- Rem: Lil Helpers (Followed Troopers)

- McGinnis: Mini Turrets

- Abrams: Siphon Life

- Lady Geist: Life Drain

- Paige: Bookwyrm (Trail)

- Paradox: Pulse Grenade

- Seven: Lightning Ball

- Victor: Aura of Suffering

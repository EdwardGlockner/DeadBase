### 卡牌生成规则

灵魅的卡牌戏法技能通过武器命中生成卡牌（而非随时间充能）。充能效率取决于攻击是否命中敌人，但实际造成的伤害量不会影响充能数值。冷却缩减会按比例提升充能效率（例：10%冷却缩减=充能效率+10%）。

注意：灵魅的卡牌生成速率初始为106%。1]

## 角色互动

- Wraith hosts weekly poker games. 亚伯兰, 炽焱 and 莫克双雄 are regular players.4]5]6]

- Wraith and 炽焱 have worked together in the past.7]

- 莫克双雄 owe money to Wraith.8] If they're on the same team, Wraith may say she called a favor to have them fighting on her side. They regard each other as friends.4]9]

- 青藤 is distrustful of Wraith. She warns her not to approach Spanish Harlem now that the Mendoza Syndicate has been defeated.4]

- 哈雷黛 warns Wraith that Captain Murphy wants to shut down her gambling ring.4]

- 劳什 mocks Wraith for betting on 比波普,10] and Wraith teases Lash because he lost to Bebop "one time".4]

- 奇能 warns Wraith not to take advantage of his students who frequent her establishment.4]

- 盖斯特夫人 says Wraith "reeks of new money". However, she respects Wraith for her cunning and ruthlessness.4]

- Calico wants to be friends with 灵魅.11]12]

## 角色趣闻

- Wraith's current character model doesn't actually have hands. However, there is a work-in-progress Wraith model in the files that has fully modeled arms and hands, as well as new animations performed with both her real arms and magic arms.

- Wraith is currently the only hero whose Sprint Speed is directly affected by Spirit Power.

- Wraith has had 4 iterations of different designs and voice actors since her creation.13] One of her earlier designs was a magician. That model was reused for the Metal Skin poster and for Sinclair's character model.

- Wraith's weapon was originally called Houdini's Shackles.

- Wraith's ammo clip size is 52 as a reference to the number of cards in a poker deck. Her original clip size was 50. It was changed based on a player's suggestion in the official Deadlock Discord.14]

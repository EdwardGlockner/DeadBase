Health Regen is a stat that heroes start with and can obtain more through items. Per second, heroes will regenerate their amount of health regeneration as health. It must not be confused with healing, which refers to the ability of helping both the player and other players regain health.

### Out of Combat Health Regen

Out of combat health regen begins 0.5 seconds after damaging/being damaged by troopers or denizens, and 4 seconds after damaging/being damaged by heroes.

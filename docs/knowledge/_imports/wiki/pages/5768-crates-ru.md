Reference extract:

Breakables are objects scattered around the map that, when broken, have a chance to drop Unsecured Souls, or in the case of a Golden Statue, a minor permanent buff. They include boxes, crates and urns. Breakables can be broken by shooting, punching, using certain abilities, dashing, or sliding through them.

Basic crates/breakables spawn after 2 in-game minutes, and respawn 3 minutes after being broken. Souls provided from crates are Unsecured Souls.

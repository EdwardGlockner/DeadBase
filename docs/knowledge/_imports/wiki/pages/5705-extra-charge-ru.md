Extra Charge is a Tier 1 Спиритизм Item that provides a bonus charge to any Charged Ability such as Тень's Карточный трюк, Плющик's Kudzu Bomb, or Семь's Шаровая молния, as well as bonus Spirit Power for any charged ability.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

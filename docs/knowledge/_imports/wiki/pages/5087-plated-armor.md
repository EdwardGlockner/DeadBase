Plated Armor is a Tier 4 Vitality Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

Blocked by both Deflection and On-Hit Prevention:

- Spreadshot weapons ( ): Triggers at once for all pellets. All pellets are deflected as though they are a single shot.

- Burst weapons ( ): Applied per bullet.

- Celeste: When deflected, an extra projectile is created. The original projectile can ricochet, as normal, and a new projectile is shot out in a random direction. The new projectile also ricochets.

- Graves: When deflected, it does not ricochet, although a visual bug might appear where a bullet projectile is created. This bullet cannot hit enemies.

- Paige: When deflected, an extra projectile is created. The original projectile pierces, as normal, and a new projectile is shot out in a random direction.

### Abilities

Effects blocked by both Deflection and On-Hit Prevention:

- Billy: Blasted (extra bullet damage from Wrecked status)

- Celeste: Light Eater (on-hit spirit damage)

- Graves: Essence Theft

- Haze: Fixation

- Holliday: Crackshot (cooldown is not applied when blocked)

- Infernus: Afterburn

- Mina: Love Bites (both buildup and spirit damage)

- Seven: Power Surge (the chaining damage is blocked per bullet. The opportunity to apply is not consumed when blocked)

- Venator: Ira Domini (blessed stakes are not affected)

- Victor: Shocking Reanimation (T1 extra damage)

- Vyper: Lethal Venom (T3 buildup)

- Wraith: Card Trick (buildup blocked by Deflection)

Effects that are not blocked:

- Grey Talon - Rain of Arrows triggers at once for all arrows. On-hit prevention does not affect bonus damage or T1 slow.

- Mirage - Djinn's Mark

- Paradox - Kinetic Carbine

- Silver - Slam Fire bonus current health damage.

- Sinclair - Spectral Assistant

- Venator - Consecrating Grenade and Gutshot deal weapon damage but cannot be deflected.

- Vindicta - Flight on-hit spirit damage.

- Wraith - Full Auto on-hit spirit damage.

### Items

Effects blocked by both Deflection and On-Hit Prevention:

- Point Blank: Slowing debuff

- Ricochet: When blocked, the bullet will never chain, or will be prevented from chaining to any more targets.

- Siphon Bullets: Even when blocked, Siphon Bullets will still go on cooldown.

- Spirit Shredder Bullets / Spirit Rend

- Stalker: Will not go on cooldown when blocked.

- Slowing Bullets / Toxic Bullets / Silencer / Inhibitor / Spiritual Overflow: Buildup

Effects partially blocked:

- Headshot Booster / Headhunter: Blocked by On-Hit Prevention. Always go on cooldown, even when blocked.

- Lucky Shot: Blocked by Deflection.

- Split Shot: Blocked by Deflection.

Effects that are not blocked:

- Armor Piercing Rounds counters Plated Armor, causing bullets to hit and apply their effects. Since both items are chance based, Plated Armor may still block bullets when Armor Piercing Rounds does not trigger.

- Burst Fire

- Crippling Headshot / Weakening Headshot

- Escalating Resilience

- Hollow Point

- Mercurial Magnum

- Mystic Shot

- Restorative Shot

- Tesla Bullets / Capacitor

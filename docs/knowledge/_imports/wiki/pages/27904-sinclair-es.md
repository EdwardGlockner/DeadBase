Sinclair son Heroes en Deadlock. Con su fiel varita magica, pueden disparar impredecibles rayos para sorprender a sus enemigos y utilizar magia para convertirlos en conejos. La Ayudante Espectral potencia su daño y puede servir como un rápido medio de escape. Sinclair incluso puede volver las habilidades de sus enemigos contra ellos con su sorprendente truco final, Participación del Público.

Henry y Savannah eran dos jóvenes ilusionistas enamorados, que enlazaron sus almas para la eternidad. Sin embargo, su relación se vio afectada luego de un trágico incidente en el escenario que acabó con la vida de ambos. Ahora, bajo el nombre compartido de Sinclair, buscan la forma de separarse y encontrar paz en el más allá.

Sinclair fue añadido a Hero Labs (como Mago) en Octubre 24, 2024, y oficialmente lanzado en Enero 17, 2025 (como EL MAGNÍFICO SINCLAIR).

## Background

### Lore

Sinclair has two different voices, Henry and Savannah. The character is initially controlled by Henry, then changes to Savannah once Henry dies in-game, and changes back to Henry when Savannah dies. The character who isn't controlling Sinclair plays the Spectral Assistant (though the Assistant currently only has a female model).

Both Henry and Savannah have to agree on who's controlling the body before the ritual.3]

The body in question is implied to be a wooden mannequin or automaton, rather than an actual organic body that Sinclair is possessing, since their footsteps sound like clanging wood and mechanical whirring while walking or using certain abilities.

Sinclair's stage names are Henry the Magnificent and The Spectacular Savannah.4]5]

Both Henry and Savannah believe they are "the responsible one".6]7]

"Sinclair" is implied to be Henry and Savannah's surname.8]

## Character interactions

- Various heroes complain about being turned into a rabbit by Sinclair.9]

- Krill says Sinclair's stage show was "incredible".10]

- Siete says he prefers his fate to Sinclair's.11]

- Celeste thinks both Sinclairs deserve each other.12]

## Trivia

- The weapon was previously named Legerdemain and Magic Wand.

- Sinclair's current design is based on one of the scrapped designs for Wraith. It is also used in a Metal Skin advert.

- Sinclair's lead developer is Hopoo,13] best known as the co-creator of the Risk of Rain series.

- The Spectral Assistant currently uses a modified version of Lady Geist's original model prior to the Six Heroes update.

- Sinclair's bunny uses the same model as Arnabus the Fairy Rabbit from Dota 2.

- Savannah's voice actor also plays the role of Allie Kinkaid in Holliday's visual novel.

- "Working Magic in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Sinclair in the Hideout.

- When copying Yamato's Shadow Transformation, the description shows "Become infused with Sinclair's shadow soul." instead of 'Become infused with Yamato's shadow soul."

Spirit Snatch is a Tier 3 元灵 Item that grants improved Melee Damage and Bonus Health.

With this item, performing a Light or Heavy Melee attack on an enemy hero will deal extra Spirit Damage and temporarily steal Spirit Resist and Spirit Power from them.

Hitting multiple heroes in one swing will steal from all struck heroes.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200, with its Component, 大伤元气, making up 800 of the cost.

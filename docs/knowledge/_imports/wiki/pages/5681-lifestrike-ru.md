Lifestrike is a Tier 3 Живучесть Item that makes your Melee attacks heal a significant amount of health on hit, as well as slowing the enemy. Lifestrike also grants increased Melee Damage and Bonus Health.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200, with its Component, Ударная кража здоровья, making up 800 of the cost.

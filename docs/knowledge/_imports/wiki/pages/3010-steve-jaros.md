Steve Jaros is a Deadlock developer. He is best known for his work as a writer on the Saints Row series, as well as on Dota 2 and Artifact.1]2]

Steve is Deadlock's lead writer.3]4]

## Trivia

- Deadlock also shares a number of voice actors with the Saints Row games, such as Andrew Bowen ( Lash), Danielle Nicolet ( Wraith), Eden Riegel ( Vindicta), Phil Morris ( Infernus), Yuri Lowenthal ( Viscous), and Jeannie Tirado ( Ivy).

- Steve's bio on Valve's website only says that he's a "lover of pro wrestling". This may have inspired the character of Lash. Abrams' voice actor Samoa Joe is a pro wrestler.

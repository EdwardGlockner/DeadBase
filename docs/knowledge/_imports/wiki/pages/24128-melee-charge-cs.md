Melee Charge is a Tier 2 Zbraň Item that grants additional Melee Damage, Heavy Melee Distance, and Bullet Resist. Its passive effect causes Heavy Melee attacks to deal extra damage every 5s.

Melee Charge is a Tier 2 Zbraň Předmět that can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

It is a component of Drtivé rukavice.

## Notes

- The bonus damage will only occur if the item activates. If it is on cooldown, the player will not gain the +25% Bonus Heavy Melee Damage.

## Notable Interactions

- Billy' Fláknutí T3 triggers Melee Charge's effect. However, Bashdown only benefits from the passive melee damage bonus, and does not trigger the 25% bonus damage.

- Viscous' Zelená pěst T3 is bugged, and does not gain 25% bonus damage from Melee Charge's effect, despite putting the passive on cooldown.

- Yamato benefits from Melee Charge due to her above-average melee damage.

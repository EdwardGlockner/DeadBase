蝰邪是一位 Deadlock 中的英雄。她在战场上高速穿梭，精准打击关键目标。蝰邪可通过滑行与闪避提升机动性，同时投掷缓速螺旋刃与注入致命毒液。当甩出石化飞索时，不仅能对群体造成减速与伤害，还会将不幸的目标彻底石化。

蝰邪是一个冲动行事的逃犯，她每次被逮捕都能成功越狱，因此在 New Jersey Tri-State Area 结识了一大帮亡命之徒（同时也是她的敌人）。

蝰邪在 October 24, 2024 被添加进 Hero Labs，此期间名为“蝰蛇”，而后于 January 17, 2025 正式推出。

## 背景故事

### Lore

Vyper has been to New Jersey,1] Staten Island,2] Philadelphia,3] Illinois and Wisconsin.4]

Vyper makes references to pruno, an improvised alcoholic beverage also known as "prison wine".5]6]

## 角色互动

- Vyper thinks 劳什 is "awesome".7] When killing Lash, she may say she loves him and ask for an autograph.8]9]

- Vyper dislikes 比波普, Lash's pitfighting rival.10]

- Vyper is a friend of 莫克双雄.11]12]

- Vyper believes 炽焱 "used to be hard, but now he's just a bitch".13]

- Vyper says her Screwjab Daggers are better than 希弗's Serrated Knives.14]

- Vyper says 大和 murdered a guy for owing the Seventh Moon a "C-Note" ($100).15]

- Vyper is barred from taking out another "marker" (slang for an informal loan) from 灵魅's gambling ring, presumably because she still owes money to Wraith.16]

- Vyper says 卡厉可 has a reputation of "sloppy work" due to "a hit that went bad", a possible reference to the attempted assassination of 口袋.4]

- Vyper comments on 灰爪's health, due to his age.4]

- Vyper wants 蜃景 to "stop yelling about the Djinn".17]

- Vyper has been kicked out of the 悖论 museum.18]

- Vyper's current appearance is a modified version of Neon Prime's Kali.

- Vyper's lead developer is Hopoo,19] best known as the co-creator of the Risk of Rain series.

- Vyper hides her legs in her slide animation, making her look like a snake.

- Vyper is the only hero who starts with an unlocked ability by default.

Diviner's Kevlar is a Tier 4 活力 Item that provides Ability Duration to the purchasing hero.

Diviner's Kevlar has a passive that, upon casting an ultimate ability, the purchasing hero gains a temporary Barrier and Spirit Power.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

## Notes

- 亚伯兰' Siphon Life & Shoulder Charge will use the current spirit power when the ability does damage

- 比波普's bombs will benefit from the bonus spirit power even if Diviner's Kevlar expires before the bomb explodes, as long as the bomb was applied prior to it expiring.

- 炽焱's Flame Dash will also reflect the current spirit power bonus when the ability does damage

- 麦金妮's turrets that are spawned while the +40 spirit power is active will keep this buff for the whole turret duration

- 薇妲's flight duration scales off of spirit; if she uses her ultimate while flying, the duration of the flight will get extended, however the 'bonus spirit damage' portion of her flight will terminate unexpectedly early.

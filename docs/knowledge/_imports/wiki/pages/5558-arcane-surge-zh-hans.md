Arcane Surge is a Tier 2 元灵 Item that grants Stamina and Stamina Recovery. Its passive effect causes a hero's next Dash Jump to grant extra Ability Range, Duration, and Spirit Power to the next ability used.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600, with its Component, 耐力补给, making up 800 of the cost.

## Notes

- Items and passive abilities cannot benefit from the conditional buff - they will not gain additional spirit power, duration, or range.

- The buff which provides the player with the conditional ability range, ability duration, and spirit power lasts for 7 seconds.

- The first ability cast after gaining the buff will consume the buff.

- The conditional ability range and ability duration are snapshot on ability use.

- The conditional spirit power is tied to the 7 second timer of the conditional buff. The +15 spirit power will only be available for 7 seconds after the player initially gains the conditional buff.

Examples:

- This consumes the buff, and applies +15 spirit power to 狙击's damage.

- The buff is consumed.

- The 球状闪电 gains +20% ability duration and +20% ability range.

- The 球状闪电 gains +15 spirit power for 1 second only. For the rest of its duration, it does not benefit from +15 spirit power.

- The buff is consumed.

- 澎湃电力 gains +20% ability duration and +20% ability range.

- 澎湃电力's damage benefits from +15 spirit power for 1 second only. For the rest of its duration, the damage does not benefit from +15 spirit power.

- 澎湃电力's total duration scales natively with spirit power at time of cast. The additional duration due to the +15 spirit power is snapshot on cast, and is not lost when the 7s conditional buff is consumed.

Cadence is an unreleased Hero in Deadlock. She is an Ixian woman and the lead singer of the jazz band Cadence and the Requiem Boys.

## Abilities

- Anthem

Burst into an inspiring song! You and any ally that comes within range get an instant reload with an extra large clip, and have a Fire Rate bonus that increases until the song ends.

- Silence Contraptions

Disarm yourself perform a long-range dash melee attack. For the duration of the song, you remain disarmed and your melee abilities apply a debuff that disarms and EMP's enemy targets.

- Lullaby

Lull your foes to sleep! Any enemy that comes in range of Cadence sleeps until woken by damage, Cadence goes out of range, or the song ends. Cadence has bonus regeneration while singing.

- Crescendo

Encase nearby enemies in glass in an increasing range over a short duration. Cadence then breaks the glass with her powerful voice, breaking any shields on the them, damaging them, and giving them massive bullet vulnerability for a duraiton after.

## Backstory

### Lore

Cadence is the lead singer of the jazz band Cadence and the Requiem Boys. She sings the hit song "Paying the Price".1] She is a side character in Ivy's visual novel.

Cadence is Ixian.2]

## Trivia

- The viking helmet's model appears to be a combination of the meshes for the Team Fortress 2 items Tyrant's Helm and Viking Braider, with the latter edited to resemble braided hair behind the head.

- Cadence's placeholder minimap icon uses an image of the Neon Prime version of Haze.

- Two work-in-progress images of Cadence in Ivy's visual novel, with a new Ixian design, were leaked on a livestream in February 2025.

- At one point, Lullaby's music was changed to the Main Menu music for Neon Prime with Anthem receiving the former's old song.

- Cadence and The Requiem Boys are promoted in a theater in the Cursed Apple.

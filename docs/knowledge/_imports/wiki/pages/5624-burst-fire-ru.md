Burst Fire is a Tier 3 Оружие Item that grants increased Slide Distance. Its passive effect causes the user to briefly gain additional Fire Rate and Movement Speed whenever one of their bullets hits an enemy hero.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200, with its Component, Спешная стрельба, making up 800 of the cost.

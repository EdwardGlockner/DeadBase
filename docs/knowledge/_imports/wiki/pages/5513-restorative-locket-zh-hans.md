Restorative Locket is a Tier 2 Vitality Item that provides Spirit Resist to the purchasing hero.

The item gains a Restoration Stack whenever an enemy uses an ability within range. Items do not trigger this passive effect.

The ability consumes all stacks (minimum 1) to heal target ally and can heal a maximum of 400 HP (plus Spirit Scaling) as well as always restoring 2 stamina points.

It can be purchased for 1,600 from the Shop.

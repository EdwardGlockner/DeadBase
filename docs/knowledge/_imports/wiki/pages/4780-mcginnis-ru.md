McGinnis is a Hero in Deadlock. She wields a machine gun and her abilities revolve around the usage of structures like turrets and walls, which she can create to support allies and hold down positions to the benefit of her team. If the situation goes awry, she can launch a powerful barrage of rockets at her enemies.

A talented engineer who started her career from a young age, McGinnis isn't fond of theoretical work, thus spending most of her time working in a mechanics shop, often meddling with the supernatural.

## Weapon

### Notes

- Services Rendered takes 2 seconds to spin up, increasing McGinnis' fire rate and bullet spread, and slowly winds down at 25% per second (4 seconds total at max spin) as indicated by the grey indicator around the crosshair.

- The DPS and Bullets per second stats listed in game use the max spin up values.

- Services Rendered's base fire rate starts at 5 bullets per second (Cycle time: 0.2) and spins up to 12.5 bullets per second (Cycle time: 0.08), averaging around 11 Bullets per second per clip at the beginning of a match.

## Background

### Lore

McGinnis' first name is Margaret, but she goes by Maggie.1]

McGinnis is described as a "notorious misanthrope".2]

Maximilian Fairfax "paraded McGinnis incessantly" to the New York elite when he first discovered her as a genius teenage inventor.3]

McGinnis was worried that she would lose her job when Fairfax lost the sovereignty of the Island of Cadenza to the US Government.4]5]

McGinnis likes to drink beer.3]

## Character interactions

- McGinnis built Динамо's body.6]

- Карман criticizes McGinnis for working for Fairfax Industries.7]8]

- In The Binding of Oathkeeper, one of the story paths has McGinnis building a new ward for Леди Гайст.

- McGinnis accuses Калико of stealing her ideas.9]

- McGinnis wants to talk to Заточка and "fix him".10]11]

- McGinnis is "not a fan" of Cadence.12]

- McGinnis may say she wants to get Вязкус' goo after killing him.13]

- McGinnis doesn't frequent Инфернус' bar, Jezebel's, because she finds it "too fancy".6]

- McGinnis offers help to remove Семь's hat.6]

- In Neon Prime, McGinnis was named Forge. She had a relationship with Astro (Holliday).14]15] She trained fighting with Lash in his dojo.16]17]

- McGinnis and Bebop are currently tied for the highest base ammo count at 66.

- In The Binding of Oathkeeper artwork, McGinnis has a mermaid tattoo in her left shoulder.

- McGinnis' Heavy Barrage sound effects were changed to sound less like a real air raid siren.

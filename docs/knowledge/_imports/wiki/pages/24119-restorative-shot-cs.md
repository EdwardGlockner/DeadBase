Restorative Shot is a Tier 1 Zbraň Item that grants additional Weapon Damage. Its passive causes a hero's next bullet to heal them for a flat amount every few seconds. The amount of health restored is dependent on whether the bullet hits an enemy Hero or NPC.

Restorative Shot is a Tier 1 Zbraň Předmět that can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

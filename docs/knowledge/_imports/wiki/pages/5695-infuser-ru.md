Infuser is a Tier 4 Живучесть Item which grants Spirit Lifesteal, Spirit Resist and Bonus Health to the purchasing hero.

Infuser has an Active component, which will grant the hero additional Spirit Power and Spirit Lifesteal for a limited duration.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400, with its Component, Спиритическая кража здоровья, making up 1,600 of the cost.

The Rejuvenator is a single rune that spawns above the Mid-Boss pit after it is slain. It appears as a large, green crystal. After the Mid-Boss is killed, the Rejuvenator descends over 6 seconds and cannot be claimed until it fully touches the ground. Once it lands, any player in the match can hit it with a heavy melee attack, granting the Rejuvenation Credit buff to their entire team. Each heavy melee hit grants one Rejuvenator Credit to the team, up to 3. Hitting the Rejuvenator also heals the first player who punched it to full health.

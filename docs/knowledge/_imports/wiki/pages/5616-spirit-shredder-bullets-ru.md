Spirit Shredder Bullets is a Tier 2 Оружие Item that applies a Spirit Resist debuff against targets and increases the user's and their allies spirit lifesteal against the targets hit. It was previously named Soul Shredder Bullets.

Spirit Shredder Bullets is a component of Спиритический разрыв.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

## Notes

- The Spirit Lifesteal from the debuff is independent of the diminishing returns from other sources of spirit lifesteal. It will always grant the player a flat +8% spirit lifesteal.

- The debuff can be applied with melee attacks, as well as abilities that are considered melee attacks.

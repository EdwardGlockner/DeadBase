Colossus is a Tier 4 活力 Item that provides Bonus Health and Weapon Damage to the purchasing hero.

Colossus has an Active component which will grant the hero using it additional Bullet Resist, Spirit Resist, an aura which slows the movement speed and dash speed of nearby enemies, and will increase the model size of the hero by 20%. This effect lasts for 7 seconds.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400, with its Component, 额外生命, making up 800 of the cost.

## Notes

- While active, a teal-colored circle appears around the hero to indicate the border of the enemy movement slowing effect.

Arctic Blast is a Tier 4 元灵 Item that grants Spirit Resist and has an Active that deals Spirit Damage and freezes enemies around the player. Frozen enemies take bonus damage.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400, with its Component, 冰天雪地, making up 1,600 of the cost.

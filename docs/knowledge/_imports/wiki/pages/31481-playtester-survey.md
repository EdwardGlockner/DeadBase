The Playtester Survey is a 3 question survey that will ask the player to answer questions with a 1 to 5 rating. It appears after a game and is intended to provide quick feedback to the developers.

## Questions

| Question | Scale | 1 | 5 |

| --- | --- | --- | --- |

| How powerful is this item? | 1-5 | Too Weak | Too Strong |

| How interesting or fun is this item? | 1-5 | Needs Redesign | Very Fun |

| How fun is it to play against this hero? | 1-5 | Awful | Great |

| How fun is it to play as this hero? | 1-5 | Awful | Great |

| How powerful is this hero? | 1-5 | Weak | Powerful |

| How good of a frontliner is this hero? | 1-5 | Awful | Great |

| How good of a carry is this hero? | 1-5 | Awful | Great |

| How good is this hero at disables or crowd control? | 1-5 | Awful | Great |

| How good is this hero at non-cc utility or allied healing? | 1-5 | Awful | Great |

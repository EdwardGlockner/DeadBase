Raw Bunnyhopping is a type of bunnyhopping that only utilises jumping.

Raw Bunnyhopping does not consume any stamina.

To execute, jump as you hit the ground, making sure not to air jump. If you do not have stamina, you can buffer jumps by holding space whenever you are about to hit the ground. Raw Bunnyhopping is difficult to execute due to sharing the same input as air jumping.

Raw Bunnyhopping can be beneficial for travelling in a straight line; for moving around corners, Slide Bunnyhopping is better. Perfect Raw Bunnyhops can technically be faster than Slide Bunnyhopping only at speeds lower than 900 u/s, and with good Air Strafing.

To execute, press Jump as soon as you hit the floor.

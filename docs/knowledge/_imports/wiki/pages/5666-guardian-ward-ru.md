Guardian Ward is a Tier 2 Живучесть Item that increases the Ability Range of the purchasing hero.

Its active ability grants the user a Barrier and Movement Speed for a short duration. Can be self-cast.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

Guardian Ward is a Component of Божественный барьер.

Compress Cooldown is a Tier 2 Spirit Item which gives Cooldown Reduction to an Imbued ability of the purchasing hero. It was previously named Improved Cooldown.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

Compress Cooldown is a component of 超速冷却.

## Notes

- If Compress Cooldown is imbued on 无双魔术师's 模仿大师, it does not reduce the cooldown of copied ultimates, only the default cooldown of the ability.

Divine Barrier is a Tier 4 Живучесть Item that provides Ability Range to the purchasing hero.

Its active ability removes all debuffs (non-stun) from the target and grants them a Barrier and Movement Speed for a short duration. Can be self-cast.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400. Its Component, Заслон стража, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

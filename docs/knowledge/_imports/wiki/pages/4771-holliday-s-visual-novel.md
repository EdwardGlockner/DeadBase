## Trivia

- Holliday's post-visual novel conversations give context to where some of the story paths will go. Based on these lines, Haze will help Holliday in the OSIC path and Seven will help her in the Allie Kinkaid path.

- Mo & Krill are also expected to appear in Holliday's visual novel based on an unfinished conversation between them and Holliday, although it's unknown what role they will play in the story.

- Holliday's friend and Allie Kinkaid share their voice actors with Trapper and Savannah (Sinclair), respectively.

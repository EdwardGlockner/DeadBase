Drifter is a Hero in Deadlock. Something ancient has arrived in New York... a nameless horror known only as the Drifter. Relentless as time, as certain as death, once Drifter sets his eyes on a victim there's nothing they can do to stop him. They can try to run, but his Bloodscent drives him forward, leading him to isolated prey. Even if they manage to escape it doesn't matter, as Stalker's Mark allows Drifter to appear right behind his enemies before lunging in for the kill with a vicious Rend. Soon Eternal Night comes, and a faint whistling in the breeze will be joined by a chorus of screams.

Drifter was released on August 27, 2025 as the fifth hero of the Six New Heroes update (fourth winner of hero vote).

## Background

### Lore

Unlike the other heroes, Drifter isn't participating in the ritual for a wish, but instead for the carnage.2] He views the wish as a "bonus".3]

The Drifter has lived for centuries.4] His age is unknown, but he refers to New York as "New Amsterdam",5] which implies he's been around at least since 1665.6] He also implies that he knew Doc Holliday from the 19th century.7]

Drifter is not the Troubadour.8]9] He has never met him before, and only knows him by reputation.10]

Drifter speaks in a Cajun accent from Louisiana, indicating that he may be associated with 17th century Acadian French immigrants.11]

Drifter has been hunted by the Baxter Society, the Swords of Saint Benedict, and Silver.12]13]14] The OSIC aren't after Drifter.15]

Drifter seems to be scared of fire, and expresses relief if he doesn't have to fight Infernus.16]

Drifter likes to steal his victim's coats.17]18]

## Character interactions

- Drifter and Oathkeeper are old friends.19] Drifter can't believe Lady Geist could lock away "someone as fun as Oathkeeper".20]21]

- Drifter thinks Mina is a "pathetic excuse for a vampire".22] Mina is terrified of Drifter.23]24]

- Drifter warns Holliday to let go of her obsession with the Troubadour before the Troubadour finds out about her chasing him.25]

- Drifter and Grey Talon have a long history with each other. Drifter refers to Grey Talon by his first name "Wesley".26] While Grey Talon despises Drifter,27]28] Drifter holds Talon in high regards.29]30]31] Drifter enjoys tormenting Talon, seemingly by hurting his loved ones.32]33] There was a particularly brutal interaction between the two in Oklahoma.34] Vyper mentions that Drifter is a "legend" in Oklahoma.35]

- Drifter knew Shiv before he joined the Baxter Society.36] If he kills Shiv, he calls him a "disappointment" and mentions that killing him feels "Shakespearean".37]38]39] He also mentions that, had Shiv not "put himself in a muzzle, he'd be worried".40] When hunting Shiv, he refers to himself as an "old friend".41] How Shiv and Drifter met each other is unknown.

- Drifter has killed Venators before,13] though he had higher expectations for the playable Venator.42] If they're on the same team, Venator promises to kill Drifter after the ritual.43]

- Drifter doesn't enjoy killing Bebop because he's a machine,44] Sinclair because they're already dead,45] and Victor because he's just putting him out of his misery.46]

- Drifter has high expectations for Seven,47]48] but thinks he still has too much humanity left in him.49]

- The Doorman has met Drifter before.50]

- Paradox is annoyed that Drifter has nothing worth stealing.51]

- Drifter thinks Mirage is handsome. 52]

## Trivia

- Drifter was one of the heroes in a private build who was accidentally leaked on stream in May 2025.53]

- Drifter leaves claw marks when sliding on a wall.

- Drifter's forward air dash and wall jump animation resembles the pounce animation of the Hunter, a recurring enemy in Valve's Left 4 Dead series.

- Drifter's light melee animation before the August 29, 2025 update resembles one of The Hunter's possible claw attack animations.

- Drifter's sprinting animation following the August 29, 2025 update and prior to the September 4, 2025 update resembles The Hunter's running animation.

- "Prowling in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Drifter in the Hideout.

- During the 2025 winter event, one of three Christmas songs would be whistled instead; Joy to the World, Twelve Days of Christmas, and God Rest Ye Merry, Gentlemen.

- Drifter has drawings on his arms and hands that seem to be based on the Hobo Code.

Spirit Rend is a Tier 3 Weapon Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200. Its component, Spirit Shredder Bullets, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

- As with Spirit Shredder Bullets, the primary passive debuffs can be applied by melee attacks and abilities that deal melee damage.

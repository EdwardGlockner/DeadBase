Cultist Sacrifice is a Tier 3 Weapon Item that grants increased Weapon Damage, Bullet Resistance against NPCs, and Out of Combat Health Regen.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

## Notes

- The buff persists through death.

- Cultist Sacrifice applies additional damage to Non-Player Characters. This includes Troopers, lane structures like Guardians, Walkers, neutral entities such as Denizen, and the Patron.

- Player-created NPCs, such as Макгиннис Мини-турель, are not affected.

- Cultist Sacrifice's active counts as spirit damage. This means it can activate on-hit spirit damage procs such as Спиритический ожог.

Monster Rounds is a Tier 1 Zbraň Item that grants Out of Combat Regen and additional Weapon Damage and Bullet Resist against NPCs.

Monster Rounds is a Tier 1 Zbraň Předmět that can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

It is a component of Rituální dýka.

## Notes

- Monster Rounds applies additional damage to Non-Player Characters. This includes Troopers, lane structures like Guardians, Walkers, neutral entities such as Denizen, and the Patron.

- It does not apply additional damage against McGinnis' Přenosná střílna.

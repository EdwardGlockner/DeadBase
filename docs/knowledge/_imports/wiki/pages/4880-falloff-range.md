Reference extract:

Falloff Range is a stat that determines the minimum and maximum distance in which a Hero can deal Weapon Damage to a target.

- Bebop's weapon has a separate Max Range stat that increases with Boons, and deals no damage beyond its max range.

- Drifter's weapon deals no damage beyond its max range.

- Despite having no damage falloff, the falloff range is factored in when calculating Graves' Build-Up per bullet, most notably the buld-up from Essence Theft.

- As an exception, the effect from Ricochet has damage falloff, calculated based on the distance from the main target to the ricochet'd target.

- Silver (Transformed)'s weapon has a fixed range that cannot be increased.

Falloff range can be increased with Items.

### Explosive bullets

Alternate fire weapons with explosive bullets deal 50% damage at maximum explosion radius, multiplied by the regular damage falloff.

## Notes

- Cursed Relic does not remove the Unstoppable status, and does not remove buffs from Unstoppable targets.

- Unlike all other stuns, it does not prevent movement completely, but rather slows the target heavily akin to Sleep. Jumping and dashing is still disabled.

- Its duration is not affected by Ability Duration upgrades (e.g. Superior Duration).

- The Curse status does not disable passive effects. This means the player can still activate Counterspell, since it's a passive effect applied to parrying.

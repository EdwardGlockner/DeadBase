Rapid Recharge is a Tier 3 Spirit Item that provides Bonus Ability Charges, faster charges recharge time, and extra Spirit Power for charged abilities to the purchasing hero.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200, with its Component, 额外充能, making up 800 of the cost.

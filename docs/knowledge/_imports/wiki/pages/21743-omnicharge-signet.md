Omnicharge Signet is a Legendary Spirit Item that can only be obtained in Street Brawl.

- Abilities with build-up charges instead of cooldowns, such as Graves' Jar of Dead and Wraith's Card Trick, are treated as uncharged abilities.

- Abilities that become charged on upgrades, such as Infernus' Flame Dash and Yamato's Flying Slash, are treated as uncharged abilities, even if already upgraded.

## Known Bugs

- If imbued on Victor's Pain Battery, the player cannot use the ability.

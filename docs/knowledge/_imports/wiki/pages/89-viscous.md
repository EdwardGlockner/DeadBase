Viscous is a Hero in Deadlock. He's green.4] The Deep will be silent no longer once Viscous throws Splatter, covering the area in green goo which slows down his victims and leaves them vulnerable to Puddle Punches, which will knock his enemies away from their friends and his friends away from their enemies. Even if they manage to catch Viscous it doesn't matter, as The Cube can make people completely invulnerable long enough for them to recover. Soon Goo Ball comes, and walls painted green will be joined by echoes of screams.

Viscous is a kind, if not childish psychic sea anemone whose home in the Bermuda Triangle has been invaded by a mysterious, dangerous predator only known as the Adversary. He reached the surface in a body built from ectoplasmic goo; his mission to summon the Patrons to protect his home in the Deep.

Viscous was released on August 1, 2024.

Deep Sea Interpreter has an alternate fire mode, which releases a larger projectile that explodes in a small area of effect upon collision, or after flying for long enough. This alt-fire takes 5 ammo rounds per shot and deals 42+1.6 damage on a direct hit.The alt-fire mode can be used even with fewer than 5 rounds of ammo, and still deals its full damage.

## Trivia

- He's green.4]

- Viscous was the first new hero to be added in Deadlock after the start of the playtest.

- Viscous' head was changed from black to green in the 2024-09-26 Update, because his old icon was easily confused with Dynamo.

- A work-in-progress alternate model can be found in the files, labeled Viscous v2, in which he is a large man with a coral arm and an octopus-like creature in his head.

- A new model labeled Viscous_tourist, in which he wears shorts, sunglasses, an orange cap and a portable camera, has also been referenced in the files and leaked in images in June 2025. The current icon for Goo Ball shows the camera, glove and boot from this new model.

- In Neon Prime, Viscous was a pufferfish in a suit, instead of a sea anemone.

- "Wishing the Hideout was The Cube" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Viscous in the Hideout.

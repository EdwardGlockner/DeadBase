— The Newscaster

A Maelstrom is a celestial event that brings the supernatural to the mortal world.1]

50 years later, in the present day, a second Maelstrom is about to occur. This time, the world is aware that it's coming, and some cults have prepared a ritual to summon the Patrons during the celestial event. It's implied that summoning the Patrons outside of the Maelstrom is impossible.6]

The duration of the Maelstrom is expected to last between 20 minutes to 2 hours,7] during which mystic energy is at its apex.8]

The OSIC has warned New York citizens to stay indoors for the duration of the Maelstrom.9]10]

The third Maelstrom isn't expected to happen for another 50 years,11]12] but it could happen earlier.13]

## Ritual

The ritual is an event that takes place during the second Maelstrom, in which two teams of six (or more) individuals are fighting to summon a Patron,14]10] an extraplanar elder god,15]11] who can grant them a wish. The ritual can only be completed once the enemy team's Patron is destroyed.

Fonts of power all over New York City are being tapped as part of the ritual.16] The New York Oracle is providing live coverage of the ritual.17]

Haze has prepared extensively for the ritual by performing drills, research, stretching, divination and fortune telling.18] Warden has trained his whole life for the ritual.19]

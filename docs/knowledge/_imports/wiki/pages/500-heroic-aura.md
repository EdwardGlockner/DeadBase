Heroic Aura is a Tier 3 Weapon Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

## Notable Interactions

The active effect is applied to hero summons such as McGinnis' Mini Turrets and Graves' Deadheads and Ghouls. For Deadheads, it increases their attack rate.

Reference extract:

Gravity Scale is a Stat that modifies how gravity acceleration affects the player.

Lower gravity scale makes a hero lighter and "floatier", and higher gravity scale makes them heavier.

Air Control and Air Acceleration are stats connected to (but not directly affected by) Gravity Scale. These stats affect how much the player can control their aerial movement.

## Heroes with gravity scale modifiers

### Base Gravity Scale Stats

| Hero | Gravity Scale (%) |

| --- | --- |

| Celeste | -25 |

| Ivy | -10 |

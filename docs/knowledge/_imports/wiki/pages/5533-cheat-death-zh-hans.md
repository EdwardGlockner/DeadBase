Cheat Death is a Tier 4 活力 Item that grants the hero bonus Health and Spirit Resist.

It has a passive that makes the player immune to death for 4s when they would take lethal damage (health reduced to 0), but also makes them weaker while the effect is active.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

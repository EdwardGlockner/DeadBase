Tesla Bullets is a Tier 3 Weapon Item with a passive that grants a chance for any bullets fired to shock their target, causing the damage to chain to a nearby enemy.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

Тесла-пули is a component of Конденсатор.

- Tesla Bullets is one of several items to feature in promotional posters around the Map.

- Tesla Bullets is named after Serbian-American inventor Nikola Tesla.

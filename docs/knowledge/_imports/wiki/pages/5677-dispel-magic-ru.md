Debuff Remover is a Tier Item Not Found. [[Item Not Found.]] item that reduces the duration of all negative effects applied to you, and has an active ability that purges all non-ultimate negative effects currently applied to you, granting bonus Movement Speed if any were removed.

It also provides Bonus Health to the purchasing hero.

It can be purchased from the Shop for Cannot format non-number value. (its Component, Уменьшитель эффектов, makes up 1,600 of the cost).

Healing Tempo is a Tier 4 Vitality Item that provides Healing Effectiveness, Spirit Resist, and extra Health Regen. Its passive grants bonus Fire Rate and Move Speed whenever you heal yourself or an ally.

## Notes

The bonus fire rate applies to allied 麦金妮' 迷你炮塔s, but not allied Troopers.

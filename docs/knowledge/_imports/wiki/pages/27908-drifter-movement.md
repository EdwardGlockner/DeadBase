## Stalker's Mark Teleport Momentum Conservation

Stalker's Mark is Drifter’s second ability. Using it sends a mark, which upon hitting a valid target allows you to teleport to said target at any time during the duration of Stalker's Mark.

To preserve air dash momentum, cast Stalker's Mark, air dash, then ambush while still holding air dash momentum. The air dash momentum you had before the teleport will be carried with you.

Reference extract:

He's proper, he's polite, but the humble Doorman of The Baroness Hotel is more than meets the eye. While he had only planned to experience life on this plane for a few weeks, The Doorman quickly discovered that the New York service industry was a great way to see the strange demands that please humans.

The entity known as "The Doorman" is an ancient extraplanar fledgling god and cosmic being5]6]7] who used to slaughter and torment humans a long time ago, feeling glee at their fear and misery.8]9]10]

At some point, the fledgling god decided to experience life on Earth disguised as a human for a few weeks, and somehow became involved with the Baroness Hotel in New York as soon as it opened.8] He adopted the title of "The Doorman" as his name to stay grounded in his new role,11] and enjoyed it so much that he has stayed at the Baroness ever since.

The Doorman is capable of warping reality and bending space and time.12]13]14] He claims to be able to see the future, although it's unknown if he's bluffing about it.15] It's implied that the Doorman weakened himself to live amongst humanity.16]17]

The Doorman once required the Baxter Society's intervention at the Baroness.18]

The Doorman does not work at the Baroness alone, having coworkers such as "Jacques"19] and "Francis".20]

### Personality

The Doorman appears polite and respectful when talking to the other heroes, even if they are rude to him.21]22] However, he has supernatural knowledge about them, and uses this to torment his enemies while they're trapped in the Baroness, or to convince them to stay and forget about the ritual.23] During the ritual, he may fall back on his sadistic old ways when on a killstreak.9]10]

The Doorman likes working at the Baroness since he gets to observe humanity.24] He has a very dedicated work ethic, and finds that serving humans currently has a "novel charm" compared to his past experience of tormenting them.8] He does not discriminate against any guests of the Baroness,25] although he will kick people out if they cause a disturbance or break any rules.26]27]

The Doorman often refers to the other heroes as "mortals", and believes they are weak and childish compared to him.28]29]30] He looks forward to helping his allies "enrich their meaningless lives".31]

Most heroes don't know the true nature of the Doorman and are surprised by his exceptional powers.32] The OSIC seem to be aware of the Doorman's true nature, as they consider the Doorman participating in the ritual as "overstepping".33] The Doorman occasionally teases his true nature in conversations with his allies, but doesn't linger on it.8]

### Appearance

The Doorman appears as a slender young white man with somewhat long limbs, broad shoulders, glowing blue eyes that lack irises, perfectly symmetrical red hair with the exception of a front curl, and perfectly symmetrical freckles on his cheeks. He wears a classic bellhop uniform with a dark red jacket and round hat with gold and black accents, black pants with a golden stripe on the sides, black shoes, and white gloves. His jacket has a keyhole-shaped pin on his chest and keyhole-shaped buttons. He carries a glowing golden keyring behind his left hip.

His weapon is a bespoke handgun attached to another keyring, that shoots key-shaped projectiles, and floats as a golden halo behind his head when he uses Hotel Guest. He reloads his gun by throwing it in a small door portal, and then grabbing it as it exits through another door portal over his hand.

The Doorman's true appearance is unknown.

### Information

The Doorman knows about Oathkeeper and the Troubadour.34]35]

## Character Relationships

### The Patrons

The Doorman calls the Patrons "old friends" and refers to them as City Mother and Amber King.36]37]

Both Patrons are seemingly familiar with the Doorman, with their heralds expressing surprise and joy at seeing him fighting on their behalf.38]39] They respect the Doorman and view his murderous side as "who he was meant to be".40]

### Mirage

Mirage and Nashala Dion are guests at the Baroness during their stay in New York. When on enemy teams, the Doorman feels sorry for killing Mirage,41]42] and Mirage is upset for having to leave the Baroness.43]

### Calico

The Doorman is familiar with Calico and likes her.44]45] He talks with her about "professional interests in town", likely meaning that Calico told him about her job as an assassin.46]

The Doorman loves Ava and, in the event that Calico will be unable to take care of Ava anymore, says he'll treat her "like a queen" at the Baroness Hotel.47]48]

### Wraith

The Doorman thinks Wraith is a "delight" and is grateful towards her since she's a generous tipper.49]50] He calls her a friend.51]

### Lady Geist

The Doorman thinks Lady Geist is rude and doesn't expect any tips from her.52]21] Lady Geist believes that the Baroness has lost its standards.53]

### Apollo

Apollo has stayed at the Baroness Hotel in the past.54] He likes The Doorman and tipped him "last week".55]

While the conversation is unfinished, The Doorman appears to give his best wishes to Apollo's father.56]

## Character Interactions

- The Doorman doesn't seem to know what Warden wants,57] which could mean he's telepathic like the Patrons as opposed to being omniscient, since they can't read Warden's desires either.58] The Doorman does seem familiar with Warden's parents and derides them as failures.59]60]

- The Doorman respects Infernus since they both work in the service industry and is apologetic when killing him.61]62]

- The Doorman is actively keeping Paradox out of the Baroness.63]

- The Doorman has met Drifter before.64]

- The Doorman has kicked Silver and Vyper out of the Baroness.26]27]

- The Doorman likes McGinnis' inventions and calls her "the brightest mind humanity has to offer".65]66]

- The Doorman likes Paige's taste in books and invites her to host a storytelling event at the Baroness Hotel.67]68]

- When killing the Doorman, Krill reassures Mo that he'd never betray him.70]

- Mina and Lady Geist don't like talking to The Doorman since they view him as just "the help".71]22] However, Mina knows that Doorman is a "cosmic being".7]

- Ivy has had brunches at the Baroness before and is apologetic when fighting the Doorman.72]73]

- Venator recognises that Doorman is more ancient and powerful than he lets on.5]74]

- Seven knows that Doorman isn't normal and wants to uncover his secrets.75]76] The Doorman thinks Seven is far from becoming the god that he wishes to be,77] and believes he's more delusional than Lash.78]

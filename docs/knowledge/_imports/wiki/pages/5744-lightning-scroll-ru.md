Lightning Scroll is a Tier 4 Спиритизм Item that provides Bonus Health and Sprint Speed. The purchasing hero's Spirit Damage will also cause enemies hit to have their Movement Speed be reduced.

Lightning Scroll has a passive effect that causes any damage done by your ultimate to apply bonus Spirit Damage and a brief Stun after a short delay. This can only be triggered once per target per ultimate, and cannot be used with ultimates that have charges (such as Виндикта's Контрольный выстрел.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400 (its Component, Мистическое замедление, makes up 1,600 of the cost).

## Notes

- Карман's ultimate does not trigger Lightning Scroll, but they can still buy it.

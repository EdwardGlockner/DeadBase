Express Shot is a Tier 3 Weapon Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200. Its component, High-Velocity Rounds, makes up 800 of the cost.

- Yamato's alternative fire

- Paradox's Kinetic Carbine

## Burst weapons

For weapons where single click launches a series of bullets Express Shot fires a full extra burst of bullets. The second burst is launched immediately with the first burst, thus the total duration of attack is almost the same.

The bonus damage is applied in the following way:

- 1st burst: only the first bullet gets 125% bonus damage. The rest use normal damage.

- 2nd burst: every bullet gets 125% bonus damage.

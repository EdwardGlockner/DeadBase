Leech is a Tier 4 Vitality Item that provides Spirit Lifesteal, Bullet Lifesteal, Bonus Health, Weapon Damage, and Spirit Power to the purchasing hero.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400. Purchasing one of its Components, Пулевая кража здоровья or Спиритическая кража здоровья, discounts the cost by 1,600 and purchasing both the components discounts the cost by 3,200.

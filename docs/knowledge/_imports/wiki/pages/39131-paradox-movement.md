## Time Wall Momentum Stop

Time Wall((by default, 2) is Paradox’s second ability. Shortly after activated, it spawns a time wall that stops time for all enemy bullets and projectiles, and damages and slows opponents in it.

When activated, Time Wall stops all momentum.

This can be used to stop momentum in the air and air dash back for the full distance. This can be used to bait opponents or quickly change direction.

To execute, be in the air and activate Time Wall. All momentum beforehand will be nullified and you should stop in the air.

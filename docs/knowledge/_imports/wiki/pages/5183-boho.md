## Trivia

- Boho was one of the heroes in a private build who was accidently leaked on stream in May 2025.1] A playable, although minimalistic, version of Boho was added to the files in the Shop Rework Update. A low quality image of Boho's model was later posted on Reddit in August 2025, and a better quality image was posted on Twitter in December. Boho's ability kit was updated in the Six New Heroes Update, adding three new abilities.

- Boho's codename might refer to Bohemianism, a cultural and social movement related to non-traditional lifestyles of artists during the mid-19th century.

- "Luxuriating in the Hideout" is unused, but is speculated to be displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Boho in the Hideout.

- Billy is the currently the only hero to say "Boho" instead of the witch.2]

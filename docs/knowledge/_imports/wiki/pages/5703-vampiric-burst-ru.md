Vampiric Burst is a Tier 4 Живучесть Item that provides Bullet Lifesteal, Bullet Resist and Bonus Health to the purchasing hero.

Its active ability grants the user Bullet Lifesteal, Fire Rate and Ammo.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400, with its Component, Пулевая кража здоровья, making up 1,600 of the cost.

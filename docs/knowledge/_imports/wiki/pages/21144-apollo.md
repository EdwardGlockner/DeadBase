Apollo is a Hero in Deadlock. With Flawless Advance the fencer begins his approach, timing his strikes perfectly to inflict more damage. Counter-attacks aren't a concern for Apollo, as a flourish of his blade deflects the damage and triggers a quick Riposte, and Disengaging Sigil allows him to jump out of the fray with ease. For his final blow, Apollo dons his fencer's mask and channels Ixia's might with Itani Lo Sahn, leaving enemies defeated in his wake.

As the prince of Ixia, Apollo comes from a land of beauty, tradition and conflict. Political violence was common in Ixia, but when Apollo was sent to New York for his own safety, he knew the situation was serious. Now a highly skilled fencer at Blackmore Academy, Apollo knows that in due time, he will be called back to Ixia... and when he returns, he will show why he's worthy to wield his family's ancestral blade.

Apollo was released on February 12, 2026 as the last hero of the Old Gods, New Blood update.

Pride of Ixia has no damage falloff and deals no damage beyond its max range.

## Trivia

- Yoshi's Apollo memeApollo's ultimate "Itani Lo Sahn" is Ixian for "I have no equal".2]

- Apollo was first teased in the Hideout with a rapier over the item shop.

- A possible inspiration for Apollo is the hero concept Fencer (Apollo's original codename) by player nathanieljbrill in the Deadlock forums. The Fencer concept used a sword as the primary weapon and had the abilities to slow enemies, deflect incoming attacks, and use a charged attack.

- "Striving Towards Perfection in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Apollo in the Hideout.

- Apollo had a fourth wall-breaking line about being the last hero released in the Old Gods, New Blood update, saying that voters had "finally come to their senses".3] His release poster also referenced this, with Apollo holding a clock and the caption "About Time". He was also the first hero to have a specific line recorded for his release, as other heroes used one of their regular lines.

- On the day of Apollo's release, Yoshi shared an image of Apollo on the Deadlock Discord referencing the "I studied the blade" meme. It reads: "When you were voting Rem, I studied The Blade. When you were voting Silver I mastered Mantle Slide. When you were voting Celeste I cultivated Inner Strength. And now that the world is on fire and the patrons are at the gate you have the audacity to come to me for help?"

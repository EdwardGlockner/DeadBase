Duncan Naoki Drummond, a.k.a Hopoo, is a Deadlock developer. Their username in the official Deadlock Discord is @hopoo.

## Overview

Duncan was the co-founder of Hopoo Games and has since been hired by Valve, along with other former Hopoo Games' staff.1]

Duncan is the lead developer of the heroes Vyper and Sinclair,2] and also worked on Calico, Holliday, Mina, Paige, and Victor.3]4]

## Trivia

- Deadbolt and Deadlock both feature a plot about supernatural creatures arriving on Earth, demons, candle people, a fire-themed patron god, and a currency called "souls".

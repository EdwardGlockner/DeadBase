Backstabber is a Tier Item Not Found. [[Item Not Found.]] Item that improves sneak attacks from behind the enemy heroes. It also reduces footstep sound distance and provides bonus health.

Attacking enemies from behind with your weapon or melee opens a wound dealing spirit damage over time and reducing their bullet resist. When nearby an enemy with a wound, heroes will gain increased move speed and be able to see the wounded target through any walls.

Calico is a Hero in Deadlock, a former monster hunter turned hired assassin. Possessing her cat, Ava, Calico uses the shadows to her advantage to surprise her foes, slinging Gloom Bombs and attacking with a Leaping Slash for damage and healing. By Returning to the Shadows, she can turn any fight to her advantage, disappearing after a fight or engaging again when the enemy least expects her.

As a contract killer, Calico is glad to finally be earning significant money by assassinating other people. Despite the good that the Baxter Society did by slaying monsters, it simply didn't pay well enough for her.

Calico was added to Hero Labs on October 24, 2024, and officially released on January 17, 2025.

## Background

### Lore

Calico has been an assassin for at least 5 years, as she was hired to kill Pocket 5 years before the events of the ritual.1] Pocket survived the assassination attempt due Calico's bullet missing their heart.2] It's implied that this failed assassination earned Calico a reputation for "sloppy work" amongst other assassins.3]

Calico is nicknamed as "the most dangerous woman on the planet".4]5] She's currently being hunted by the International Police,6] Scotland Yard,7] and the OSIC.8]9] She once assassinated someone named "Siegfried" under Mirage's nose.10]

Calico's motivation in the ritual is vague, but she seems to want to be the "Apex Predator" by wishing for power and protection.11]12]

Ava is Calico's cat familiar.13] Calico adores Ava and refers to herself as "mummy" when talking to Ava.14]15]

Calico has knowledge about the Deep,16]17] the Troubadour,18] and Oathkeeper.19] She's implied to know something about Victor's origins.20]

Calico learned Return To Shadows while she was in the Baxter Society.21]

Calico has a son.22]

## Character interactions

- Vyper tries to buy Ava with a "crisp Lincoln" ($5).25]

- As the assassin hired to kill Pocket, Calico enjoys "tying up loose ends" when she kills Pocket in-game.26] She also mentions that Maximilian Fairfax will "have questions".27] Pocket, naturally, dislikes Calico for almost killing them.28]29]

- Calico used to work with Grey Talon in the Baxter Society. While Grey Talon is disappointed in Calico for becoming an assassin,30] he doesn't resent her and was content to leave her alone.31]32]33] Calico views Grey Talon as a "relic of a bygone era",34] but also says she will mourn his death if she kills him in-game.35]

- Calico has respect for Paradox.36] She also loves the Paradox Museum.37]

- Calico wants to be friends with Wraith.38]39]

- Calico has history with Raven and likes him.40]41]

- Calico compares Holliday's introverted personality to Grey Talon's.42]

- Venator knew Calico from when she was in the Baxter Society, but loathes her now that she's an assassin.43] Venator believes that, for the right price, Calico would be willing to kill Ava.44] When killing Calico, Venator mentions that it was "for Grey Talon".45]

- The Doorman is familiar with Calico and likes her.46]47] He loves Ava and says he'll treat her "like a queen" at the Baroness Hotel.48]49]

- Mirage believes Calico is beyond redemption.50]

- Haze has been ordered to kill Calico. If they're on the same team, Haze says she's going to kill Calico before the day is out.9]

## Trivia

- Calico was called Nano in Neon Prime.

- At one point in development, Calico's gameplay style was inspired by Dota 2's Broodmother.

- Calico was an arms dealer in her original background.

- Ava is a Sphynx cat.

- "Playing With Ava in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Calico in the Hideout.

- When in the Hideout's Atrium, switching to Ava and then running near the smaller version of her will cause the kitten to follow you for the duration of the spell.

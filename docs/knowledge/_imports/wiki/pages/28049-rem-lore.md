Reference extract:

An accidental stowaway from the world of dreams, Rem has found himself stranded in the city that never sleeps. Driven by his curious nature, Rem and his helpful friends have been seen wandering across the five boroughs in search of a way back home. So if you ever find yourself dozing off while taking the Blue line home from work, maybe you’ve just had an encounter with one of the Cursed Apple’s newest denizens.

Rem is a child,7]8] and exhibits childlike characteristics.9]10] Rem can't read and doesn't understand what big words like "nuance" or "sarcastic" mean.11]12]13] According to the Patrons, Rem has a family back home who misses him.14]

Rem can see people's dreams when they're asleep. This allows him to learn information he wouldn't normally know, such as how to speak Ixian,15] that Mo wants to audit a pottery class,16]17] or Yamato's true name.18]

Rem brought his lucky pillow to the ritual.19]

### Personality

As a child, Rem is curious and wondrous.20]21] He's very blunt when talking about people,22]23] and isn't interested in why other people are in the ritual if their reasoning is boring.24] Rem hopes that his allies will become his friends for life and shows a lot of care for them.25]26]27] Enemy heroes often think Rem is cute and feel bad about hurting him.28]

Rem seems to be perpetually tired.29]30] He feels lost and wants to go home,31]32] partially so he can sleep some more.33] Rem loves sleeping and claims to be the best at it.34]

Rem calls the Lil Helpers his "friends" and gets them to do things he's too lazy to do.35]36]

Rem often comments on what other characters smell like.37]38]39] Rem thinks shoes are stupid.40]

### Appearance

Rem is a small biped furry creature with the proportions of a human toddler. His body is covered in black fur. He has a short beak for a mouth, large round fully white eyes, and tall cat-like ears, the inside of which is purple with a drifting star pattern. He has 4-fingered hands, his feet are digitigrade paws with 3 toes, and he has a long thin tail ending in a tuft of fur. He wears pyjamas consisting of a jacket and a cone hat with a blue, pink and purple wavy stripes pattern, with patches on the back of the jacket. He carries his blue lucky pillow and a golden pocket watch with a crescent moon pendant. His weapon is a blue-green candle from which he shoots wispy projectiles.

The Lil Helpers are smaller spherical creatures who, like Rem, have black fur, big round white eyes, tall ears, and a cone hat. They have flat feet.

Rem's hybrid anatomy of bird and mammal resembles that of a wingless Griffin or Keythong in ancient mythology.

### Information

Rem's name references Rapid Eye Movement, a unique and active phase of sleep in which most dreaming occurs.

## Character Relationships

### Haze

Haze and Rem are friends.41]42]43] Haze promised to help Rem get home and dislikes him fighting in the ritual due to the dangers.44]45]

Rem calls Haze his "mom".46]

Rem sees Haze when he sleeps and claims that "she's him".47]48]

Rem seems to have caused Haze some trouble prior to the ritual,49] but how the two met and how they know each other is currently unknown.

## Character Interactions

- Rem refers to Grey Talon as "grandpa."50]51]

- Rem likes Vyper and hopes she could be his "cool aunt".52]53]

- Rem wants to trade hats with Infernus.54]

- Rem thinks Billy could "teach him all kinds of fun words",55] but also thinks that he should wait until he's older to hang out with Billy.56]

- Rem calls Holliday a "sad sheriff with dead sister".57]

- Rem likes that Vindicta also dislikes wearing shoes.40]58]

- While linked to Lady Geist's dreams, Rem thinks that Oathkeeper is fun.59]

- Seven warns Rem to stay away from anyone who mentions the OSIC, claiming that they won't help Rem get home.60] Rem finds Seven scary.61]

- Venator promises to protect Rem and help him get home while they're on the same team.62]63] If they're on opposing teams, however, Venator believes Rem's putting on a façade and calls him a "furry bastard".64]65]

- Graves wishes she could make Rem a pet.66]

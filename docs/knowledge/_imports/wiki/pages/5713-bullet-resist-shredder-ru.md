Bullet Resist Shredder is a Tier 2 Спиритизм Item that grants Bonus Health and Melee Resist to the purchasing hero. It has a passive which reduces the Bullet Resist on enemies that the purchasing hero deals Spirit Damage to.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

## Notes

- This is not a stacking effect, repeated applications will refresh the duration of the debuff.

- The debuff can be utilized by other players. Teammates can benefit from this debuff.

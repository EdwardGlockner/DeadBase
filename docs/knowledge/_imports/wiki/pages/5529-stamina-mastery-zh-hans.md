Stamina Mastery is a Tier 3 活力 Item that gives Stamina, Stamina recovery, and Air Jump/Dash distance as well as letting the user do a second air dash and air jump. It was previously named Superior Stamina.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200 (its Component, 耐力补给, makes up 800 of the cost).

## Notes

- Stamina Mastery no longer grants a bonus wall jump after the 2025-04-04 Update wall jump rework.

## Trivia

- The Hidden King's name and appearance have similarities to The King in Yellow, a fictional supernatural entity created by Robert W. Chambers.

- A proposed rename to the Hidden King was The Broken Crown.

- The Hidden King announcer's voice actor was recast with the new model in the Old Gods, New Blood update. The current voice is speculated to be either Clancy Brown (Mr. Krabs in Spongebob Squarepants) or Darin De Paul (Reinhardt in Overwatch). Developer Kaori has so far chosen not to reveal the name of either the current or previous voice actor to not "spoil the fun".10] They have also said that the previous Amber Hand actor can still be heard in the game in a different role.11]

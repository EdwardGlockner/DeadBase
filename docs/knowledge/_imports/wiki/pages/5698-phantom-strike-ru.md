Phantom Strike is a Tier 4 Vitality Item that provides Spirit Power and Weapon Damage to the purchasing hero.

Phantom Strike's Active, when used, will teleport the hero to a target enemy, dealing damage and applying Slow and Disarm for 3 seconds.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

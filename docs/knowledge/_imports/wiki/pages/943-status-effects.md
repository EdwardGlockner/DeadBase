Status Effects are modifiers or effects applied to units or players after certain conditions have been met. Buffs are positive status effects, while debuffs are negative. They will last for a set duration unless cleared through certain items or abilities. Status effects can be displayed over an entity's health bar, as a transparent overlay on the entity's body, or not displayed at all.

## Debuffs

Bleed

### Bleed

A negative status effect that deals damage equal to a percentage of a unit's health over time.

Sources:

- Drifter's Stalker's Mark

- Vindicta's Crow Familiar

- Shiv's Serrated Knives

- Decay

- Toxic Bullets

Note: Serrated Knives applies a unique variant of bleed that deals flat damage rather than percent health damage.

Burn

### Burn

A negative status effect that deals Spirit Damage over time.

Sources:

- Infernus' Afterburn

- Venator's Consecrating Grenade (deals Weapon Damage and Pure Damage instead of Spirit)

- Spirit Burn

Curse

### Curse

A negative status effect that interrupts, disarms, and silences abilities and active items.

Sources:

- Grey Talon's Spirit Snare

- Cursed Relic (also removes non-ultimate buffs)

- Mystical Piano (also applies movement slow)

- Patron and Shrines defensive attack

Adjacent Effects:

- Apollo's Itani Lo Sahn applies an effect similar to Curse, but it also applies Slow Motion and does not interrupt abilities.

Damage Output Reduction

### Damage Output Reduction

A negative status effect that reduces the damage output of the afflicted target. Some sources can reduce all damage types outputted, and others may only reduce a specific type.

Sources:

- Infernus' Afterburn (Tier 2) - Decreases spirit damage output (Built-Up from Bullets)

- Mirage's Fire Scarabs (Base and Tier 3) - Decreases all damage outputs

- Warden's Alchemical Flask (Base and Tier 2) - Decreases weapon damage output

- Graves' Essence Theft (Base and Tier 2) - Decreases weapon damage output (Built-Up from Bullets)

- Inhibitor - Decreases all damage outputs (Built-Up from Bullets)

- Silencer - Decreases spirit damage outputs (Built-Up from Bullets)

Disarm

### Disarm

A negative status effect that prevents the afflicted target from firing their weapon. They are still capable of casting abilities and using melee attacks.

Disarm affects abilities that modify the player's primary fire, such as Venator's Ira Domini and Paradox's Kinetic Carbine. However, it does not affect abilities that deal Weapon Damage without primary fire, such as Haze's Bullet Dance or Venator's Consecrating Grenade and Gutshot. It also doesn't affect Vindicta's Assassinate.

### Time Stop

A negative status effect that slows the speed of the player's actions, including the effects of inertia and gravity. Also known as Time Slow or Slow Motion.

## Hero Specific Effects

The following Status Effects are either exclusively relevant to or only applied by a specific Hero.

Affliction

### Affliction

A negative status effect applied by Pocket's Affliction ability. It deals constant damage over time for a long duration, however it is non-lethal and cannot reduce a target's health below 1. It also does not apply item procs.

Eternal Night

### Eternal Night

A negative status effect applied by Drifter's ability of the same name. Inflicted heroes have their vision severely limited.

Fixation

### Fixation

A negative status effect that increases the target's damage received from Haze. It is unique to her, applied through her ability of the same name.

Hotel Guest

### Hotel Guest

A negative status effect applied by The Doorman's ability of the same name. The target is sent to the Baroness Hotel and has to make their way to the elevator to escape.

Lethal Venom

### Lethal Venom

A negative status effect applied exclusively by Vyper's ability of the same name. After a short delay, it deals Spirit Damage to the target. The max damage depends on the target's missing health.

Malice

### Malice

A negative status effect applied exclusively by Lady Geist's ability of the same name. It inflicts a decaying Movement Slow on the target and increases the amount of damage Lady Geist deals to them. It can be stacked up to 5 times and refreshed.

Petrified

### Petrified

A negative status effect applied by Vyper's Petrifying Bola. Inflicted heroes are interrupted, unable to move and perform actions, but block all incoming damage, with the exception of Vyper's Lethal Venom (both the buildup and damage).

Rabbit Hex

### Rabbit Hex

A negative status effect applied by Sinclair's ability of the same name. Inflicted heroes change shape into a rabbit, with its corresponding hitbox, and move faster but have amplified received damage. The hero cannot perform any actions and abilities in rabbit form besides walking and jumping, but any actions and abilities initiated before the transformation are not interrupted.

Tether

### Tether

A negative status effect applied by Vindicta's Stake ability. It binds all nearby targets to a central point, forcing them to stay within a short radius of the center.

Wrecked

### Wrecked

A negative status effect applied by the passive of Billy's Blasted ability. Melee hits will inflict wrecked on enemies. When Blasted is activated, Billy will gain additional bullet damage against Wrecked enemies.

## Countering Debuffs

Certain abilities and items can be used to mitigate or remove negative status effects.

### Temporary Buffs

These effects grant a short-term immunity to certain debuffs. For a full list of sources, see the main entry for each buff.

- Invincibility: Grants temporary immunity to all damage and hostile effects.

- Unstoppable: Grants temporary immunity to crowd control effects.

- Indomitable: Grants immunity to a single crowd control effect.

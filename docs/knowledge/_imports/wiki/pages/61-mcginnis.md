McGinnis is a Hero in Deadlock. The head of Fairfax Industries Military R&D department, McGinnis has a high opinion of her work which includes her machine gun, automatic numerous Mini Turrets, Spectral Walls, that she can create to protect teammates, hold down positions and trap enemies, Medicinal Specter, which dispenses health to nearby allies, and finally, a devastating spectral Heavy Barrage that can reach even faraway enemies.

A talented engineer who started her career from a young age, Margaret "Maggie" McGinnis isn't fond of theoretical work, thus spending most of her time working in a mechanics shop, often meddling with the supernatural. While the ethics of brainstorming the occult war machine are grey at best, McGinnis isn't that fussed so long as she gets her paycheck.

### Notes

- Services Rendered takes 2 seconds to spin up, increasing McGinnis' fire rate and bullet spread, and slowly winds down at 25% per second (4 seconds total at max spin) as indicated by the grey indicator around the crosshair.

- The DPS and Bullets per second stats listed in game use the max spin up values.

- Services Rendered's base fire rate starts at 5 bullets per second (Cycle time: 0.2) and spins up to 12.5 bullets per second (Cycle time: 0.08), averaging around 11 Bullets per second per clip at the beginning of a match.

### Mini Turret

MINI TURRET

- Turrets have 80% damage resist that decays over 6s, and 35% melee resist and 35% spirit resist that do not decay.

- Turrets deal 50% damage to Troopers and Denizens and 30% damage to Objectives.

- It will initially target the nearest enemy hero in line of sight when it is placed. If no hero is in sight, it will target the nearest enemy unit.

- It will only switch targets once its initial target dies or leaves sight, or when McGinnis starts shooting a hero in sight, in which case all turrets will attempt to aim at the hero being shot.

- Shooting a unit or hitting an enemy hero with Melee Attacks, abilities or items will not change the turret's target.

- Damage Falloff does not affect incoming damage to the turrets. The turrets will always take full Weapon Damage from an enemy, regardless of distance.

- Turrets yield 40 souls + 4 per minute when killed. Part of the bounty can be denied as a Soul Orb.

- Turrets receive additional damage from Monster Rounds and Cultist Sacrifice.

## Trivia

- McGinnis' design is similar to popular depictions of the American World War II cultural icon Rosie the Riveter - notably the red headband, blue shirt, jeans overalls, red socks and bulky physique.

- In The Binding of Oathkeeper artwork, McGinnis has a mermaid tattoo on her left shoulder.

- McGinnis' Heavy Barrage sound effects were changed to sound less like a real air raid siren.

- "Tinkering in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as McGinnis in the Hideout.

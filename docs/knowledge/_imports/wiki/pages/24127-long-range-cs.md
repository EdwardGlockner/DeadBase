Long Range is a Tier 2 Zbraň Item that grants increased weapon Fall-off range and Sprint Speed. Its passive causes heroes to deal additional Weapon Damage while they are far away from their target.

Long Range is a Tier 2 Zbraň Předmět that can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

It is a component of Odstřelovačské hledí.

Quicksilver Reload is a Tier 2 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

It is a component of Mercurial Magnum.

- Abilities that do not deal damage still trigger the instant reload and bonus Fire Rate effects.

- The passive buff has a fixed duration of 12 seconds and is not affected by cooldown reduction.

## Notable Interactions

Heroes with long reload times and quick-charging or multi-charge abilities can make good usage of Quicksilver:

- McGinnis with multiple turret charges

- Infernus with multiple Napalm charges

- Haze who benefits from maintaining Fixation stacks on targeted enemies

- Vindicta Crow Familiar has a short cooldown after second upgrade

- Mo & Krill Scorn has a short cooldown and heals a percent of damage dealt

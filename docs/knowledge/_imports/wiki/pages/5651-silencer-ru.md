Silencer is a Tier 4 Оружие Item that increases Spirit Resist. Its passive reduces the target's Spirit Damage, additionally, Silencer has a passive effect which causes the purchaser's bullets to build up a silence effect on enemy targets. Once reached, the Hero is silenced for a short duration.

It can be purchased for 6,400.

## Notes

- Silencer's Damage Reduction Debuff effect on Spirit Damage will apply to enemy players with Unstoppable activated; But only the Damage Debuff portion is applied, the Silenced aspect will not take effect.

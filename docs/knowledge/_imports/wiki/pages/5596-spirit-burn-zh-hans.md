Spirit Burn is a Tier 4 元灵 Item that provides Ability Range.

Its passive causes dealing significant Spirit Damage to an enemy hero to trigger an explosion, burning the target and nearby enemies while reducing their healing. This effect can be triggered on non-heroes for half-damage and will not cause the passive to go on cooldown unless a hero is hit.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400.

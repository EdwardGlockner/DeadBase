Golden Goose Egg is a Tier 1 Spirit Item that can be purchased from the Shop for 800.

Golden Goose Egg is consumed once it's used, and can then be re-purchased.

This item is disabled in Street Brawl, but the enhanced version can still be accessed with console commands.

## Buffs Granted

The buffs received follow the scaling of Golden Statue buffs at the time the egg is hatched.

## Trivia

- Golden Goose Egg is the first consumable item added to Deadlock.

- The item's name is a reference to the Aesop's Fable The Goose that Laid the Golden Eggs.

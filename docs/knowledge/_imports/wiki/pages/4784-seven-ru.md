Seven is Hero in Deadlock. He is a powerful mystic who doesn't abide by the rules of lesser men. Utilizing his psychic mastery of electrical currents, Seven excels in rapidly taking control of a fight. Through stuns, roving area denial and unbridled surges in power, he leads enemies right to where he wants them within his ever-growing storm cloud before putting them in their place.

It is unknown what actions led to Seven being imprisoned within Lost Whisper. However, Seven managed to turn what was meant to be his execution into the bloody murder of his captors. Finally free and waiting within the underbelly of the Cursed Apple, he wishes to reach "apotheosis" by completing the ritual.

## Background

### Lore

Seven claims he was imprisoned in Lost Whisper because he "offended small-minded simpletons with fragile egos". He says he "doesn't like" murder, but rather is "indifferent" to it.1]

Seven has done research on the Astral Gates, and he credits Professor Dynamo as an influence in his work. He also tells Dynamo that he has loved someone.2]

Seven's goal of "apotheosis"3] means the glorification of a subject to divine level,4] implying that Seven wants to become a god, or gain godlike powers.

On the run from the OSIC, Seven currently lives in New York's old subway tunnels. He tells Mo & Krill he has "particular needs" regarding the creation of new tunnels.1]

The New York Oracle reporter Allie Kincaid is writing a story on Seven.5]6]

## Character interactions

- A likely possibility is that Seven is Kelvin's old friend Edrick, who "vanished". Леди Гайст says Edrick "caused a scene" at one of Kelvin's fundraisers.12] This is supported by one of Seven's lines to Kelvin that mirrors one of Kelvin's lines about Edrick.13]14]

- Seven believes Кельвин has a bigger ego than Хлыст.15]

- Seven has a strong conviction that exceptional magic users should use their powers, regardless of how others might judge them for it. He criticizes Динамо for wanting to "throw away" the "great gift" of his star body, teases Инфернус for "putting on a muzzle" and "wasting" his powers, and says Макгиннис is "wasting her brilliance" working for Fairfax Industries. He respects Плющик for "seeing the world as it was and deciding it was not acceptable to her".1]

- Парадокс threatens to reveal the location of Seven's safehouse.1]

- Seven wants to commission Парадокс for a job.1]

- When on the same team, Seven teases Пелена for working alongside him and asks how soon will they start fighting each other once the ritual is over.1]

- Макгиннис offers help to remove Seven's hat.1]

## Trivia

- Seven during Deadlock Pre-Alpha (Neon Prime) was named Gigawatt.

- Seven is the only character who parries with his back.

- Seven's clothes has numbers that form a Gorowase, a form of Japanese wordplay with numbers. 4279 can be read as Shi (4) Ni (2) Na (7) Ku (9) = Shininaku (死になく), or "Become unkillable" (rough translation).

- Seven's name may be a reference to Numerology, in which the number 7 is associated with spirituality and the search for knowledge.16]

- Seven is currently the only hero with a limping animation which plays when he is low on health.

## 背景故事

### Lore

Grey Talon believes the Friends of Humanity are behind the murder of his family.1]2]

Grey Talon chose Shiv as his replacement in the Baxter Society.3]4]

Grey Talon's son was named Nathan.5]

Grey Talon has visited South Ixia and said it was "beautiful".6]

## 角色互动

- Grey Talon and 薇妲 have hunted the Friends of Humanity together and regard each other as friends.7]8]9]

- Grey Talon says his "stepdaughter" is Ixian, like 亚伯兰.10] However, it seems that the intended term was "daughter-in-law", as evidenced by Talon's background and Abrams' response.

- 岚梦 knows Grey Talon. She refers to him by his first name and knew his late son, Nathan.5]

- 大和 respects Grey Talon for his age and skill.11]12]

- 沃督 says he looked up to Grey Talon and apologizes for killing him.13]14]

- 卡厉可 compares 哈雷黛's introverted personality to Grey Talon's.15]

- 卡厉可 calls Grey Talon "a relic of a bygone era",16] but also says she will mourn his death.17]

- 哈雷黛 likes Grey Talon due to their shared goal of revenge.18]

- 卡厉可, 麦金妮 and 蝰邪 comment on Grey Talon's health, due to his age.18]19]

## 角色趣闻

- Grey Talon's current model is inherited from his design in Neon Prime, in which he was called Orion.

- Grey Talon's bow was originally called Koschei.

- Gray Talon is currently the only hero whose Bullet Damage is directly affected by Spirit Power.

- Grey Talon has been affectionately nicknamed "Grandma" by the playerbase.

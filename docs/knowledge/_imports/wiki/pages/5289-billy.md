Billy is a Hero in Deadlock. A white hot ball of rage, furious at the very notion of... something. Billy is a chaotic front-liner who never backs down from a challenge. With Rising Ram he gets up close and personal, before slamming down with Bashdown to create shockwaves through his enemies. Running isn't even an option when Billy can use his ultimate Chain Gang to grapple enemies and pull them back in. A quick swig from his bottle gets him Blasted, and his enemies totally wrecked.

From looting to public vandalism, Billy makes sure anyone who can be bothered to listen gets to hear "the truth". Don't ask him how he got the goat head.

Billy was released on August 20, 2025 as the second hero of the Six New Heroes update (first winner of hero vote).

Due to Billy's Blasted passive, hitting a melee attack reloads his ammo by either 100% (heavy melee) or 35% (light melee).

## Trivia

- "Billy goat" is a term used for a male goat.

- Billy's weapon name, Biquette, means "young female goat" in French. It is a reference to "Biquette the punk rock goat", who went viral on the internet for hanging around punk rock and metal concerts.

- Billy makes a reference to the Rejuvenator rework, saying that "it used to go out to everybody until the Man decided it was too good".5]

- Billy was one of the heroes in a private build who was accidentally leaked on stream in May 2025. Being early in development, he was using Shiv's model as a placeholder.6] Billy's work-in-progress portrait was later leaked by an anonymous Discord user in July 2025.

- "Ranting in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Billy in the Hideout.

- One of Billy's in-game hero select screen labels is G.O.A.T., a common acronym standing for "greatest of all time".

- Wrecked was originally called 'Pissed', remnants of this can still be seen while hovering over the icon next to the healthbar while Billy is Blasted.

- Billy had the largest lead in the Six New Heroes contest, at 30%.7]

- Billy possesses a full set of teeth at the front of his mouth. This is unlike real goats, who lack an upper set and instead have a hard dental pad.

- Billy eats his gun magazine in his reload animation. This is a reference to the popular myth that goats can consume and digest any material, including inedible ones.

- Billy does a sign of the horns when riding the zipline. This can be a reference to both his actual goat horns and his love of punk music, as well as a common salute in Satanism and occult practices.

There are currently 38 heroes available, with more to be released as the game develops.

Heroes have set base stats that they can improve through leveling up or purchasing items. They also have additional Power Increases, gained at specific levels which grant bonuses to specific stats. Heroes can hold up to 9 individual items at a time. This can be increased to 12 items if Extra Slots are unlocked. Some hero stats are also affected by Spirit Power, referred to as Spirit Scaling.

38 heroes are available in the current alpha build, with additional characters confirmed for future releases.

## Unused Heroes

There are a number of heroes in Deadlock that can, or could only be accessed using console commands. Some of them are older heroes inherited from Neon Prime, while others were added to the game more recently. Some of these heroes were playable in the Hero Labs experimental gamemode, and were later removed.

Bullet Lifesteal is a Tier 2 Живучесть Item that provides Bullet Lifesteal and Bonus Health to the purchasing hero.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 1,600.

Bullet Lifesteal is a Component of Кровопийца, Яростный экстаз, and Порыв вампиризма

Torment Pulse is a Tier 3 Спиритизм Item that provides Bonus Health and Spirit Power to the purchasing hero. It periodically (every 1.5s, scaled with cooldown) deals Spirit Damage to the 2 closest enemies.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

## Notes

Torment Pulse can be useful for constantly activating ability and item effects that depend on dealing spirit damage, such as Измельчитель пулестойкости and Растущее воздействие.

## Trivia

- When controlling Управляемая сова, Torment Pulse ceases to damage enemies based on its proximity to Серый Коготь’s body, and instead triggers based on the owl position. Although the pulses are happening at the owl location, the lightning VFX visually starts from the stationary body of Grey Talon.

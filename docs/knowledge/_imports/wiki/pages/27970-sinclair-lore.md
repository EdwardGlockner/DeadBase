Henry's parents told him he was too young and impulsive to mess with real magic, and so they gave him a deck of cards to play with. He took to sleight of hand naturally and soon was dazzling his classmates with fantastic feats of illusions that were done without mystic artifacts or incantation. When he was in high school he met Savannah, and their mutual love of stage magic sparked a whirlwind romance. Soon they fell in love and created an act together. Obviously they were meant to be; and so at prom, Henry proposed to Savannah and they married soon after graduation. Unsurprisingly both sets of parents were not thrilled at this prospect, but Henry and Savannah knew they were soulmates. And so they cemented their love for one another by having a blood mystic bind their souls for eternity. When an on-stage accident claimed both their lives, no one was sure who to blame. Henry claims Savannah was out of position. Savannah says Henry didn't wait for his cue. But one thing's for sure, since their souls are tied to each other neither one of them have been able to move on. Now a bickering pair of ghosts, Henry and Savannah need to work together if they want to have any chance of undoing their eternal union.

### Personality

Henry and Savannah hate each other.8] When calling upon their partner as a Spectral Assistant, they say it's "just like old times".9]

While Henry is more lively, Savannah is more gentle and straightforward.10] Still, they both possess an whimsical vocabulary and tone of personality, much like magicians often present themselves.11]

Both Henry and Savannah believe they are "the responsible one".12]13]

### Appearance

Sinclair's current model is a modified version of one of Wraith's unused designs.

The Sinclairs' body is a featureless white humanoid wearing a magician suit, implied to be a wooden mannequin or automaton based on its sounds. They have a generic human face with no mouth or eyes, and the only notable feature is a pencil moustache (curved in their icon). They wear a white shirt with a black tailcoat and pants, a white bowtie and a black top hat, and a golden medallion on their chest. The body has a bulky chest and slender limbs. Their weapon is a stage magic wand.

The Spectral Assistant is a translucent purple woman using Lady Geist's original model, minus the shawl.

### Information

Henry and Savannah both inhabit the same body. During the ritual, the body is initially controlled by Henry, then changes to Savannah once Henry dies, and vice-versa. The character who isn't controlling the body plays the Spectral Assistant (though the Assistant currently only has a female model).

Both Henry and Savannah have to agree on who's controlling the body before the ritual.14]

## Character Interactions

- Various heroes complain about being turned into a rabbit by Sinclair.15]

- Although their conversation is incomplete, it's implied in Holliday's interaction with Sinclair that she relates to them due to having had a divorce before.16]17]

- Krill says Sinclair's stage show was "incredible".6]

- Seven says he prefers his fate to Sinclair's.18]

- Celeste thinks both Sinclairs deserve each other.19]

- The Shopkeeper tells the Sinclairs that they need counseling, and a gun.20]

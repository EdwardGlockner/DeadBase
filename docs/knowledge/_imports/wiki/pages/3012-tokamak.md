## Trivia

- Tokamak's model was a modified version of Bebop's model.

- Fathom's second ability, Breach, is a combination of two abilities Tokamak had: Functionally, it is identical to Tokamak's Dying Star and its name was taken from a removed ability for Tokamak also called Breach.

- Raven's ultimate ability, Revelation, is also functionally similar to Tomamak's Blinding Radiance: Enemies are afflicted with debuffs and damage if they look towards them while their ability is active.

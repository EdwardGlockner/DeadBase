Apollo is a Hero in Deadlock. With Flawless Advance the fencer begins his approach, timing his strikes perfectly to inflict more damage. Counter-attacks aren't a concern for Apollo, as a flourish of his blade deflects the damage and triggers a quick Riposte, and Disengaging Sigil allows him to jump out of the fray with ease. For his final blow, Apollo dons his fencer's mask and channels Ixia's might with Itani Lo Sahn, leaving enemies defeated in his wake.

Apollo was released on February 12, 2026 as the last hero of the Old Gods, New Blood update.

## Background

### Lore

Apollo is 18 years old2] and is referred to as "the Prince of Ixia"3] or "Crown Prince of North Ixia", though he finds the title inaccurate.4] His father is referred to as a warlord, and has the authority equivalent to a governor and a general.5] Apollo is proud of his father and believes his father is proud of him.6] His father says "loyalty is everything" and "the first step to victory begins with crushing the enemy's hope".7]8] When killing Silver, Apollo mentions that his father would've turned her into a cloak.9]

Apollo holds his Ixian culture in very high regard.10]11] He refers to the other playable Ixians, Abrams and Infernus, as his "brothers".12]13] While he doesn't hate humans, he believes Ixians are above them.14]15] Apollo is content at Blackmore Academy,16] but he refuses to see New York as his home and is only here because his father wanted him to be.17]18] Ixian is his native language.19]

Apollo was taught fencing at a young age, having learned Riposte when he was only 5 years old.20] He seems to be a fencing champion,21] and hopes to one day represent Ixia at the Olympics.22]23] Apollo inherited his sword from his father.24]

Apollo's wish is unknown, but he seems to be fighting for the sake of Ixia.25]26] While mentioned that Apollo's father expects great things from him,27] Apollo claims that he isn't fighting in the ritual for his father.28]

Apollo is extremely confident in his skills and believes himself to be the best.29]30] He holds honor and discipline in high regards.31]32]33] His attitude often causes other characters to regard him as vain.34]35]

Apollo says his life has been personally affected by political assassinations.36] Notably, while he speaks often of his father, he never mentions his mother.

The symbol etched in Disengaging Sygil is Apollo's family sigil.37] He has fond memories of his father teaching him how to cast it.38]

Apollo has no siblings.39]

Apollo believes Maximilian Fairfax is a "great man".40] Both Apollo and his father are fans of Fairfax Industries.41]

## Character interactions

- Apollo and Graves are classmates, and are each other's lab partners.42]

- Apollo is shocked that Infernus doesn't speak Ixian nor care for Ixian culture.43]44]

- Apollo respects Ivy but believes she shouldn't have saved Spanish Harlem, believing that they should've saved themselves rather than rely solely on Ivy.45]46]

- Apollo sends his father's regards to Ambassador Nashala.48]49]

- Apollo accidentally invites Viscous to one of his father's galas.50]

- Apollo commends Vindicta for her war against the Friends of Humanity, although he dislikes her dishonorable tactics.51]52]

- Haze is worried about Apollo participating in the ritual since him dying would cause an international incident.53]54]

- Krill compares Apollo to a teenage version of Lash.34]

## Trivia

- Apollo was first teased in the Hideout with a rapier over the item shop.

- A possible inspiration for Apollo is the hero concept Fencer (Apollo's original codename) by player nathanieljbrill in the Deadlock forums. The Fencer concept used a sword as the primary weapon and had the abilities to slow enemies, deflect incoming attacks, and use a charged attack.

- "Striving Towards Perfection in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Apollo in the Hideout.

- Apollo's ultimate "Itani Lo Sahn" is Ixian for "I have no equal".55]

Boundless Spirit is a Tier 4 元灵 Item that provides Spirit Power, Bonus Health, and Out of Combat Health Regen to the purchasing hero.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 6,400. Its Component, 灵力高涨, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

## Notes

- Currently, it is the only item that gives you a percentage of your current Spirit Power.

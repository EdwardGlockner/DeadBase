Runed Gauntlets is a Legendary Weapon Item that can only be obtained in Street Brawl.

## Notes

- The parry-piercing passive will go into cooldown only after a parry has been pierced.

- Viscous' Puddle Punch can still be parried even when Runed Gauntlets are equipped.

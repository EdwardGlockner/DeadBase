The Tunnel Rats are an underground (both figuratively and literally) group that assists the black market that operates between the "boroughs", using a network of underground tunnels.1]

The Tunnel Rats used to do business with the Mendoza Syndicate.2]

## Notable members

Mo & Krill act as enforcers for The Tunnel Rats, punishing anyone who disrespects the group. They oppose the gangs who occasionally try and exploit the tunnel system.

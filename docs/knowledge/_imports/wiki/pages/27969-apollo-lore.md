A land of beauty, tradition and conflict; North Ixia is no stranger to brutal political violence. So when Apollo was told that it was for his own safety he was being sent to New York for school, he knew the situation was serious. Adjusting to life at Blackmore Academy didn’t come easy to Apollo. His school was supposed to be filled with The Cursed Apple’s best and brightest but all he saw, with few exceptions, was a sea of aggressive mediocrity. Electing not to waste his time with frivolous things like parties and dances, he instead put his energy on the piste, where he rigorously trains as captain of the Blackmore fencing team. However, at the end of the day Apollo does not fret too much on the tedium of life in New York. He knows that eventually the time will come when his father will call on him to come home and he will get to show that he is worthy of wielding his family's ancestral blade.

Now a student at Blackmore Academy, Apollo decided that, instead of wasting time going to parties and dances, he'd spend his free time training rigourously with his family's ancestral blade, preparing for the day his father would call on him to come home.11]

In the current day, Apollo is 18 years old.13] He's the captain of Blackmore Academy's fencing team and seems to be a fencing champion,14] hoping to one day represent Ixia at the Olympics.15]16] With the upcoming Maelstrom, Apollo is participating in the ritual to honor his family name and to test his skills.3]4]

Ixian is Apollo's native language.17] His ultimate " Itani Lo Sahn" is Ixian for "I have no equal".18]

Apollo says his life has been personally affected by political assassinations.19] Notably, while he speaks often of his father, he never mentions his mother.

### Personality

Apollo holds his Ixian culture in very high regard.9]20] He refers to the other playable Ixians, Abrams and Infernus, as his "brothers".21]22] While Apollo doesn't hate humans, he believes Ixians are above them.23]24] Apollo is content at Blackmore Academy,25] but he refuses to see New York as his home and is only there because his father wanted him to be.26]27]

Apollo is extremely confident in his skills and believes himself to be the best.28]29] He holds honor and discipline in high regards.30]8]31] His attitude often causes other characters to regard him as vain.32]33]

Apollo believes he is in the prime of his life and can't waste it making memories.34]35]

### Appearance

Apollo is a slim young Ixian man with red skin, amber eyes with vertical slit pupils and black sclera, blond hair with dark roots and an undercut, a pair of tall dark horns in his forehead, and a tail ending in a triangular tip with a golden ornament. He wears a red Blackmore Academy uniform jacket over a white shirt with unbuttoned collar, white fencing uniform pants and shoes with red highlights and the number "1", a white fencing glove in his right hand, and a golden ring with a black jewel in his left pinky finger. His weapon is a golden and black sword that shoots projectiles, with an elaborate rainguard and a red jewel at the pommel.

### Information

The symbol etched in Disengaging Sigil is Apollo's family sigil.36] He has fond memories of his father teaching him how to cast it.37]

Apollo believes Maximilian Fairfax is a "great man".38] Both Apollo and his father are fans of Fairfax Industries.39]

## Character Relationships

### Apollo's father

Apollo's father is referred to as a warlord, and has the authority equivalent to a governor and a general.40]

Apollo is proud of his father and believes his father is proud of him.41] While mentioned that Apollo's father expects great things from him,42] Apollo claims that he isn't fighting in the ritual for his father.43]

Apollo's father says "loyalty is everything" and "the first step to victory begins with crushing the enemy's hope".7]44]

Apollo inherited his sword from his father.45]

When killing Silver, Apollo mentions that his father would've turned her into a cloak.46]

### Graves

Graves and Apollo are classmates at Blackmore Academy, and are each other's lab partners.47]

Apollo believes Graves is a "decent" lab partner, but disapproves of her truancy.48] Graves calls Apollo "the Golden Boy",49] and doesn't hate Apollo as much as she wishes she did.50]

Graves and Apollo are familiar with each other's parents, and insult each other over their parental relationships.51]

### The Doorman

Apollo has stayed at the Baroness Hotel in the past.52] He likes The Doorman and tipped him "last week".53]

While the conversation is unfinished, The Doorman appears to give his best wishes to Apollo's father.54]

## Character Interactions

- Apollo is shocked that Infernus doesn't speak Ixian nor care for Ixian culture.55]56] Infernus thinks Apollo "made being Ixian his whole personality".57]

- Apollo doesn't respect Pocket for running away "from their obligations",58] and believes they "pissed away" their chance to have the world at their fingertips.59]

- Apollo respects Ivy but believes she shouldn't have saved Spanish Harlem, believing that they should've saved themselves rather than rely solely on Ivy.60]61]

- Apollo sends his father's regards to Ambassador Nashala.64]65]

- Apollo accidentally invites Viscous to one of his father's galas.66]

- Apollo commends Vindicta for her war against the Friends of Humanity, although he dislikes her dishonorable tactics.67]68]

- Haze is worried about Apollo participating in the ritual since him dying would cause an international incident.69]70]

- Krill compares Apollo to a teenage version of Lash.71]

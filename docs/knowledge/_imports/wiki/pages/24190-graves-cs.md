Graves is a Hero in Deadlock. The dead have been making a whole lot of demands lately, and only Graves has the courage to oblige. That severed lich hand she found in the cemetery has proven itself a valuable ally, allowing her to latch onto her enemies and steal their power for herself. Her curiosities about the passage of life are further put to use with Jar of Dead, collecting the remains of the fallen, shaking them up, and throwing them back at her enemies to grant those remorseful spirits a final shot at revenge. With Grasping Hands she strikes a deep crack in the earth, allowing those down below to grab onto enemies' ankles and stop them from escaping her reach. And when all else fails, a Borrowed Decree will summon the dead to fight alongside her.

Graves was released on January 29, 2026 as the second winner of the Old Gods, New Blood update contest.

## Background

### Lore

Graves' motivation in the ritual is unknown, but she seems to be fighting on behalf of her mentor: the lich.1]2]3] Graves idolises the lich and considers her her best friend.4]5] There are some implications, however, that the lich is just using Graves to get what she wants.6]7] While occasionally warned about the dangers of a lich's hand, Graves ignores the warnings.8]9]10]

According to the Patrons, Graves works tirelessly to honor the dead and grant them peace, and many souls are in her debt.11]12] She considers her role as a voice for the dead as "an honor."13]

Graves seems to like cats, showing affection for Ava (despite pointing out how ugly she is)14]15] and believing that the world would probably be better off if 90% of humans were replaced by cats.16]

Graves once wrote an essay about Johann Geist in a humanities course.17] She's currently writing a paper on Wuthering Heights.18]

Graves has a sister.19]

Graves does babysitting.20]

While Graves' age hasn't been mentioned, she shares classes with Apollo, who is 18 years old.21]22]

## Character interactions

- Graves calls Apollo "the Golden Boy."23]

- Graves shows interest in trying to help the undead characters, such as Kelvin, Vindicta and Victor.24]25]26]

- Graves thinks Dynamo's jacket is cool.27] She dislikes Infernus' fedora.28]

- The Lich knows Grey Talon and warns Graves of him.29] She also warns Graves about Seven's power.30]

- Graves really likes Yamato, even though she knows she shouldn't like her.31]32]33]

- Graves' mentor wants Abrams' Tome.34] Graves also thinks her mentor would want to get her hands on Kelvin's stone.35]

- Seven warns Graves that her mentor is using her to "live glories they could not achieve on their own."9] He also warns Graves that the path she's on will eventually get her killed. Seven warns Graves because he "hates to see talent wasted."36]

- Haze offers a place at the OSIC to Graves, suggesting a job as a Medium.37]

- Infernus offers to help Graves if she ever finds herself in over her head, as a form of payment for Hank saving his life when he was Graves' age.38]

- Holliday and the Shopkeeper call Graves a "theatre kid."39]40]

- According to Rem, Graves smells like wet dirt.41]

- On a kill line, Graves mentions Shiv "took something that doesn't belong to him".42]

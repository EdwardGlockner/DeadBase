Bullet Evasion is a stat that prevents a percentage of incoming bullets from hitting the target. Instead, the bullets will phase through the target, potentially hitting any target behind them.

## Evasion Immunity

Bullets with the following effects cannot be prevented by Evasion.

- Paradox's Kinetic Carbine

- Mystic Shot

- Lucky Shot

- Armor Piercing Rounds

## Sources of Bullet Evasion

- Grey Talon's Rain of Arrows T3 gives him 30% Bullet Evasion for the duration of the ability.

- Haze's Bullet Dance has 30% Bullet Evasion for the duration of the ability, with 40% added on T3.

- Mirage's Dust Devil gives him 30% Bullet Evasion for 4s after emerging from the tornado, with 30% added on T2.

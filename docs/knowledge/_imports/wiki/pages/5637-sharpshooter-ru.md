Sharpshooter is a Tier 3 Оружие Item that grants increased Sprint Speed (at the cost of Move Speed), weapon fall-off range and zoom. Its passive effect causes attacks to deal additional Weapon Damage while the user is far enough away from their target.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200. Its Component, Дальняя дистанция, makes up 1,600 of the cost.

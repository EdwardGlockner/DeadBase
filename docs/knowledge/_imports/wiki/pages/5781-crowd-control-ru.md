Crowd Control (CC) refers to effects that alter an opponent's capability to function, including their movement, ability use, weapon firing, and many others.

They can be applied both by Hero's abilities and Items from the Shop.

Similar to Stun, it prevents the target from shooting, using abilities, and slowing them to 2m/s. However, it does not Interrupt. Receiving any damage wakes the target instantly.

The only source of Sleep is Пелена's Усыпляющий кинжал.

### Immobilize

Reduces all speed to 0 and stops all movement except falling due to gravity, but can still be moved by others. Additionally, it prevents the use of movement related abilities, such as Дозорный's Сковывающее слово. Lastly, it removes all upwards acceleration, making them fall to the ground.

### Silence

Prevents the target from activating abilities, though they can still use Item actives.

A common cause of confusion is that Silence does not Interrupt, meaning abilities that have started will always continue through Silence, such as Семь's Грозовое облако.

### Item Silence

Prevents the target from using Item actives, though they can still use abilities.

Examples include the Curse Item and Замедляющие чары for some items.

### Disarm

Prevents the target from firing their main weapon but melee attacks can still be used. Note that abilities that use weapon-damage can still be used while Disarmed, such as Пелена's Танец пуль.

Examples of Disarm include Мо и Мелкий's Песочная волна and Чары обезоруживания.

### Interrupt

Interrupts instantly cancel all ability casts and channels. It alone does not prevent further use of abilities. When targets are Stunned they are also Interrupted.

Examples of Interrupt (other than Stun) include the Curse and Нокдаун Items and Серый Коготь's Спиритическая ловушка.

### Displace

Exerts some force on the target that modifies their acceleration or velocity, moving or knocking them around.

Examples include Хлыст's Удар по земле, Бибоп's Взрывной апперкот and Рука-захват, Виндикта's Кол, the Спасательный луч and Нокдаун Items, and more.

### Time Stop

The target acts as if time is frozen, pausing their movement, animations, casts, channels.

The effects of time stopped abilities can still function. For example, if Динамо is time stopped during his Сингулярность channel, it still pulls and stuns enemies. Note that this technically lengthens the duration of the Сингулярность.

The only source of Time Stop is Парадокс's Кинетический карабин.

### Hex

The target is transformed into a rabbit.

The target gains a smaller model and hitbox and moves faster, but will take increased damage. Hexed targets cannot use their weapon, melee, abilities, or items. Abilities and heavy melee attacks will not be interrupted when hexed.

The only source of Hex is Синклер's Кроличий сглаз.

## Crowd Control Reductions and Immunities

Effects that reduce the intensity of crowd control, either partially or completely.

### Slow Resistance

Reduce the effectiveness of slows.

For example, Инфернус' Пламенный рывок normally moves at 12 meters per second, but with a 30% slow and its innate 50% slow resistance it is slowed down by 15% to 10.2m/s.

### Invincibility

Become unable to be hit or targeted. All projectiles and area effects phase through while you also can not be targeted by Items such as Curse or Abilities such as Мо и Мелкий's Комбо.

Examples include Развоплощение and Калико's Возвращение в тени and the transformation phase of Ямато's Превращение в тень.

While Ямато cannot move during the transformation phase, both Развоплощение and Возвращение в тени allow limited movement.

### Purge

Instantly remove all debuffs.

Some debuffs delay the crucial negative effect, such as Бибоп's Бомба-липучка. Therefore, if the bomb is attached, then the target purges their debuffs, the bomb never explodes.

Examples include Вязкус' Куб (Tier 3), Ямато's Превращение в тень, and Debuff Remover.

### Unstoppable

Become immune to all forms of crowd control.

For example the Неудержимость Item, Абрамс' Сейсмический удар (Tier 3), and Ямато's Превращение в тень.

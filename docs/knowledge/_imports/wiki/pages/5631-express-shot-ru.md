Express Shot is a Tier 3 Weapon Item that provides bonus Bullet Velocity and Fire Rate to the purchasing hero. After not attacking for 4s, your next attack will fire twice in quick succession with increased damage and velocity. This attack consumes extra ammo.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200.

## Notable Interactions

Heroes with slow, heavy-hitting weapons can make good usage of Express Shot:

- Парадокс's Кинетический карабин

- Заточка's alternative fire

- Вязкус' alternative fire

- Ямато's alternative fire

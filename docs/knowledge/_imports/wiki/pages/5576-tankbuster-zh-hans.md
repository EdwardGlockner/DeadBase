Tankbuster is a Tier 3 Spirit Item which grants Bonus Health to the purchasing hero. It was previously named Improved Burst.

It has a passive which causes additional Spirit Damage to enemies who receive a single instance of ability spirit damage above a certain threshold from the purchasing hero.

It can be purchased from the Shop for 3,200 (its Component, 秘术爆发, makes up 800 of the cost).

## Notes

- Tankbuster only goes on cooldown when it affects enemy heroes. It will not go on cooldown when you hit a Trooper.

- Some forms of Spirit Amp applied by items count as separate damage instances and thus don't contribute to the single hit such as Escalating Exposure.

- Damage amp and spirit resist reduction will not help you in achieving the damage threshold to trigger the passive.

- Damage reduction and the target's spirit resistance will not prevent the passive from triggering when it otherwise would.

- Interaction with spirit shields: The triggering of the additional damage is entirely unaffected by the target's spirit shields (or lack thereof).

- 薇妲 uses assassinate on an enemy 沃督. The baseline Assassinate on its own is not enough damage to kill the 沃督, but the increased damage (due to the additional damage from triggering Tankbuster) is enough to secure the kill. Since the additional damage from triggering Improved Burst is applied after the Assassinate, it is technically the item's damage which secures the kill, not the Assassinate. Thus, 薇妲 will not receive bonus souls from Assassinate's passive.

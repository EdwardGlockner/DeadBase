Soul Rebirth was a Tier 4 Vitality Item that provided Spirit Power to the purchasing hero and increased basic respawn time.

Soul Rebirth had a Passive component which would respawn the hero with 50% health at the location they died after 4 seconds; this effect had a cooldown of 180 seconds, which could be reduced with additional Cooldown Reduction.

On November 7, 2024, this item was removed from the game.

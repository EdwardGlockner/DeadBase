Silver is a Hero in Deadlock, making her living tracking down lowlifes, but today New York’s misanthrope lycanthrope plans on summoning a god. Fortunately, her career as a bounty hunter has prepared her well for all that the ritual requires. She makes quick work of adversaries by catching them with her Entangling Bola, Boot Kicking them in the face, and finishing them off with a Slam Fire from her rifle. But inevitably, the Lycan Curse comes calling whether she likes it or not, and her foes will regret being too close when it does.

Lilah Silver has been a werewolf for as long as she can remember, insisting that she has her Lycanthropy under control. Despite her successes as a bounty hunter, Silver struggles with alcohol, smoking, financial, and personal troubles on a daily basis, driving her to seek the Patron's help to "thrive".

Silver was released on February 2, 2026 as the third winner of the Old Gods, New Blood update contest.

Hair of the Dog reloads single shots and the reload can be interrupted.

Loose Muzzle deals damage in a close range, cone-shaped attack that can hit multiple enemies. The 7 meter falloff range cannot be increased by items such as Sharpshooter. It has no ammo count and no reload, and does not deal headshot damage.

It can activate Weapon item effects such as Ricochet. Split Shot has no effect on Loose Muzzle.

- This attack stacks Fire Rate from each confirmed hit on enemies and NPCs. The amount of fire rate gained per hit is found by dividing the Lycan Curse maximum Fire Rate by 16. The fire rate buff lasts approximately 6 seconds before resetting if no damage has been dealt with Loose Muzzle, and does not transfer to Silver when Lycan Curse ends.

## Abilities

Silver is unique among heroes in having two distinct sets of abilities — one for her default (human) form and one for her transformed (werewolf) form — which swap automatically when Lycan Curse transforms her. Her ultimate, Lycan Curse, is shared between both forms and triggers the transformation.

## Trivia

- During development, Silver was originally codenamed Shapeshifter, whose name was leaked in November 2025.3]

- Silver had a scrapped ability where she tracked bounties during the match, as indicated by unused voicelines still in the files.

- The name of Silver's weapon, Hair of the Dog, is a shortening of the expression Hair of the Dog that Bit You, referring to alcohol consumed to lessen the effect of a hangover.

- Silver is the first hero to possess a weapon without a traditional projectile or ranged option, as her primary fire in Werewolf form swings her claws to maim enemies in melee range. She's also the first hero to have two sets of abilities.

- "Figuring Out What Happened Last Night in the Hideout" is displayed as one's status in the in-game Friends List when playing as Silver in the Hideout.

- It's also a common component-word primarily to describe appearance of non-human specimen (e.g. "silver back", "silver fang", etc.).

- Silver's release date, February 2, 2026, was under a full moon, a common association with werewolves.

- Silver's hairstyle is known as a Wolf Cut, originally known as a Shag.

- Silver's name typography has the shape of a crescent moon in the "R" curve.

Lilah Silver is a mess. She drinks too much, she smokes too much, and the less is said about her personal life the better. But despite Silver's flaws she has managed to carve out a career as a successful bounty hunter, and while she would like to think that her successes are solely because she has incredible intuition, the fact that she’s a werewolf doesn’t hurt.

### Biography

Silver has been a werewolf for as long as she can remember.5] She claims to have her Lycanthropy under control,6] but the transformation can trigger by itself during the ritual.7]8]

As a bounty hunter, Silver has brought Vyper into custody on several occasions and is after a bounty on The Troubadour.9]10]

Silver is currently renting a place in New York.4]11] Silver's accent implies she's originally from somewhere further south in America.

Silver has an unnamed ex-partner who accused her of pushing people away.12]

Silver once got kicked out of the Baroness Hotel during brunch.13]14]

Silver used to play the piano.15]

### Personality

Silver hates her life and is aware of her self-destructive tendencies.16]17]18] She's often snarky towards her allies,19]20] and is paranoid that they might steal her bounties.21]22]

While Silver doesn't love being a bounty hunter, she believes she isn't good at anything else and would end up homeless if she didn't do a job that paid well.23]24] Silver tries to take bounties in alive, although her methods usually end up killing them.25] Silver's bounty hunting methods are also implied to never be subtle.26]27] Even though she usually kills her targets, Silver doesn't want to take up a career as an assassin.28]

Silver has a habit of spending all of her money on rent or alcohol.11] She likes to drink whiskey, which makes her hair smell like Bourbon according to Rem.29]30] She intends to go to a bar after the ritual.31]32]

Silver implies that she used to be a lot more violent.33]

### Appearance

Silver is a skinny white woman with a long blonde "wolf cut" haircut and tall gray wolf ears poking out, amber eyes, and big claw scars in her face and chest. She has a blonde wolf tail, and her left hand is black and furry. She wears an orange jacket over a low cut gray shirt, blue jeans, black belt, and brown boots with fur. Her clothes are dirty and ragged. She has a dog-like collar with a broken pendant in the shape of a crescent moon, and carries handcuffs, shotgun shells and wolf paws in her belt. Her weapon is a lever-action shotgun.

When transformed, Silver becomes a tall, muscular, androgynous wolf monster with gray and black fur, a long blonde mane, bigger tail, large 4-fingered hands with claws, and digitigrade feet with 3 toes. Her clothes are ripped, her jacket becomes an open vest and her pants become shorts.

### Information

The name of Silver's weapon, Hair of the Dog, is a shortening of the expression Hair of the Dog that Bit You, referring to alcohol consumed to lessen the effect of a hangover.

## Character Relationships

### Shiv

Silver loathes Shiv with a passion and refuses to believe he could change from his criminal past.34]35] She believes he deserves a fate worse than death.36]

An unfinished conversation with Grey Talon hints at Silver in disbelief that Grey Talon would support Shiv.37]

How Silver and Shiv met each other and why Silver hates Shiv is currently unknown.

### Abrams

Silver is old friends with Abrams, referring to him as "Abe".38]

According to Silver, they used to make "bad decisions" before Abrams became "boring".39]

### Infernus and Hank

As a heavy drinker, Silver frequents Jezebel's and is friends with Hank and Infernus.40]41]

Silver refers to Infernus as "Fern".42]

### Vyper

Silver has hunted and caught Vyper so many times that Vyper's use of Petrifying Bola comes from Silver's Entangling Bola.43]44]

Silver thinks Vyper is more pathetic than her.45]

### Mo & Krill

Mo and Krill have worked with Silver in the past and have a good working relationship with her.46]

While on the other team, they express concern over "how desperate" Silver has gotten and wish they could've helped her.47]

## Character Interactions

- Silver plays in Wraith's weekly poker games.48]

- Silver offers to talk with Holliday about Holliday's self-destructive tendancies, as Silver claims to be an "expert".49] She also tries to relate with Holliday as a fellow "trainwreck", although Holliday pushes her away.50]

- Silver is implied to have been hunting Drifter for a while and hates him.51]52] Silver is also after a bounty on Calico.53]

- Silver and Venator get along with each other, planning to go drink whiskey together after the ritual.54]32]

- Silver calls Mina a "Stacy".55]

- Silver makes fun of Grey Talon's health due to his age.56]

- Celeste offers to have a drink with Silver after the ritual.31]

- According to Rem, Silver's hair smells like Bourbon.30]

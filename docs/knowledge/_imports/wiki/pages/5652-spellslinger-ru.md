Spellslinger is a Tier 4 Оружие Item that provides Cooldown Reduction and a passive weapon bonus when abilities are cast.

Whenever an ability or item is cast, the user will gain a stacking buff that improves Fire Rate and Reload Speed.

This item is also referred to as Enchanted Holsters in the game files.

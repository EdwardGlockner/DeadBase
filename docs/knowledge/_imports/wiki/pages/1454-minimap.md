The Minimap is a simplified depiction of the map from above. It is used in a match to navigate the map, see the location of teammates, and to see any enemy players within the player's or players' teammates line of sight.

In spectator mode, the Minimap has The Archmother side as North and The Hidden King side as South by default.

### Things that will not reveal the player to enemies on the minimap

- Standing near enemy Troopers while they are not shooting at or targeting the player.

- Using abilities or items on enemy Troopers or heroes.

- Being targeted or shot at by an enemy hero from beyond approximately 50–60 meters, even if they have line of sight on the player.

- Enemy Troopers riding a zipline near or above the player.

- Shooting at summons such as Graves' Ghouls or Deadheads or deployables such as McGinnis' Mini Turrets.

- Being hit by summons or deployable abilities (Though it will make the player's portrait appear in the HUD for the hero who summoned the unit).

## Invisibility

Invisibility (from Veil Walker, Shadow Weave, or Haze's Smoke Bomb) prevents revealing under the following rules:

- Breaking invisibility requires: using abilities, shooting, or being within proximity of an enemy hero with line of sight (walls block this).

- Exception: Haze's Smoke Bomb allows using items and abilities without breaking invisibility or revealing her on the minimap.

- Buildings and objectives do not break invisibility or reveal the player.

- Graves' Ghouls will chase invisible players but will not explode on them.

## Hero Interactions

- Calico cannot be revealed on the minimap while using Ava, even if shot by troopers, guardians, or walkers.

- Mirage's Djinn's Mark will reveal invisible enemies on the minimap when activated.

## Item Interactions

- Stalker provides wall-hack vision of the target but does not reveal them on the minimap for the team.

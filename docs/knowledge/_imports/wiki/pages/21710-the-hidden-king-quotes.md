## Removed/Unused Voice Lines

### Old Voice

The Hidden King's voice lines were changed twice, first in the November 21, 2024 update. Kaori confirmed that the voice actor was the same, only the line delivery was changed. It was changed again in the Old Gods, New Blood update, in which the Hidden King's voice actor was recast.
